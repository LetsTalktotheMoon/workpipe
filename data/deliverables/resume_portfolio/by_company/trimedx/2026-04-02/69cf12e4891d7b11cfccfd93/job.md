# Senior Platform Engineer

**Company:** TRIMEDX
**Location:** Indianapolis, IN
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
TRIMEDX is a company focused on serving clients, patients, and communities with care and performance. The Senior Platform Engineer designs and maintains technology platforms that empower software development teams, championing DevOps principles to enhance automation and collaboration.

## Core Skills
- Systems engineering
- SaaS platform management
- Infrastructure lifecycle methodology
- Scripting
- Automation

## Minimum qualifications
- Three or more years of experience in systems engineering is required
- In-depth knowledge and skills in infrastructure/systems lifecycle methodology, scripting, and automation for both on-premises and SaaS environments
- Experience managing SaaS platforms, including configuration, integration, monitoring, and security
- Knowledge of interface standards, interoperability, and common testing tools and methods
- Familiarity with SaaS vendor management, licensing, and compliance requirements
- Experience designing and operating internal developer platforms
- CI/CD pipelines, and infrastructure as code solutions
- Proficiency with infrastructure-as-code tools (e.g.
- Terraform
- Ansible)
- CI/CD systems, and containerization (Docker
- Kubernetes)
- Strong understanding of platform reliability, scalability, and security best practices
- Experience collaborating with software engineering teams to deliver platform features and support developer workflows
- Solid foundation in infrastructure or DevOps, with hands-on experience in cloud platforms (AWS
- Azure
- GCP)

## Preferred qualifications
- Exposure to SaaS platforms preferred
- Professional certifications and advanced degrees in related fields are a plus
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or a related field is preferred
- Familiarity with observability tools (e.g.
- Prometheus
- Grafana
- ELK Stack
- Datadog)

## Responsibilities
- Manage software platforms supporting application development and deployment
- Lead design and implementation of advanced platform features and architecture
- Develop and manage CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code (IaC), and automation frameworks to support rapid and reliable deployment of applications and services
- Provision and manage cloud infrastructure resources, ensuring scalability, reliability, and cost efficiency
- Create and maintain self-service tools for development teams
- Integrate security protocols and compliance checks into platform architecture and automated workflows
- Collaborate with software developers, product, and security engineers to deliver infrastructure features that improve reliability, efficiency, and security
- Collaborate with software engineering teams to understand their needs and deliver platform features that accelerate development and deployment cycles
- Maintain platform documentation, onboarding guides, and best practices to support developer adoption and operational excellence
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to platform infrastructure
