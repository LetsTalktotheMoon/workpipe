# Senior Data Engineer

**Company:** Real Chemistry
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Real Chemistry is a global agency focused on transforming healthcare through innovative services. The Senior Data Engineer will design, build, and scale data infrastructure, ensuring data quality and mentoring junior engineers.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- Pandas
- AWS S3
- AWS Redshift

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Data Engineering, or related field—or equivalent experience
- 5–7+ years of experience in data engineering, with proven success building and scaling data systems
- Expert knowledge of SQL and Python, including performance optimization and data manipulation libraries (e.g.
- Pandas)
- Strong experience with AWS data products (S3
- Redshift
- Athena
- Lambda) and familiarity with multi-cloud environments
- Deep understanding of data modeling, schema design, and best practices for large-scale data solutions
- Demonstrated ability to design secure, reliable pipelines and troubleshoot complex issues
- Experience leading projects and mentoring other engineers
- Excellent communication, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills
- Comfortable managing multiple priorities and delivering in a fast-paced environment

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with data transformation and productivity tools (dbt
- Jinja, etc.)
- Familiarity with Snowflake (Notebooks
- Worksheets
- VS Code plugin)
- Knowledge of data visualization platforms such as Looker Studio or similar BI tools
- Exposure to digital marketing analytics and measurement frameworks

## Responsibilities
- Architect and optimize robust, scalable ETL pipelines and workflows to ingest, transform, and deliver data from multiple sources
- Ensure data integrity and governance through validation frameworks, schema design, and proactive monitoring
- Leverage advanced SQL and programming skills to extract, transform, and analyze data efficiently
- Develop automation using Python
- Pandas
- AWS Lambda, and related tools to improve operational efficiency
- Design and manage data platforms, including Snowflake
- Athena
- Redshift
- BigQuery, and other cloud-based solutions
- Collaborate cross-functionally with analysts, strategists, and business leaders to translate business requirements into technical solutions
- Mentor and coach junior data engineers, setting standards for code quality, documentation, and best practices
- Lead vendor and partner integrations, ensuring seamless data delivery and alignment with internal standards
- Communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical stakeholders, providing clear insight and direction
- Drive innovation, identifying opportunities to improve performance, scalability, and usability of our data systems
