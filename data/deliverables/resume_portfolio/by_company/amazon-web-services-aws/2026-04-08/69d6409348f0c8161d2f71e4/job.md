# Software Development Engineer, AWS Training and Certification

**Company:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon Web Services (AWS) is the world’s most comprehensive and broadly adopted cloud platform. The Software Development Engineer will contribute to building learning systems that reach millions of AWS customers, focusing on developing event-driven microservice architecture applications and enhancing existing functionalities to improve customer experience.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Software Design
- Software Architecture
- Design Patterns
- System Reliability

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- 1+ years of designing and developing large-scale, multi-tiered, multi-threaded, embedded or distributed software applications, tools, systems, and services using: C#
- C++
- Java, or Perl experience
- 1+ years of Object Oriented Design experience
- Bachelor's degree or foreign equivalent in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Mathematics, or a related field

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- Building learning systems that will reach millions of AWS Customers
- Partners, and Users
- Creating new services and capabilities to meet emerging needs as the business grows
- Diving deep into complex technical problems to enhance learning tools and customer experience
