# Full-Stack Software Engineer - GenAI

**Company:** AIG
**Location:** Atlanta, GA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
AIG is a leading global insurance organization providing a wide range of property casualty insurance and other financial services. As a Full-Stack Software Engineer for GenAI, you will design, develop, and operationalize GenAI data pipelines to enable innovative AI models and analytics, collaborating across teams to drive business insights and value.

## Core Skills
- Generative AI
- Data Engineering
- Software Development
- Python
- TypeScript

## Minimum qualifications
- Minimum of 3 years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in at least one high-level programming language (Python
- Typescript
- Java
- JavaScript/React), a strong background in Typescript is a plus but not required
- Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code
- Experience with testing frameworks (pytest, unittest)
- An understanding of containerization and compute optimization
- Build and maintain scalable and reliable APIs to expose data and functionalities to other services and applications
- Identify and implement performance optimization for existing code and data processing workflows, including leveraging containerization technologies for parallel execution
- Contribute to the design and architecture of our data platform and applications
- Participate in code reviews to ensure code quality and adherence to best practices
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines

## Preferred qualifications
- Prior experience in Foundry is a big plus but not required
- You are passionate about exploring innovative AI and Generative AI technologies, using them to drive impactful solutions within large-scale enterprise environments
- You would enjoy working within the financial services or insurance industry and understand the unique data challenges these sectors face

## Responsibilities
- Collaborating with stakeholders to identify and deliver innovative data solutions that meet the evolving needs of the business
- Building and maintaining GenAI data pipelines across the business
- Partnering with data architects and senior engineers to implement best practices, ensuring sustainable and scalable technical designs
- Working closely with analytics, application development, and data science teams to foster collaboration and drive innovation
- Delivering on-time, high-quality results on fast-paced schedules while maintaining rigorous standards for development and code management
