# Software Engineer, Product Frontend (2-8 YOE)

**Company:** Airtable
**Location:** Buffalo-Niagara Area
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Airtable is the no-code app platform that empowers organizations to transform their business processes. The Software Engineer, Product Frontend will work with cross-functional teams to design and implement innovative features, ensuring a high-quality user experience while collaborating closely with product management, design, and data science teams.

## Core Skills
- Full-stack web development
- Frontend development
- JavaScript
- React
- Node.js

## Minimum qualifications
- 2-5 years of industry experience with modern full-stack web development, with particular emphasis on user facing feature development and full stack, frontend leaning work
- Fluent in one or more frontend and backend technologies
- Interested in programming in JavaScript and React
- Excited by a product-oriented full stack or frontend role
- Good understanding of web technologies and large-scale web application architecture
- Background in computer science or a related field
- Belief that the best product is the one that helps users accomplish their goals while growing the business
- Thoughtful approach to decision making
- knowing when to move fast and when to do things right
- Strong convictions, weakly held and assume validation and iteration are a given to produce the best results
- Strong ownership instincts and sweat the details

## Responsibilities
- Spec and build product features that are beautiful, consistent, and fast, that encourage exploration, and that earn the trust of our customers through predictable, reliable, and thoughtful interactions
- Work at all levels of the stack, from AWS
- Node.js, and SQL to React
- HTML. and CSS to build and maintain end to end products on Airtable
- Partner with product managers, designers, and data scientists to understand the needs of potential users and build a product that unlocks their potential
- Work alongside and learn from a talented group of engineers with a diverse range of experiences and backgrounds
