# Machine Learning Engineer II , AGI Customization

**Company:** Amazon
**Location:** Boston, MA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon is seeking a passionate and talented Software Development Engineer for their Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) team to develop customization capabilities for Large Language Models. The role involves leading the development of novel training techniques and collaborating with Applied Scientists to leverage Amazon’s data and computing resources for Generative AI solutions.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Software Development
- System Design
- Architecture
- Programming

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- Experience programming with at least one software programming language
- A commitment to team work, hustle, and strong communication skills (to both business and technical partners)
- Exceptional technical expertise
- A sound understanding of the fundamentals of Computer Science and Machine Learning
- Demonstrable track record of success in delivering new features and products
- Experience delivering high quality technology products/services in a hyper-growth environment

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- Leading the development of novel LLM training techniques and optimizations
- Collaborate closely with Applied Scientists
- Leverage Amazon’s heterogeneous data sources and large-scale computing resources to accelerate development of multimodal Large Language Models and Generative Artificial Intelligence solutions
- Create reliable, scalable, and high performance AI products
