# Data Scientist

**Company:** Lincare
**Location:** Clearwater, FL
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Lincare is seeking a Data Scientist to support and lead AI-driven initiatives aimed at enhancing revenue growth and patient Lifetime Value. The role involves conducting various types of analytics to derive actionable insights and requires strong skills in machine learning, data engineering, and technical coding.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Data Engineering
- Natural Language Processing
- Large Language Models
- AI Skills

## Minimum qualifications
- building machine learning algorithms, data engineering, natural language processing (NLP), large language models (LLMs) and any other required AI skills
- Technical coding skills including SQL code
- R-Markdown and Python, statistical knowledge, complete any assigned analytical projects in a timely and efficient manner, identify, plan and implement any other use cases for AI technology, take a key role in the planning and development of Lincare's AI platform and strategy, focus on business impact and have measurable outcomes for success measurement

## Responsibilities
- Responsible for data analytics / data science on massive sets of complex and, sometimes, unstructured data to prepare for statistical analysis
- Manipulates or converts data according to analysis needs
- and develops approaches to deal with imperfections in data (e.g. missing values or inconsistent data formatting)
- Identifies and executes on best approach for assigned project which may include skills including machine learning
- NLP
- LLMs, deep learning, neural networks or any other data science function
- Develops reports using complex formulas
- analyzes and reviews results for accuracy
- Understands how to work with multiple data sources, and accurately accesses the correct data within complex databases based on business needs and reporting requirements
- Will be comfortable interacting at all levels of technology teams both internal and external to Lincare. (project managers, database designer, information architecture, multilayer off-shore support centers, etc.)
- Manages projects to ensure they are delivered successfully and in a timely manner
- Identifies opportunities to improve revenue, productivity/efficiency as well as enhance patient life time value using data science technologies
- Performs other duties as assigned
- Complies with all policies and standards
