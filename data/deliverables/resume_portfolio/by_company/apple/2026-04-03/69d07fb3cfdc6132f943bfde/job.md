# SW User Experience Automation QE

**Company:** Apple
**Location:** San Diego, California, United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Apple is where individual creativities gather together, committing to the values that lead to great work. The System-Wide Quality organization is seeking an exceptional software quality automation engineer to join the User Experience Quality team, driving the execution of automation strategies to ensure software quality at scale.

## Core Skills
- Python
- Java
- Swift
- Automation Testing
- Software Development Lifecycle

## Minimum qualifications
- B.S. in Computer Science or 3-5+ years of equivalent automation experience with strong proficiency in modern scripting or compiled languages such as Python
- Java, or Swift
- Track record of driving development and deployment of testing tools and infrastructure at scale, with knowledge of the software development lifecycle and SQA methodologies
- Ability to write, maintain, and execute comprehensive automation test plans, plus familiarity with Continuous Integration tools (e.g.
- Jenkins) and version control systems (e.g.
- GitHub)
- Ability to analyze test failures, triage issues, and prioritize bugs based on severity of user impact
- Proficiency with Apple operating systems, including iOS, macOS, tvOS, visionOS, and watchOS

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with AI-driven testing approaches or intelligent automation
- Experience with robotics-driven testing approaches
- Proficiency in Xcode
- XCTest, and XCUIAutomation
- Skill in manual software testing, with a focus on UI and UX
- Experience with data analytics, telemetry systems, and presenting technical insights to diverse audiences

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop AI-driven automation frameworks that evaluate end-to-end user experiences across Apple platforms, capturing both functional correctness and experiential quality
- Architect and maintain scalable test infrastructure that delivers continuous quality signals to provide real-time insights on software readiness
- Partner with manual UX testers to translate their expertise into automated test scenarios, as well as define and track metrics that quantify user experience quality across key use cases
- Evaluate and integrate emerging technologies—including machine learning models, computer vision, and robotics—to continuously expand automation coverage and fidelity
