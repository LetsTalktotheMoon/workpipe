# Data Analyst II

**Company:** NextGen | GTA: A Kelly Telecom Company
**Location:** Houston, TX
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
NextGen, a Kelly Telecom Company, is seeking a Data Analyst II to deliver actionable insights that drive business strategy and operational optimization. This role involves working with large datasets, performing advanced analysis, and presenting strategic recommendations to key stakeholders.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Teradata
- Tableau
- Excel
- PowerPoint

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in business
- Analytics
- Engineering
- Finance, or related field
- 3–4 years of experience in data analysis or business intelligence
- Strong SQL/Teradata experience
- Proficiency in Tableau
- Excel, and PowerPoint (Think-Cell preferred)
- Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Python
- R, or data modeling
- Understanding of KPI development and statistical significance
- Experience working with large-scale relational databases

## Responsibilities
- Perform advanced data analysis using large, complex datasets to support business decisions
- Develop and execute SQL queries across multiple platforms with a focus on performance and accuracy
- Identify trends, drivers, and opportunities through statistical analysis
- Validate data quality and resolve inconsistencies
- Partner with cross-functional teams (data architecture, business stakeholders) to support data needs
- Create dashboards, reports, and presentations (Tableau
- PowerPoint) to communicate insights clearly
- Translate complex data into meaningful business recommendations
- Track projects and tasks using JIRA and manage deliverables proactively
