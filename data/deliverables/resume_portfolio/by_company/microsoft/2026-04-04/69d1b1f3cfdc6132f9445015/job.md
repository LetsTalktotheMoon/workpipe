# Software Engineer

**Company:** Microsoft
**Location:** Redmond, WA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Microsoft is seeking engineers for their Experience plus Devices (E+D) Growth team to accelerate the adoption of Copilot and Microsoft 365. The role involves designing and developing scalable software solutions that enhance customer experiences while fostering an inclusive environment that leverages diverse perspectives.

## Core Skills
- C
- C++
- C#
- Java
- JavaScript

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or related technical field AND 2+ years technical engineering experience with coding in languages including, but not limited to
- C
- C++
- C#
- Java
- JavaScript/TypeScript, or Python OR equivalent experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Demonstrated ability to ship high-quality software on time
- Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work effectively in a collaborative team environment
- Passion for customers, design thinking, data analysis, and technology
- Ability to perform at a high level in a fast paced, dynamic environment

## Responsibilities
- Design secure, reliable, scalable, and sustainable software
- Develop, validate, release, and derive insights from software that enhances customer experiences as outlined above
- Champion customer-first design that leads to increased loyalty to Copilot and Microsoft 365
- Apply critical analyses to data to validate hypotheses and inform decisions
- Foster an inclusive environment that welcomes diverse perspectives, and in turn leverages that diversity to create more inclusive products for our customers
- Model a growth mindset by learning from others and sharing your learnings with others
