# Senior Business Intelligence Analyst, Product

**Company:** Hone Health
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Hone Health is an online medical clinic focused on transforming healthcare and enhancing longevity. They are seeking a Senior Business Intelligence Analyst to collaborate with business stakeholders, design dashboards, and develop analysis to drive strategic decisions.

## Core Skills
- Business Intelligence
- Data Analysis
- SQL
- Python
- Power BI

## Minimum qualifications
- 5+ years of experience in business intelligence, data analysis, or a similar role
- Advanced SQL skills and comfort working directly with raw tables to produce clean, performant queries
- Intermediate Python skills to model and analyze raw tables
- Hands-on experience with one or more modern BI tools (e.g.
- Power BI
- Tableau
- Looker)
- Strong data visualization and communication skills
- Passionate about helping others understand and use data to drive better decisions
- Experience working in cloud platforms (Azure
- AWS
- GCP)
- Experience working in agile, cross-functional teams
- Ability to multitask, prioritize, and adhere to deadlines while maintaining attention to detail and quality
- Comfortable in a fast-paced, frequently changing environment

## Preferred qualifications
- Familiarity with dbt or dimensional modeling concepts is a plus
- Familiarity with healthcare, telehealth, or regulatory data (e.g.
- HIPAA) is a plus

## Responsibilities
- Partner with Product & Engineering teams to understand their goals and translate them into metrics, reports, and dashboards
- Build reporting products within the application
- Build, maintain, and enhance core business dashboards using Power BI
- Utilize AI to maximize efficiency
- Use SQL and Python to extract, analyze, and transform data to support reporting and ad hoc analysis
- Own the design and documentation of key business metrics, ensuring clarity and consistency across teams
- Proactively identify trends, anomalies, and opportunities in data and share insights with stakeholders
- Collaborate with Data Engineering and Analytics Engineering to ensure data models meet reporting needs and support self-service analytics
- Contribute to the development of team-wide best practices for visualization, dashboard UX, and storytelling with data
- Create training, documentation, and data literacy resources to enable others to access and interpret data effectively
