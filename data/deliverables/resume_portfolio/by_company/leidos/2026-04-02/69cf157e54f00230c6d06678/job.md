# Software Developer  (Full Stack / Web Applications)

**Company:** Leidos
**Location:** Huntsville, AL
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Leidos is an industry and technology leader serving government and commercial customers with smarter, more efficient digital and mission innovations. The company is seeking a mid-level Software Developer with expertise in modern web technologies and full-stack development to build responsive front-end applications and scalable back-end services, operating within an Agile/Scrum team environment.

## Core Skills
- Full-stack development
- React
- TypeScript
- C#
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- Candidate must possess a bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Computer/Software Engineering, or an equivalent degree
- Must have a minimum of 4 years of professional software development experience (or 2 years of experience with master's degree)
- Must have experience in designing and maintaining software products and implementing software designs
- Must have experience in software development, data structures, object-oriented design, and effective unit testing
- Should have experience with software best practices such as peer reviews and document generation
- Must have experience with Javascript
- Must have experience with containerization technologies
- Must have experience working in Agile or scrum environment

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with computer vision or AI is a plus
- Experience leading scrum teams is a plus

## Responsibilities
- Building responsive front-end applications (React
- TypeScript) and scalable back-end services (C#
- Python, or Node.js)
- Proficiency in containerization tools such as Docker or Podman
- Developing or integrating AI and computer vision capabilities into web-based or edge-deployed applications
