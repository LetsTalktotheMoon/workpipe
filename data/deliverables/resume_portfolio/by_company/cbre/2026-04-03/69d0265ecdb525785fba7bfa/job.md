# Software Engineer

**Company:** CBRE
**Location:** Richardson, TX
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
CBRE is the world’s largest commercial real estate services and investment firm. As a Software Engineer, you will be responsible for designing, developing, and implementing software solutions while executing the full software development life cycle to improve system efficiency.

## Core Skills
- Python
- Go
- Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning
- Data Concepts

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree preferred with 1-3 years of relevant experience
- In lieu of a degree, a combination of experience and education will be considered
- Experience in programming with Python or Go
- Ability to exercise judgment based on the analysis of multiple sources of information
- Willingness to take a new perspective on existing solutions
- In-depth knowledge of Microsoft Office products. Examples include Word
- Excel
- Outlook, etc
- Organizational skills with an advanced inquisitive mindset

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in AI/ML initiatives highly preferred
- Familiarity with data concepts

## Responsibilities
- Execute full software development life cycle (SDLC), build high-quality, innovative, and performing software
- Ensure compliance with coding standards and technical designs
- Create flowcharts, layouts, and documentation to identify requirements and solutions
- Document feature specifications and identify operational feasibility
- Develop software verification plans and quality assurance procedures
- Record and maintain software functionality
- deploy programs and review user feedback
- troubleshoot, debug, and upgrade existing systems
- Provide efficient, timely, reliable, and courteous service to customers
- Coach others and shares in-depth knowledge of Software Engineering discipline and broad knowledge of several job disciplines within the function
- Exercise judgment based on the analysis of multiple sources of information
- take a new perspective on existing solutions
- Work to build consensus and convince others to reach an agreement
