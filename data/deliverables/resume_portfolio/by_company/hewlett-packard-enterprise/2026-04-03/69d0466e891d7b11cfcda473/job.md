# Software Engineer

**Company:** Hewlett Packard Enterprise
**Location:** Sunnyvale, CA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Hewlett Packard Enterprise is a global edge-to-cloud company that helps organizations connect, protect, analyze, and act on their data. The Software Engineer role focuses on maintaining quality standards for products through systematic processes, developing and executing software test strategies, and collaborating with project teams to ensure reliable testing solutions.

## Core Skills
- Software Quality Assurance
- Software Testing Methodology
- Test Plan Execution
- Debugging
- Test Automation

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science
- Information Systems, or equivalent
- 2+ years of software development experience
- Using software quality assurance tools and processes
- Software testing methodology, including execution of test plans, debugging, and testing scripts and tools
- Ability to apply analytical and problem solving skills
- Understanding of design for software and firmware running on multiple platform types
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- mastery in English and local language
- Ability to effectively communicate design proposals and negotiate options

## Responsibilities
- Designs quality assurance and test processes for portions of end-user applications, systems software, and firmware running on hardware, local, networked, and Internet-based platforms
- Analyzes design and determines test scripts, coding, automation, and integration activities required based on specific objectives and established project guidelines
- Executes and writes portions of testing plans, protocols, and documentation for assigned portion of application
- identifies and debugs issues with code and suggests changes or improvements
- Participates as a member of project team of other software quality assurance engineers and internal and outsourced development partners to develop reliable and cost-effective testing and quality assurance solutions for assigned software portion
- Collaborates and communicates with internal and outsourced development partners regarding software applications design status, project progress, and issue resolution
