# Full Stack Engineer II

**Company:** Pacific Life
**Location:** Newport Beach, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Pacific Life is dedicated to providing for loved ones and planning rewarding retirements. As a Full Stack Developer II, you will design and build scalable, cloud-based solutions supporting the Consumer Markets Division, collaborating with an agile team to solve complex business problems.

## Core Skills
- Full Stack Development
- Cloud Engineering
- AWS
- API Development
- Data Integration

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience
- 3+ years of experience in software development, including full stack, cloud, or application architecture roles
- Hands-on experience building and integrating APIs and data services using AWS cloud technologies
- Strong working knowledge of modern SDLC practices and agile development methodologies
- Experience with a mix of technologies such as: AWS services
- Relational and NoSQL databases
- Python
- C#, .NET Core
- Windows and Linux environments
- Familiarity with automation, integration, and business process management solutions
- Experience working in fast-paced, collaborative agile teams
- A willingness and ability to mentor junior engineers and contribute to team skill development

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in Financial Services, particularly Annuities or Insurance products
- Prior experience building cloud-native, scalable, or event-driven architectures
- Strong collaboration skills and a continuous improvement mindset

## Responsibilities
- Analyze complex business problems, clarify requirements, and evaluate technical feasibility across the full stack
- Contribute to solution design, including application architecture, workflows, integrations, and data flows
- Design, develop, test, and deploy full stack solutions that solve complex business challenges
- Partner with Product Owners to refine the backlog, prioritize use cases, and ensure shared understanding across the team
- Collaborate closely with business partners, architects, and engineers on integration design, development, testing, and implementation
- Build and consume APIs, develop data integrations, and leverage AWS cloud services to deliver scalable solutions
- Work with large and complex datasets to derive insights and enable new product features
- Own delivery from initial design through production release, supporting solutions in a DevOps-oriented environment
- Communicate technical concepts, trade-offs, and outcomes clearly to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Contribute to automation, quality, and continuous improvement practices across the development lifecycle
