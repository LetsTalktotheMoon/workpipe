# Engineer Senior, Software

**Company:** FIS
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
FIS is a leading provider of technology that powers the world’s economy, and they are seeking a Mobile/Web Quality Engineer to drive innovation and growth for their Digital Banking Solutions business. The role involves creating test plans and automated test scripts, collaborating with cross-functional teams, and contributing to the company's digital transformation journey.

## Core Skills
- Test planning for mobile applications
- Automated test script development
- Appium
- Detox
- JavaScript

## Minimum qualifications
- Proficient in creating test plans, test cases, and test scenarios for mobile applications across iOS and Android platforms
- Strong hands-on experience in developing and maintaining automated test scripts for mobile apps using relevant tools and frameworks (e.g.
- Appium
- Detox)
- Hands-on experience with JavaScript and Java for automation tasks and test script development
- Knowledge of React applications
- Experience integrating automated tests into CI/CD pipelines and working collaboratively with DevOps teams
- Solid understanding of mobile application frameworks, architectures, and technologies to effectively design and execute testing strategies
- Ability to analyze logs, debug issues, and troubleshoot mobile app performance, compatibility, and functionality issues
- Strong knowledge of QA methodologies, processes, and best practices, including Agile testing methodologies (e.g.
- Scrum
- Kanban)
- Experience in creating and maintaining test documentation, defect tracking, and reporting using tools such as Jira
- Zephyr
- ReportPortal
- TestRail, or Allure
- Effective communication skills to collaborate with cross-functional teams including developers, product managers, designers, and QA teams
