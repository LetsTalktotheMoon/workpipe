# Software Engineer III, Front End, Google Cloud AI

**Company:** Google
**Location:** Kirkland, WA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Google is a leading technology company known for its innovative products and services. They are seeking a Software Engineer III to develop next-generation technologies that enhance user interaction with information and AI. The role involves designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software solutions, while collaborating with teams across various projects.

## Core Skills
- TypeScript
- JavaScript
- Front-end frameworks
- Full-stack development
- API development

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
- 2 years of experience programming in TypeScript or JavaScript
- 2 years of experience with front-end frameworks, full-stack development, or API development

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science or related technical field
- 2 years of experience with performance, large scale systems data analysis, visualization tools, or debugging
- 2 years of experience with data structures or algorithms in either an academic or industry setting
- Experience developing accessible technologies
- Proficiency in code and system health, diagnosis and resolution, and software test engineering

## Responsibilities
- Write product or system development code
- Participate in, or lead design reviews with peers and stakeholders to decide amongst available technologies
- Review code developed by other developers and provide feedback to ensure best practices (e.g., style guidelines, checking code in, accuracy, testability, and efficiency)
- Contribute to existing documentation or educational content and adapt content based on product/program updates and user feedback
- Triage product or system issues and debug/track/resolve by analyzing the sources of issues and the impact on hardware, network, or service operations and quality
