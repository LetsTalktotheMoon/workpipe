# Senior Data Engineer

**Company:** Retell AI
**Location:** San Francisco Bay Area
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Retell AI is a rapidly growing voice AI startup focused on transforming call centers with cutting-edge technology. The Founding Senior Data Engineer will be responsible for building the company's analytics foundation, managing data flows, and providing reliable insights to shape various strategic decisions across the organization.

## Core Skills
- Data Engineering
- Analytics Engineering
- Data Analysis
- SQL
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- Have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Data Engineering
- Data Science, or a closely related technical field
- Have 3+ years of experience as a Data Engineer
- Analytics Engineer
- Data Analyst, or in a similar analytics-focused role
- Write production-quality SQL and are comfortable with Python or similar languages
- Have experience partnering closely with Product and GTM teams to define metrics that actually drive decisions
- Have worked at a startup or fast-growing company and are comfortable with ambiguity
- Think in systems and care deeply about metric definitions and data correctness
- Can translate messy business questions into clean, reliable analytical models
- Enjoy owning problems end-to-end rather than operating within a narrow scope

## Responsibilities
- Design, build and manage our data pipelines, ensuring all user event data is seamlessly integrated into our data warehouse
- Develop canonical datasets to track key product metrics including user growth, engagement, and revenue
- Work collaboratively with various teams, including
- Infrastructure
- Data Science
- Product
- Marketing
- Finance, and Research to understand their data needs and provide solutions
- Develop insightful and reliable dashboards to track performance of core metrics that will deliver insights to the whole company
- Become an expert in our organization’s data models and the company's data architecture
- Ensure the security, integrity, and compliance of data according to industry and company standards
