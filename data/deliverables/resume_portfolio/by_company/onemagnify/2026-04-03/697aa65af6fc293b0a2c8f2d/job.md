# AI Engineer

**Company:** OneMagnify
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
OneMagnify is an AI native, platform-enabled B2B digital agency that operates at the intersection of data, technology, and creativity. They are seeking a client-facing Technical AI Engineer who will support the development and deployment of AI initiatives, ensuring high-quality execution and timely delivery for clients.

## Core Skills
- Artificial Intelligence
- Data Science
- Large Language Models
- Object-oriented programming
- DataBricks

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree from a four-year college or university
- Experience with Data Science
- Artificial Intelligence, and Large Language Models
- Object-oriented programming experience
- Working knowledge of DataBricks
- 2+ years in a technical role with experience leading projects
- 2+ years if educational level is masters
- Solid understanding of data analytics and business intelligence tools such as Python
- R
- SQL
- Domo
- Tableau, and PowerBI
- Expert presentation skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree
- Experience in integrated marketing, digital agency, marketing services, or consulting environments

## Responsibilities
- As a technical developer our AI team, work with strategic clients to help them utilize their data to take action
- Stay up-to-date on the latest AI tools and features
- Provide Technical solutioning for our generative AI projects
- Participate in code reviews and solutioning sessions with our data scientists, ai engineers, software engineers, and other technical resources
- Lead cross-functional projects and enable collaboration across artificial intelligence and OneMagnify
