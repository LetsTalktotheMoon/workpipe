# Sr Machine Learning Engineer

**Company:** Adobe
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Adobe is a market leader in software products and services for the creative and marketing ecosystem. They are seeking a Senior Machine Learning Engineer to research, design, develop and deliver high-profile products that span GenAI features and customization of GenAI Firefly models for enterprise creators and marketers.

## Core Skills
- Machine learning
- Large language models
- Model training
- Model fine-tuning
- Model optimization

## Minimum qualifications
- B.S. or higher in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Mathematics or a related field
- 4+ years of hands-on experience building and deploying ML models in production
- Demonstrated expertise in training, finetuning, optimizing LLMs / large-scale models
- Proficiency in Python and deep knowledge of leading ML frameworks and libraries
- Strong passion for AI-driven innovation and a track record of delivering impactful, production-grade AI solutions
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to thrive in a fast-paced, collaborative environment

## Responsibilities
- Research, train, and deploy SOTA ML models for enterprise use-cases
- Leverage LLMs to analyze complex data and build advanced knowledge-extraction and insight-generation systems
- Explore and iterate on emerging LLM techniques to advance the frontier of Context Engineering
- Build and optimize agentic AI workflows that enable users to dynamically interact and refine outputs from ML systems
- Develop scalable frameworks to evaluate the accuracy, robustness and usability of complex AI agents
- Partner closely with product
- UX, business development and strategic partners to translate AI innovations into enterprise-grade production features
- Collaborate with AI Research team to constantly explore and evaluate emerging AI technologies and transform research ideas into interactive prototypes
