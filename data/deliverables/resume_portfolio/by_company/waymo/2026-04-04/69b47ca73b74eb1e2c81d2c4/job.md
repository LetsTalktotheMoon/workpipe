# Machine Learning Engineer - VLM/LLM Integration

**Company:** Waymo
**Location:** Mountain View, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with the mission to be the world's most trusted driver. They are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to develop machine learning solutions for autonomous driving, focusing on integrating large-scale ML models into production environments.

## Core Skills
- Python
- Machine Learning
- PyTorch
- TensorFlow
- Jax/Flax

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor or Master's degree in Computer Science, similar technical field of study, or equivalent practical experience
- 3+ years of experience with Python
- 3+ years of ML software experience with machine learning frameworks including PyTorch
- TensorFlow
- Jax/Flax

## Preferred qualifications
- Extensive ML infra experience: specifically in the automation of training, evaluating, and deploying large-scale models in production environments
- Deep learning experience, especially with generative models, e.g.
- LLMs/VLMs, and/or reinforcement learning
- Advanced proficiency in the internal architecture and performance tuning of ML frameworks like Pytorch
- JAX, or Tensorflow

## Responsibilities
- Work with a creative team of people who help to design, train, and evaluate the large-scale ML models that are used throughout Waymo’s systems, both onboard autonomous vehicles and offboard in simulation
- Development of ML framework and infrastructure required for integrating state-of-the-art LLM/VLM models into Waymo's diverse production environments
- Work with Waymo's perception and planning teams to transfer AI Foundational research into scalable and production-ready solutions
