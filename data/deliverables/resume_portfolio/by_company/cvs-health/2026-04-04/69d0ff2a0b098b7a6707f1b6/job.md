# Full Stack Associate Software Development Engineer (Java)

**Company:** CVS Health
**Location:** Scottsdale, AZ
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
CVS Health is focused on building a world of health around every individual, promoting a connected and compassionate health experience. The Full-Stack Developer will design and develop digital solutions for CVS Healthcare Identity, collaborating with various teams and ensuring the reliability of digital solutions through coding, testing, and documentation.

## Core Skills
- Java
- J2EE
- Spring Boot
- Spring MVC
- React JS

## Minimum qualifications
- This is a hybrid position that requires 2-3 days per week in the office. Office locations are Scottsdale
- AZ and Richardson
- TX. Candidates must live a commutable distance to either of these locations
- 6 Months+ of software development experience in building enterprise/web/cloud applications
- 6 Months+ of experience in programming languages using Java/J2EE
- Spring Boot and Spring MVC
- 6 Months+ of experience working in React JS OR Angular UI applications
- 6 Months+ of experience with building APIs and services using REST
- 6 Months+ of experience working with DevOps tools/processes for CICD (i.e. Jenkins
- Octopus)
- 6 Months+ of experience in build, testing, and deploying code to run on cloud infrastructure (AWS/GCP/Azure)
- 6 Months+ of experience with Scrum/Agile development methodologies
- 6 Months+ of experience working with RDBMS databases (e.g. SQL Server
- Oracle
- DB2
- Postgress)

## Preferred qualifications
- Exposure to cloud-based platforms is highly preferred (i.e. AWS
- GCP
- Azure)
- Capable of delivering on multiple competing priorities with little supervision
- Strong problem-solving skills and critical thinking ability
- Excellent verbal and written communication and collaboration skills
- Experience with monitoring application performance using apps like AppDynamics / Grafana / Splunk etc
- Knowledge of SAML and OAuth2 for authentication

## Responsibilities
- Partner and collaborate with the engineering managers, business analysts, architects, quality assurance, business partners, developers, and product owners to effectively translate the business requirements and architectural designs into engineering components
- You'll be involved throughout the entire development life cycle of a product and must be able to deliver an efficient solution, as well as identify and properly mitigate any risks to the product before deployment to production
- Understand business requirements and translate them into engineering solutions
- Write and execute unit tests, conduct code reviews, debug, and troubleshoot issues to ensure the reliability and stability of digital solutions
- Leverage advanced programming skills to design and implement complex features, optimize performance, and ensure code efficiency
- Integrate various software components or systems, ensuring seamless communication and interoperability between different parts of the digital solutions
- Participate in SAFe agile methodologies, attends meetings, and contributes to project planning and progress tracking
- Explore new tools, frameworks, and techniques to improve digital solutions and drive innovation within the development team
- Create and maintain technical documentation, including system designs, architecture diagrams, code documentation, and other relevant documentation while sharing their knowledge and expertise with team members, ensuring effective knowledge transfer and collaboration
