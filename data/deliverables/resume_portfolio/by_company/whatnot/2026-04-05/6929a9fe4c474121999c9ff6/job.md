# Software Engineer, New Grad

**Company:** Whatnot
**Location:** Los Angeles, CA
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Whatnot is the largest livestream shopping platform in North America and Europe, enabling users to buy, sell, and discover various products. The role involves building new features, implementing growth strategies, and maintaining a high-quality marketplace experience.

## Core Skills
- Software engineering
- Scalable systems
- Product development
- System development
- Debugging

## Minimum qualifications
- Graduating with a CS or Software Engineering degree in Dec 2025 or by Summer 2026
- Problem solver and out-of-the-box thinker
- Willing to take risks and build product and systems for 1M+ users
- Capable of building scalable systems
- Excellent product instincts. You first think about users rather than the best technical solution
- You are known for shipping products and features lightning-fast
- Ability to pick up on new technologies very quickly
- Open to relocation to Los Angeles
- San Francisco, or Seattle

## Responsibilities
- Building new features for users in our live-streams
- Implementing growth tactics and strategies to scale our buyer and seller flows
- Building systems at scale that help us deliver a high trust marketplace experience
- Writing product or system development code
- Designing, implementing, and operating our most critical and scalable services
- Carefully evaluating the technical tradeoffs of every decision
- Supporting features at every phase, from design through implementation and launch
- Triage product or system issues and debug/track/resolve by analyzing the sources of issues, network, or service operations and quality
