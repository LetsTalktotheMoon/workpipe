# Engineer I, Automation and Custom Applications

**Company:** Magnite
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Magnite is the world’s largest independent sell-side advertising platform, seeking an Engineer I for Automation and Custom Applications. In this role, you will design, develop, and maintain automation systems, create custom applications, and collaborate across teams to enhance productivity and efficiency.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- AI Agentic Workflows
- Context Window Management
- AI Orchestration Patterns
- Data Structures

## Minimum qualifications
- 0–2 years of professional or internship experience in software development
- A degree in Computer Science or a related field (or equivalent practical experience)
- A solid foundation in data structures, algorithms, and programming fundamentals
- Experience or interest in one or more of the following languages: Python
- JavaScript/TypeScript, or Java
- Curiosity about LLMs and applied AI — prompt design
- APIs, or workflow automation
- Exposure to development environments like Cursor or IDEs that integrate AI tools
- A collaborative mindset and strong communication skills
- A proactive, growth-oriented attitude — you're excited to learn from feedback and build new skills every week

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in AI Agentic Workflows: Deep understanding of context window management, dynamic skill sets, and orchestration patterns for coordinating multiple specialized AI agents

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain automation systems and backend services that enhance productivity and reliability
- Write clean, maintainable code and contribute to high-quality solutions under the guidance of senior engineers
- Build lightweight, purpose-built web applications that solve domain-specific challenges across Magnite’s ecosystem
- Use AI-assisted development tools (such as Cursor and Model Context Protocols) to accelerate coding, testing, and documentation
- Analyze how different teams work within Magnite’s products and systems
- Identify opportunities to automate or optimize manual processes, then implement reliable, scalable solutions to improve efficiency
- Partner with engineers, product managers, and operations teams to deeply understand Magnite’s products, user interactions, and the workflows that drive them
- Gain exposure to distributed systems, modern development environments, and automation frameworks while developing strong problem-solving and engineering fundamentals
- Participate in code reviews, share insights, and proactively suggest enhancements to our tools, infrastructure, and processes
