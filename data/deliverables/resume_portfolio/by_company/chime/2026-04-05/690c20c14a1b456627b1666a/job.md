# Software Engineer, Financial Platform

**Company:** Chime
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Chime is a financial technology company dedicated to helping individuals achieve financial progress. They are looking for a Software Engineer to develop a reliable and scalable financial platform that supports essential activities for millions of members, ensuring high performance and security.

## Core Skills
- Go
- Ruby
- Java
- Python
- Backend systems development

## Minimum qualifications
- Must have experience in Go
- Have 4+ years of engineering experience
- Have contributed to the development of backend systems
- Experience in payment processing systems
- Take a rigorous approach to testing backed development
- Experience with AWS or other cloud platforms
- Have an understanding of architectural patterns of large, mission-critical production services
- Have worked in continuous integration/deployment environments

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in additional backend languages such as Ruby
- Java and Python is preferred

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, test, and deploy components of a new financial platform
- Deliver secure, reliable, and high-performance infrastructure and APIs
- Work with our infrastructure engineering team to minimize the operational overhead of production systems you deploy
- Develop thoroughly tested code and conduct quality assurance on all new code alongside our QA team
- Learn and execute on agile methodologies, follow test driven development approach
- Engineers are required to participate in on-call rotation. Being on call may include responding to incidents outside of regular working hours when necessary
