# Full Stack Engineer

**Company:** Windfall
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Windfall is a people intelligence and AI company that gives go-to-market teams actionable insights. As a Full Stack Software Engineer, you will connect services, create APIs, and build UIs across multiple products in a collaborative environment, while contributing to innovative, AI-driven features for customer-facing applications and internal tooling.

## Core Skills
- Web application development
- Server-side development
- Object-oriented programming
- API development
- Frontend development

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of professional experience designing, building, and maintaining web applications
- Willingness to work a hybrid onsite schedule in our San Francisco office
- Experience developing server-side using an object-oriented approach, building API's, and working with a modern frontend framework
- Experience using transactional databases
- Ability to move quickly in a startup environment
- Impeccable attention to detail

## Responsibilities
- Improve, extend, and tie together existing backend services to support our internal facing applications
- Rapidly prototype new tools and features that will make an immediate impact on the business
- Contribute to a highly collaborative, feedback-driven, fast-paced engineering team
- Develop broad ownership over our internal applications including setting coding patterns, best practices, and guidelines
- Collaborate closely with our product team to plan and execute projects at rapid speed
