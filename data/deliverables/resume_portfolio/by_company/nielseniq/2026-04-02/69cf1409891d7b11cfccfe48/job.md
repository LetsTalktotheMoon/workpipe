# Senior Data Scientist

**Company:** NielsenIQ
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
NielsenIQ is the world’s leading consumer intelligence company, delivering insights into consumer buying behavior. The Senior Data Scientist will develop statistical and machine learning models to predict consumer behavior, lead the design and deployment of AI models, and collaborate with analytics teams to provide tailored data insights.

## Core Skills
- Statistical modeling
- Machine learning
- Artificial intelligence
- Data integration
- Model validation

## Minimum qualifications
- Master's degree in Marketing Science
- Mathematics
- Operations Research
- Computer Science
- Statistics
- Financial, and Risk Management, or related
- 3 years of experience

## Responsibilities
- Developing statistical, machine learning, and AI models to predict consumer behavior by integrating survey, household, and retail sales data
- Leading the design, prototyping, and deployment of AI and machine learning models and algorithms to enhance product and marketing success predictions
- Validating and calibrating models and algorithms to estimate the likelihood of success and volume potential of future products, ensuring accuracy and robustness
- Automating key processes, developing tools to streamline existing workflows, and ensuring scalability and reproducibility of models
- Collaborating with analytics teams to address custom client requests, offering recommendations on complex study designs, and delivering tailored data insights
- Staying at the forefront of relevant fields (Statistics
- Machine Learning
- Operations Research
- Marketing Science
- Artificial Intelligence), continuously enhancing model performance with the latest techniques
