# Senior Data Analyst - Equity Research

**Company:** Western Governors University
**Location:** SLC Metro Area
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Western Governors University (WGU) is dedicated to expanding access to higher education through innovative online programs. As a Senior Data Analyst on the Equity Research team, you will lead analyses to uncover disparities in student outcomes and present actionable insights to drive social change and inform institutional strategies.

## Core Skills
- Data Analytics
- Institutional Research
- Education Equity Analytics
- SQL
- Tableau

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in a related field
- 5+ years of experience in data analytics, institutional research, or education equity analytics
- Experience working with large-scale, complex, and high-volume data sets across enterprise or distributed data environments
- Advanced proficiency in SQL and intermediate proficiency in Tableau

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience analyzing equity gaps in student or community outcomes
- Experience analyzing survey data and sense of belonging measures
- Familiarity with qualitative or mixed methods approaches
- Experience working with education nonprofits, philanthropy, or mission-driven institutions

## Responsibilities
- Lead equity-focused analyses using SQL and Tableau to uncover disparities in student outcomes
- Translate findings into actionable recommendations for teams like Financial Aid
- Academic Programs, and Learning Analytics
- Build dashboards that track institutional progress on equity metrics
- Present insights to diverse audiences—from senior leadership to philanthropic partners
- Help guide junior team members and contribute to a collaborative, inquiry-driven culture
