# Data Analyst II

**Company:** Lennar
**Location:** Miami, FL (Job Posting Location)
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Lennar is one of the nation's leading homebuilders, dedicated to making an impact and creating an extraordinary experience for their Homeowners, Communities, and Associates. In this mid-level role, you will conduct complex data analyses, create advanced reports and dashboards, and mentor junior analysts while contributing to data process improvements.

## Core Skills
- Data Analysis
- Data Cleaning
- Python
- R
- Tableau

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in a related field
- advanced coursework or certifications preferred
- 3+ years of related experience
- Ability to articulate data-driven insights clearly to a diverse audience
- Identifies issues in data and processes, proposing effective solutions
- Builds strong working relationships across teams and functions
- Provide guidance and support to Data Analyst I in their daily tasks
- Experience with Data Warehousing concepts on AWS Platform & Snowflake
- Upholds ethical standards and sets a positive example for junior team members
- Manages complex challenges with poise, maintaining focus under pressure
- Actively contributes to change initiatives and encourages innovation within the team

## Responsibilities
- Conduct more complex data analysis and develop insights that support business objectives
- Create and maintain more advanced reports and dashboards for various stakeholders
- Work closely with business units to translate their needs into data-driven solutions
- Assist in developing data models to support predictive and prescriptive analytics
- Provide guidance and support to Data Analyst 1 in their daily tasks
- Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in data sets and present findings to senior analysts
- Contribute to the development of best practices for data management and analysis
- Proficient in advanced data cleaning techniques, such as normalization, handling categorical variables, and merging datasets
- Experience using tools like Python (pandas
- NumPy) or R for complex data manipulation
- Ability to create interactive dashboards (Tableau/Power BI), use calculated fields, and design complex visualizations that tell a compelling data story
