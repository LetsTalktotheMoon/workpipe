# Specialist, Technology Engineer (Cloud Security Engineer)

**Company:** Nationwide
**Location:** Ohio - Columbus, Three Nationwide Plaza
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Nationwide is a Fortune 100 company focused on delivering extraordinary care to its customers. The Cloud Security Engineering team is seeking a hands-on technologist to implement and mature cloud controls, ensuring security and compliance across cloud platforms while promoting a DevSecOps culture.

## Core Skills
- Cloud platforms
- Microsoft Azure
- Amazon Web Services
- Cloud security engineering
- DevSecOps

## Minimum qualifications
- Hands‑on experience with any major cloud platform (Azure preferred
- AWS
- GCP, or OCI are a plus)
- Experience engineering or operating cloud tools, platforms, or automation solutions
- Familiarity with security concepts in at least one of the following areas: IAM, vulnerability management, data protection, logging/monitoring, network controls, or encryption
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines, cloud provisioning, and automation tools
- Understanding of containers, serverless technologies, or orchestration platforms
- Proficiency in scripting, programming, or coding (Bash
- Python
- PowerShell
- Java, etc.)
- Strong interpersonal, collaboration, consulting, and documentation skills
- Demonstrated ability to build, refine, and mature processes and metrics

## Preferred qualifications
- Familiarity with security or compliance frameworks (NIST
- CIS Benchmarks
- PCI DSS)
- Experience in architecture, engineering, operations, compliance, risk management, or audit functions
- Understanding of defensive technologies (IPS
- WAF
- HIPS
- AV, etc.)
- Knowledge of network and system attack vectors and corresponding mitigation approaches
- Experience with log analysis platforms such as Splunk
- Prior experience supporting audits or remediating audit findings
- Technical experience in at least one foundational discipline: networking, servers, or endpoint engineering
- Understanding of how to evaluate, adopt, and responsibly apply AI driven tools

## Responsibilities
- Promote DevSecOps culture including training / outreach to application development teams with special focus on security controls (IAM, security compliance)
- Design, build, and/or deploy tools and reports that provide visibility into cloud accounts, instances, and services
- Implement infrastructure-as-code and CI/CD automation to standardize and scale cloud security team deployments
- Stay current on cloud security vulnerabilities, policies, standards, regulations to ensure cloud environments are managing risk appropriately
- Continuously work with business partners on vulnerability remediation strategies
- Partner with security teams to enhance cloud security solutions and establish preventative controls to support security needs via automation
- Partner with security teams to ensure migrated workloads maintain compliance and governance requirements
- Collaborate with Information Risk Management to ensure all cloud products / services are meeting configuration and compliance standards
- Implement and support data protection and encryption strategies for cloud services, products, and data
- Implement and support best practice requirements for logging and monitoring
