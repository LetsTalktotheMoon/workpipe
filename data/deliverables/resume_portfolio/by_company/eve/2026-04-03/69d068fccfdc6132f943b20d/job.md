# Product Engineer (Full Stack)

**Company:** Eve
**Location:** San Mateo, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Eve is redefining legal technology for plaintiff law firms, and they are seeking a Product Engineer to join their innovative team. In this role, you will be responsible for developing core software, driving product direction, and building AI-native solutions to streamline legal operations while collaborating closely with customers and internal teams.

## Core Skills
- Full-Stack Development
- Front-End Development
- Back-End Development
- AI Integration
- AI Application Development

## Minimum qualifications
- Proven track record of building incredible products and thrive in environments where you're the primary decision-maker for your projects
- Comfortable working across the entire stack, from front-end user interfaces to back-end services and AI integrations
- Strongest on the front-end, but confident and willing to dive into back-end work when needed
- Experience or strong interest in building with AI and a desire to gain more experience creating production applications using AI
- Value shipping quickly and iterating based on real-world usage
- Enjoy working collaboratively but are also highly effective when working independently

## Responsibilities
- Develop Core Software: Build, ship, and maintain features that make up Eve’s core product
- Drive Product Direction: Collaborate with the team to understand user needs, define new features, and decide on implementation strategies
- Full-Stack Development: Own and scale the user-facing experience of Eve products, turning powerful capabilities into intuitive, magical interfaces
- Collaborate with backend
- ML, design, and product teams to bring complex features to life in a seamless way
- AI Integration: Design and build AI-native solutions that power and streamline every aspect of law firm operations, creating tools that transform how legal work gets done
- Rapid Deployment: Embrace a fast shipping cadence, continuously iterating based on user feedback
- Work with Customers: You work with customers directly to solve their problems - innovate and iterate
