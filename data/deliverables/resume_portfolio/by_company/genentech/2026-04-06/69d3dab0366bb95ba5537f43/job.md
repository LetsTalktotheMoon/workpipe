# Machine Learning Engineer/Senior Machine Learning Engineer - Infra

**Company:** Genentech
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Genentech is a leading biotechnology company focused on innovation and healthcare access. They are seeking a highly motivated and skilled Machine Learning Engineer/Senior Machine Learning Engineer to enhance their ML infrastructure, collaborating closely with engineers and data scientists to address their needs and drive technical solutions.

## Core Skills
- AWS
- Python
- C++
- Go
- Rust

## Minimum qualifications
- BS/MS with 2-3 years of industry experience required
- Proven experience designing, deploying, and managing infrastructure on AWS (EC2
- S3
- RDS, etc.)
- Proficiency in scripting languages (e.g.
- Python) and compiled languages (e.g.
- C++
- Go
- Rust)
- Strong experience with Git and repository management
- Excellent problem-solving skills and a proactive attitude, with the ability to anticipate needs and drive projects to completion
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams and influence technical decisions
- Ability to thrive in a fast-paced environment and adapt to evolving requirements while adhering to corporate guidelines
- BS/MS with 3-5 years of industry experience required
- Demonstrated ability to lead technical projects from conception to completion and deliver high-quality, scalable, and reliable software

## Preferred qualifications
- Extensive experience with large-scale ML model platforms and tools
- Deep understanding and experience with PyTorch implementation, especially regarding scaling inference performance
- A history of significant contributions to open-source projects or industry standards

## Responsibilities
- Design, implement, and maintain scalable and reliable ML infrastructure
- Collaborate closely with ML engineers and data scientists to understand and address their infrastructure needs
- Document infrastructure designs, processes, and operational procedures
- Proactively identify and solve complex, often cross-functional, technical problems within the ML infrastructure domain
- Mentor junior engineers and drive technical alignment and influence across teams
