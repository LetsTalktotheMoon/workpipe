# Senior Devops Engineer

**Company:** Avive Solutions Inc.
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Avive Solutions, Inc. is a VC-backed healthcare technology company focused on revolutionizing cardiac arrest response with their advanced Automated External Defibrillator (AED) and software platform. The Senior DevOps Engineer will play a key role in designing and maintaining cloud-based infrastructure to support the company's innovative products and ensure efficient deployment processes.

## Core Skills
- Linux command line
- Bash scripting
- Python scripting
- API/SDK usage
- Infrastructure as Code (IaC)

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Information Technology
- Engineering, or related field or compensating additional experience in lieu of degree
- 3-7 years of experience in a DevOps or similar role, focusing on automation coding, system integration, and operations
- Ability to work comfortably in a command line a Linux computing environment
- Proficiency in scripting languages (Bash & Python) and use of APIs/SDKs
- Proficiency in IaC languages (Pulumi
- Terraform)
- Proficiency in CI/CD tools (GitHub Actions
- ArgoCD)
- Knowledge of containerization technologies (Docker
- Kubernetes
- AWS ECR/EKS)
- Strong knowledge of and good ability to work with source control systems (Git)
- Strong experience working in cloud-based environments (AWS)
- Strong experience in root cause analysis and resolution of operational issues

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with ElasticSearch
- ElasticSearch Agent & Kibana
- Experience with setup and deployment of Kubernetes Dashboard
- Experience with website certificate management
- Experience with WireGuard VPN
- Proficiency in Linux administration, networking and firewalls
- Experience with security best practices and tools
- Experience incorporating security compliance and best practices into DevOps automation workflows (e.g.
- DevSecOps)
- Professional certifications in cloud platforms, security, or DevOps technologies are highly desirable
- Enthusiasm for learning new technologies, improving existing processes, and staying current with industry trends

## Responsibilities
- Design, implement, and maintain robust, scalable infrastructure and automation solutions
- Automate and optimize infrastructure provisioning, configuration management, deployment processes and operational repetitive tasks
- Execute infrastructure and application deployments to upper cloud environments
- Develop, maintain, optimize CI/CD pipelines and operational workflows
- Monitor & optimize system performance & resource utilization and identify bottlenecks
- Implement scalable and reliable systems that support product growth and team agility
- Troubleshoot, determine root causes and resolve technical issues
- Participate in code reviews and provide feedback on best practices
- Collaborate cross-functionally with cloud, firmware, and hardware development teams to integrate and align solutions
- Document operational processes, tools, and workflows to ensure knowledge sharing and smooth transitions
