# Software Dev Engr Sr

**Company:** CSG
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
CSG is a company that empowers businesses to connect with their customers through innovative technology solutions. They are seeking a Software Developer Engineer to enhance and support their customer-facing applications while collaborating with various teams to ensure usability and functionality.

## Core Skills
- C#
- NET WinForms
- Web Services
- Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA)
- SQL Server

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Computer Engineering, related field, or equivalent experience
- Proficiency in English in a business environment
- 5 years of development experience with C#, .NET WinForms
- Web Services
- SOA
- SQL Server and OO design
- Experience with Scrum / Kanban environments within an Agile environment
- Leader in utilizing AI tools

## Responsibilities
- Report to a Sr. Manager Software Development to contribute to the Integrated Customer Solutions Development Team to support the enhancement, maintenance, and support of ACSR
- ACP Voice
- Backoffice
- MCP
- Payment Manager
- CIT, and ACP Order Management
- Design, analyzes, writes code, supports, and debugs CSG's customer-facing applications in collaboration with product owners, developers, testers and operational engineers
- Participate in planning, retrospectives, daily scrum, demo and team design meetings
- Communicate with product owners, developers, testers, operational engineers, clients and end-users in a manner that helps enhance the usability of the applications
