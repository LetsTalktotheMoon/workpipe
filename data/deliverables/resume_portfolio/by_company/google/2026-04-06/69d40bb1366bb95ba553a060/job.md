# Business Intelligence Generalist, Product Data Warehouse

**Company:** Google
**Location:** Chicago, IL
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Google is a leading technology company seeking a Business Intelligence Generalist for their Product Data Warehouse team. The role involves leading the measurement and monitoring of data products, developing KPIs, dashboards, and conducting analyses to provide insights and drive sales initiatives.

## Core Skills
- Business Intelligence
- Data Analytics
- SQL
- Data Visualization
- Looker

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
- 2 years of experience in business intelligence or data analytics
- 2 years of experience in SQL and data visualization (e.g.
- Looker
- Tableau
- Power BI
- Qlik, or similar technologies)

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with data warehousing or data modeling concepts
- Experience with big data technologies (e.g.
- Hadoop
- Spark
- Python, or R)
- Experience with translating business requirements to BI solutions
- Experience with explaining technical analysis to stakeholders

## Responsibilities
- Drive Ads and Measurement feature adoption by developing scalable, shareable sales tools, dashboards, data, and programs using internal tools
- Synthesize advertiser data and conduct analyses to deliver insights and actionable recommendations for product and sales leadership
- Define and prioritize KPIs to drive key global initiatives that will be used by regions, countries, and sales pods in all sales channels
- Be a strategic thought partner to commercial leaders of products
- Manage special projects as assigned
