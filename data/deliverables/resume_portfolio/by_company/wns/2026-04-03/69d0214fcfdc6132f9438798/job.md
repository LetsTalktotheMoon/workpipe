# Data Scientist

**Company:** WNS
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
WNS is a company that values learning, data, scale, and agility. They are seeking a Data Scientist who will leverage collaboration skills to extract insights from complex data sets and manage analytics projects in the retail domain.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Data Bricks
- PowerBI
- Predictive Modeling
- Machine Learning

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in quantitative field (Statistics
- Mathematics
- Computer Science
- Engineering, etc.)
- At least 2-3 years' of experience in quantitative analytics or data modeling
- Deep understanding of predictive modeling, machine-learning, clustering and classification techniques, and algorithms
- Familiarity with Big Data frameworks and visualization tools (Cassandra
- Hadoop
- Spark
- Tableau)
- Strong collaboration skills
- Ability to extract valuable insights from highly complex data sets
- Hands-on experience on SQL
- Data Bricks
- PowerBI
- Understanding of Data Science and its application into business
- Ability to create Business & technical presentations, reports etc. to present the analysis findings to the end clients and for business development

## Responsibilities
- Strong domain knowledge and thought leadership in retail domain
- The ideal candidate would be adept at understanding customer's business challenges and define appropriate analytics approach to design solution
- Candidate should be able to think from first principles to define & evangelize solutions for any business problem
- This is a hands-on role and will be required to manage day to day delivery activities and guide the team in executing analytics projects by analyzing large volume of data
- Ideal candidate should have hands on experience on SQL
- Data Bricks
- PowerBI
- Should understand Data Science and its application into business
- He / She would also be responsible for creating Business & technical presentations, reports etc. to present the analysis findings to the end clients and for business development
