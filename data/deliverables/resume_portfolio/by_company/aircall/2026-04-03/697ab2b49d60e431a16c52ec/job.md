# Software Engineer

**Company:** Aircall
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Aircall is a unicorn, AI-powered customer communications platform used by 22,000+ companies worldwide. They are seeking a Software Engineer to join a new team focused on building an ambitious product that leverages voice and AI to transform sales operations.

## Core Skills
- Full-stack development
- AWS services
- Cloud-based architectures
- Software development practices
- Scalable system architecture

## Minimum qualifications
- At least 3 years of professional experience in full-stack development
- Experience with AWS services and cloud-based architectures
- Deep understanding of software development practices, scalable system architecture, security best practices and cloud-based platforms
- Exceptional analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to effectively troubleshoot complex issues
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
- you thrive working closely with product managers, designers, and cross-functional teams
- Comfortable working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment with evolving priorities

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience building products from 0-to-1 or in early-stage team environments
- Experience with the complete software development life cycle, from design to deployment and operation
- Experience with building secure, scalable, and performant software

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate closely with product managers, designers, and other stakeholders to translate requirements into technical solutions
- Have key responsibilities within the design, architecture, and end-to-end delivery of features across the full stack
- Build and maintain frontend interfaces with React and backend services with Node.js and TypeScript
- Write clean scalable code and test and deploy applications and systems
- Revise, update, refactor, and debug code
- Participate in code reviews to ensure code quality and share knowledge
- Be part of an engineering organization delivering high quality, secure, voice solutions to Aircall clients
- Involvement in product and platform performance optimization and live site monitoring
- Participate in an on-call rotation to respond to critical system issues and ensure continuous system reliability
- Security is at the forefront of your mind in everything that you do
