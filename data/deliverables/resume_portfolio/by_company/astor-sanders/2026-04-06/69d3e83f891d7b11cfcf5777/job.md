## About the job
Astor & Sanders Corporation is an award-winning IT solutions provider headquartered in McLean, VA, seeking a skilled Drupal Developer to join their dynamic team. The role involves designing, developing, and maintaining Drupal-based websites and applications, implementing custom modules and themes, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver effective Drupal solutions.

## Core Skills
- Drupal development
- PHP
- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
- Strong proficiency in PHP
- HTML
- CSS
- JavaScript, and MySQL
- Experience creating and customizing Drupal modules and themes
- Familiarity with Drupal's API, hooks, and command-line tools such as Drush
- Understanding of web standards, accessibility, and responsive design principles
- Experience with version control systems like Git
- Ability to work collaboratively in an Agile development environment
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of professional experience developing with Drupal (versions 8 or higher preferred)

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain Drupal-based websites and applications
- Implement custom modules and themes tailored to project requirements
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and translate them into Drupal solutions
- Ensure code quality through peer reviews and adherence to best practices
- Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to Drupal sites, modules, and integrations
- Integrate third-party APIs and services as needed
- Keep up-to-date with Drupal core updates and contributed modules to maintain security and performance
- Document development processes, configurations, and customizations
- Provide support and training to end-users when necessary
