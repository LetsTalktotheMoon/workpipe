# Business Intelligence Analyst

**Company:** Helm
**Location:** Plymouth, MI
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
HELM is seeking a Business Intelligence Analyst to analyze performance data and drive marketing strategies for renowned brands. The role involves generating reports, identifying trends, and collaborating with stakeholders to optimize reporting solutions aligned with corporate objectives.

## Core Skills
- Business Intelligence
- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI
- Microsoft Office
- Advanced Analytics

## Minimum qualifications
- BA/BS in Business or a related field
- 3-5 years previous experience in any one or more of these disciplines: BA
- PM -OR- Recent successful internship in a relative field
- Proficient computer skills including Microsoft Excel
- Power BI
- Microsoft Office
- Email and Web
- Strong organizational and project management skills
- An understanding of Advanced Analytics, and various Data Science techniques
- Some knowledge in either Python
- R, and/or other Advanced Analytical languages or tools
- Outstanding customer service skills

## Responsibilities
- Provide ongoing reports by generating and providing analysis
- Interpret data and analyze results using statistical techniques
- Develop and implement data analytics and other strategies that optimize efficiency and quality
- Acquire data from primary or secondary data sources
- Identify, analyze, and interpret trends or patterns in complex data sets
- Filter and 'clean' data by reviewing reports and performance indicators to locate and correct problems
- Locate and define new process improvement opportunities
- Use knowledge of the specific data available, forecasting/analysis methods and visualization capabilities to enhance requirements and provide insightful suggestions to business stakeholders
- Communicate analysis and findings clearly to senior leaders and non-technical audiences
- Make recommendations for new data visualizations to measure business & programs
