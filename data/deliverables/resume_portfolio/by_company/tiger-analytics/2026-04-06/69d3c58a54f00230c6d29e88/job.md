**Company:** Tiger Analytics
**Location:** Virginia, United States

## About the job
Tiger Analytics is a company focused on cloud solutions, and they are seeking a Cloud Engineer to manage cloud infrastructure and services. The role involves designing and managing scalable data platforms, implementing DevOps practices, and ensuring robust cloud networking fundamentals.

## Core Skills

- Azure
- Cloud infrastructure

- Strong experience with cloud infrastructure and services across AWS and/or Azure
- Data pipelines

- DevOps practices, including CI/CD pipelines

- Cloud networking fundamentals (VPC/VNet, routing, firewalls, load balancing)
