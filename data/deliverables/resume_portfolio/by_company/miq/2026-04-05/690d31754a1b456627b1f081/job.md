# Solutions Engineer

**Company:** MiQ
**Location:** New York, United States
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
MiQ is a global programmatic media partner for marketers and agencies, dedicated to leading the programmatic industry. As a Solutions Engineer, you will collaborate with various teams to scope client analytics opportunities and deliver tailored solutions, ensuring high-quality project execution and effective communication with stakeholders.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- Tableau
- Cloud computing platforms
- Data analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Analytics, or a related field
- 2-4 years in the field of programmatic media, digital marketing, or analytics
- Knowledge of SQL
- Python
- Tableau, and cloud computing platforms
- Experience with data analysis, data design, and technical storytelling
- Experience working with clients and solving their media & analytics needs
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills
- Experience in project management a plus
- Great attention to detail
- Ability to work as part of a team
- Excellent critical thinking, verbal, and written communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- A master's degree is a plus

## Responsibilities
- Creating custom project proposals, automating and executing repeatable workflows, building dashboards and decks to deliver technical outputs in a client-friendly format
- Accomplishing tasks on time and to a high quality, managing different deadlines and priorities
- Collaborating with engineering and analytics teams to build solution development roadmap and deliver outputs
- Staying focused and contributing to projects. Organizing stakeholders and multiple milestones
- Providing technical assistance alongside other departments to develop existing solutions and find new solutions for MiQ clients
- Proactively identifying and filling process and knowledge gaps to grow our capabilities as a team
- Working with internal stakeholders, building relationships and working across differences to find solutions
- Working with clients to understand current challenges and formulate problem statements to be further developed into custom solutions
- Crisp and clear communication to external partners/clients or internal stakeholders
- flexing communication style to persuade others and influence positive outcomes
- Being the voice of the market, providing development ideas to Data Science and Product team to fuel product innovation
