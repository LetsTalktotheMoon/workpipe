# Frontend Engineer, AI Observability & Evals Platform

**Company:** LangChain
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
LangChain is on a mission to make intelligent agents ubiquitous, providing a platform for building, evaluating, deploying, and operating AI agents at scale. The Frontend Engineer will develop and enhance features on LangSmith, focusing on user-facing components and collaborating with cross-functional teams.

## Core Skills
- React
- JavaScript
- TypeScript
- Babel
- Vite

## Minimum qualifications
- Front-end engineering experience with strong proficiency in React
- JavaScript and TypeScript
- Hands-on experience with front-end development tools like Babel
- Vite
- Webpack
- NPM, and Yarn
- Familiarity with REST APIs and experience working closely with fullstack and backend engineers
- Knowledge of modern authorization mechanisms, such as JWTs
- A passion for user experience, design and building clean, engaging, and intuitive interfaces
- Strong written and oral communication skills, with the ability to explain technical concepts clearly and concisely to both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- The DNA to thrive in a start-up, fast-paced environment. Views unstructured environments as an opportunity to figure out the most impactful work and help define the future success of the company
- An ownership mind-set. You are self-driven and look for opportunities to make an impact
- Strong computer science fundamentals, ideally with a degree in computer science or related field

## Responsibilities
- Develop new user-facing features using React & Typescript
- Build reusable components and front-end libraries for future use
- Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code
- Optimize components for maximum performance across a vast array of web-capable devices and browsers
- Collaborate with fullstack and backend developers and UX/UI designers, to enhance usability
