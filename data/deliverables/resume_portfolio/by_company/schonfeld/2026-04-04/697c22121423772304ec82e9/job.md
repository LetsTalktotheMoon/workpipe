# Platform Engineer

**Company:** Schonfeld
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Schonfeld is a global multi-manager hedge fund that strives to deliver industry-leading risk-adjusted returns for its investors. The Platform Engineer will design, build, and maintain the quantitative development platform, ensuring its high availability and scalability while collaborating with quant and development teams for seamless application deployments.

## Core Skills
- Python
- Kubernetes
- Container Orchestration
- AWS
- Azure

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent experience)
- Experience architecting end‑to‑end solutions in partnership with our quant and dev teams
- Strong coding skills in at least one language, ideally Python, but open to other languages
- Strong knowledge of Kubernetes and container orchestration
- Extensive experience with major cloud providers (AWS
- Azure) and cloud-native technologies
- Experience building and maintaining CI/CD pipelines leveraging DevOps practices
- Excellent cultural fit and ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to effectively communicate with Portfolio managers

## Preferred qualifications
- Ability to understand and model business workflows in a financial institution, and reflect them in platform and tooling decisions
- Passionate about cloud-native technologies and has a strong desire to learn and grow
- Has a deep understanding of Infrastructure as Code (IaC) principles and practices
- Is adept at translating complex requirements into robust technical solutions
- Is committed to driving engineering excellence and continuous improvement
- Have experience in tools like coder and prefect

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate with quant and development teams to ensure seamless application deployments, and to establish comprehensive coding environments from day one
- Automate infrastructure provisioning and management processes
- Contribute and lead efforts to continuously improve the platform reliability, security, and cost optimization
- Participate in L3 on-call rotations and incident response processes
- Mentor and upskill team members on cloud-native technologies and best practices
