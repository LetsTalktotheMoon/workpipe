# Senior Data Engineer

**Company:** EliseAI
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
EliseAI is focused on improving housing and healthcare industries by integrating AI agents into workflows to enhance efficiency and reduce costs. As a Senior Data Engineer, you will play a key role in building and maintaining data infrastructure, optimizing data flow, and supporting data-driven decision-making across the organization.

## Core Skills
- Data engineering
- Python
- PostgreSQL
- Relational databases
- Data modeling

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of professional experience in data engineering
- Programming proficiency in python
- Experience with PostgreSQL or other relational databases
- Proven experience in data modeling and database design
- Experience with ETL design, implementation, and maintenance
- Knowledge of data warehousing solutions, particularly Snowflake
- Understanding of development workflows and best practices
- Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail
- Strong communication and collaboration skills
- Ability to work independently and as part of a team

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with data quality management and monitoring tools
- Familiarity with AWS services
- Familiarity with data visualization techniques and tools
- Knowledge of machine learning pipelines and tools
- Experience with real-time data streaming platforms
- Familiarity with DBT
- Airflow

## Responsibilities
- Completely own our data pipeline, playing a crucial role in designing, implementing, and maintaining our data infrastructure and ETL pipelines
- Lead the engineering team in making sure all code processes produce meaningful data that is stored in a performant manner
- Build and maintain high-quality data solutions and large-scale data pipelines that process vast amounts of data
- Optimize data flow and collection for cross-functional teams
- Design core data objects and models for the entire company
- Implement and maintain data quality checks and monitoring systems
- Develop client-facing reporting solutions
- Support data-driven decision-making through the creation of metrics datasets and dashboards
- Collaborate across teams to optimize and improve data quality while delivering user-centric experiences
