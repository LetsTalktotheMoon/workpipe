# Business Operations Analyst

**Company:** Motorola Solutions
**Location:** Chicago, IL
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Motorola Solutions is a global community focused on keeping people safer through technology. They are seeking a Business Operations Analyst to design and develop methods for analyzing big data to generate actionable insights for client services and product enhancement.

## Core Skills
- Data Analysis
- Machine Learning
- Big Data
- Data Integration
- Algorithm Development

## Minimum qualifications
- Masters degree or Bachelors degree + 2 years experience

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop methods, processes, and systems to consolidate and analyze unstructured, diverse big data sources to generate actionable insights and solutions for client services and product enhancement
- Interact with product and service teams to identify questions and issues for data analysis and experiments
- Identify meaningful insights from large data and metadata sources
- Interpret and communicate insights and findings from analysis and experiments to product, service, and business managers
- Develop and implement machine learning models and algorithms to cleanse, integrate, and analyze large datasets from multiple disparate sources
