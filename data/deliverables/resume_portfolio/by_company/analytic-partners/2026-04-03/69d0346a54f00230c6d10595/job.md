# Data Science Analyst

**Company:** Analytic Partners
**Location:** Miami, FL
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Analytic Partners is a global leader in commercial measurement and optimization, turning data into expertise for the world’s largest brands for almost 25 years. They are seeking a highly analytical and driven Solution Analyst / Data Scientist to apply advanced data analysis techniques and AI-driven solutions to solve complex business problems in marketing analytics.

## Core Skills
- Mathematics
- Econometrics
- Statistics
- Python
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Strong academic background in Mathematics
- Econometrics
- Statistics
- Proficiency in Python
- SQL, and/or R for data analysis and modeling
- Experience with data manipulation, statistical analysis, and model development
- Solid understanding of econometric techniques and their business applications
- Knowledge of AI agent frameworks (e.g.
- LangChain) and LLM applications
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to work with complex datasets

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in marketing analytics, such as marketing mix modeling, attribution, or customer analytics
- Experience building end-to-end data science solutions or analytics platforms
- Familiarity with cloud platforms and data engineering concepts
- Experience with visualization tools (e.g.
- Tableau
- Power BI, or Python libraries)

## Responsibilities
- Analyze large, structured and unstructured datasets to extract actionable insights and support business decision-making
- Develop and implement data-driven solutions for marketing performance measurement, including ROI analysis and optimization
- Design and build statistical and econometric models (e.g., marketing mix models, forecasting models)
- Leverage AI and machine learning techniques, including AI agents and LLM-based solutions, to enhance analytical capabilities
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate business requirements into analytical frameworks and technical solutions
- Identify opportunities to apply advanced analytics to improve marketing efficiency and effectiveness
- Automate data pipelines, reporting, and analytical workflows using modern tools and technologies
- Communicate insights clearly to stakeholders through data visualization and storytelling
