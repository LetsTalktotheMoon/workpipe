# Data Engineer

**Company:** Capstone Investment Advisors
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Capstone Investment Advisors is a leading asset management firm specializing in derivatives and complementary strategies. As a Data Engineer, you will build and maintain a market data platform, develop data solutions for investment professionals, and ensure data quality and integrity while working closely with product and portfolio managers.

## Core Skills
- Python
- SQL
- Market data platform pipelines
- Financial data
- Snowflake

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or a related field
- 4 – 6 years in a software development focused role
- Proficient in Python and SQL
- Excellent communication skills and the ability to work with, and inspire, a wide cross section of data professionals

## Preferred qualifications
- Knowledge of fixed income products (options, swaps, futures, etc.)
- Familiarity with AWS ecosystem
- Understanding of streaming pipelines
- Awareness of key financial vendors (e.g. Bloomberg
- Refinitiv
- FactSet)
- Knowledge of OLAP platforms and workloads using tools like Snowflake or Trino
- Experience with timeseries databases (e.g. ArcticDB
- KDB+)
- Experience working with trading teams, data teams and infrastructure teams

## Responsibilities
- Contribute to the design, build and support of a world class data platform that covers real time market data, unstructured data and alternative data
- Develop data solutions that enable investment professionals to efficiently extract insights from data
- Build tools and automation capabilities for data pipelines that improve the efficiency, quality and resilience of our data platform
- Working directly with Product and Portfolio Managers to understand their data needs and provide end-to-end data solutions that address requirements
- Work with timeseries databases (e.g. ArcticDB
- KDB+) to store, query, and manage dense time-series data effectively
- Build out data quality and integrity tools to ensure robust data validation and error handling
- Investigate data issues, working with data providers to ensure seamless interconnection between different data sets
