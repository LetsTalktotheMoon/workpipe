# Co-Pilot Engineer

**Company:** O3 Technology Solutions
**Location:** Princeton, NJ
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
O3 Technology Solutions is seeking a skilled Co-pilot Engineer to design, build, and optimize AI-powered copilot solutions that enhance user experience and productivity. The role involves developing AI copilot solutions, integrating them with enterprise systems, and collaborating with various stakeholders to improve workflows and response quality.

## Core Skills
- AI/ML
- Generative AI
- Large language models
- Prompt engineering
- AI workflow design

## Minimum qualifications
- 3 to 5 years of experience in AI/ML, software engineering, automation, or related roles
- Experience with AI/ML concepts, generative AI, and large language models
- Strong understanding of prompt engineering and AI workflow design
- Hands-on experience with APIs, automation, and system integration
- Experience working with user interface development technologies
- Ability to interact with customers and translate business needs into technical solutions
- Knowledge of cloud platforms such as Azure
- AWS, or Google Cloud
- Strong problem-solving, communication, and collaboration skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Microsoft Copilot
- Copilot Studio, or similar AI assistant platforms
- Knowledge of Python
- JavaScript
- React
- Power Platform, or low-code tools
- Experience with RAG, agentic workflows, or conversational AI
- Understanding of security, privacy, and compliance in enterprise AI solutions

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop AI copilot solutions using modern AI platforms and frameworks
- Build and optimize prompts, workflows, and orchestration logic to improve response quality and accuracy
- Develop and enhance user interfaces to deliver intuitive and user-friendly experiences
- Integrate copilot solutions with enterprise systems
- APIs, and business applications
- Interact directly with customers and business users to gather requirements, understand use cases, and provide solution support
- Collaborate with product managers, designers, data engineers, and stakeholders to define use cases and deliver solutions
- Test, validate, and fine-tune copilot performance based on user feedback and system metrics
- Monitor, troubleshoot, and improve the reliability, security, and scalability of AI-driven solutions
- Document technical designs, configurations, and deployment processes
- Stay current with advancements in generative AI
- LLMs, and Copilot-related technologies
