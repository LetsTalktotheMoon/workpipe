# Machine Learning Scientist - GenAI, KIT

**Company:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
**Location:** Bellevue, WA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon Web Services (AWS) is the world's most comprehensive and broadly adopted cloud platform. They are seeking an Applied Scientist to drive innovation with Gen AI, focusing on building advanced AI systems and optimizing cloud services to enhance customer experience.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Generative Artificial Intelligence
- Natural Language Processing
- Deep Learning
- Reinforcement Learning

## Minimum qualifications
- PhD, or Master's degree and 4+ years of CS
- CE
- ML or related field experience
- 3+ years of building models for business application experience
- Experience in patents or publications at top-tier peer-reviewed conferences or journals
- Experience programming in Java
- C++
- Python or related language
- Experience in any of the following areas: algorithms and data structures, parsing, numerical optimization, data mining, parallel and distributed computing, high-performance computing

## Preferred qualifications
- PhD in engineering, technology, computer science, machine learning, robotics, operations research, statistics, mathematics or equivalent quantitative field
- Experience in professional software development

## Responsibilities
- Lead, invent, and design technology that impacts customers across all AWS services
- Work on building Agentic AI systems, fine-tuning large language models, and reinforcement learning
- Generate strategic insights and recommendations from large datasets
- Take projects from idea to experiment to prototype to live production system
