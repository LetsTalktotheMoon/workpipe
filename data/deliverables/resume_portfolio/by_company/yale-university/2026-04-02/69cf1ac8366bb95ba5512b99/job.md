# IT, Software Engineer 1

**Company:** Yale University
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Yale University is dedicated to contributing to a better tomorrow and is seeking an IT, Software Engineer 1 to design, modify, and implement software applications. The role involves collaborating with faculty and departmental teams to understand data needs, managing data pipelines, and developing reports to support decision-making.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Software Testing
- Systems Analysis
- Data Analysis
- Report Development

## Minimum qualifications
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Well-developed interpersonal, written, communication, and organizational skills
- Familiarity with basic systems analysis methods and techniques
- Knowledge of commonly used concepts, practices, and procedures within a field
- Bachelor's Degree and no experience or equivalent education and experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree in a related field or the equivalent combination of education and experience

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate with YSM faculty, school leadership, and departmental teams to engineer a clear understanding of data and business needs
- Build and present reports and analyses that support informed decision‑making and expand self-service data access
- Manage and maintain data pipelines that support timely preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual reporting
- Design and develop custom and ad-hoc reports that are well-structured, intuitive, and enable users to answer questions through self-service tools
- Support data preparation for annual surveys and compensation analyses
- Partner with departmental business offices to collect, validate, and engineer high-quality academic data with a focus on relevance, accuracy, and integrity
- Identify process inefficiencies and engineer improvements that streamline workflows, enhance productivity, and expand opportunities for self-service reporting and analysis
- Develop a strong understanding of the academic organizational structure and its associated data domains, including demographics, compensation, clinical metrics, grants and contracts, and research space
- Develops software solutions to meet customer requirements through team and individual efforts
- Participates in the analysis and composition of requirements, design of architectural and component software features, design and implementation of system, design and implementation of test plan, and documentation of final product
