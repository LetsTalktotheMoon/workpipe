# Analyst, Commerce Analytics

**Company:** Condé Nast
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Condé Nast is a global media company producing high-quality content for over 1 billion consumers. They are seeking an Analyst for Commerce Analytics to provide actionable insights and support the growth of the Commerce team by responding to data requests and conducting analyses.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Google Analytics
- Mode
- Tableau
- Looker

## Minimum qualifications
- 2+ years of experience in data analytics, preferably in a fast-paced environment
- Strong SQL skills for data extraction and analysis
- Hands-on experience with analytics and visualization tools such as Google Analytics
- Mode
- Tableau
- Looker, or similar platforms
- Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel and/or Google Sheets
- Ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver high-quality work under tight deadlines
- Strong communication skills with ability to present complex data insights to non-technical audiences
- Detail-oriented approach with commitment to data accuracy and quality
- Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively across teams and learn quickly
- Passion for digital media and commerce

## Preferred qualifications
- Familiarity with Python or R is a plus
- Understanding of affiliate marketing and/or e-commerce business models is a plus

## Responsibilities
- Prepare and present weekly and/or bi-weekly performance reporting for our portfolio of Commerce brands, highlighting key trends, anomalies, and providing data-driven recommendations to optimize performance
- Conduct ad-hoc analyses to support strategic decision-making around revenue growth, traffic optimization, content performance, and partnership opportunities
- Develop deep expertise in commerce metrics including affiliate revenue, click-through rates, conversion performance, and audience engagement patterns
- Create custom reports and dashboards to address specific business questions and support ongoing initiatives
- Serve as a data resource for commerce teams, helping stakeholders understand data sources, interpret results, identify limitations, and frame analytical questions effectively
- Work closely with internal data engineering and business intelligence teams to troubleshoot data issues, improve data pipelines, and ensure reporting accuracy
- Collaborate with third party data providers (affiliate networks, analytics platforms) to resolve discrepancies, validate data integrity, and optimize data collection
- Document data definitions, methodologies, and reporting processes to ensure consistency and knowledge sharing across the team
- Identify opportunities to streamline reporting workflows and improve data accessibility for stakeholders
- Translate business questions into analytical frameworks and provide recommendations based on findings
