# Data Analyst

**Company:** 1stDibs
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
1stDibs is a leading global marketplace that connects design lovers to extraordinary pieces. As a Data Analyst, you will analyze trends, design dashboards, optimize data collection, and collaborate on A/B tests to influence user experience.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Data Analysis
- Dashboard Design
- Looker
- Tableau

## Minimum qualifications
- 2+ years in data or product analytics
- Proficiency in SQL is required
- An ability to see both the granular details and the 'big picture' of a luxury marketplace
- You thrive when succeeding together and can succinctly exchange ideas with both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- A degree in a quantitative field (Statistics
- Math
- Econ
- Business) or equivalent practical experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Familiarity with Python or dbt is a plus
- we value your ability to listen and learn new tools

## Responsibilities
- Partner cross-functionally to identify trends that guide our global business strategy
- Design scalable, self-serve dashboards (Looker/Tableau) that help your teammates make faster, smarter decisions
- Lead efforts to streamline data collection and translate complex business needs into clear data products
- Collaborate with Product and Marketing to run A/B tests that directly influence our user experience
