# Senior Software Engineer, Cloud Infrastructure

**Company:** Nuro
**Location:** Mountain View, California (HQ)
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Nuro is a self-driving technology company on a mission to make autonomy accessible to all. They are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to join their Infrastructure team, focusing on building scalable solutions and automation to enhance developer experience and infrastructure reliability.

## Core Skills
- Python
- Go
- Public Cloud
- Terraform
- Docker

## Minimum qualifications
- You have start-up DNA: You have demonstrated an ability to thrive in the ambiguity of a dynamic, start-up environment or have the DNA to do so
- You have excellent communication skills
- You have software development skills, preferably in Python or Go
- You are experienced with developing in a public cloud environment
- You are experienced with Terraform
- Docker
- Kubernetes, and Linux
- You are experienced with building, scaling, and maintaining production systems
- You possess a bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Electrical Engineering, or a closely related field or 3+ years of equivalent experience

## Preferred qualifications
- You have cloud/hybrid networking experience
- You have software architecture experience
- You have experience in deploying and managing large distributed applications (e.g. with Kubernetes and ArgoCD)
- You have experience designing and building observability systems

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate and work cross-functionally amongst teams are Nuro to align on customer needs
- Build high-quality and scalable solutions for infrastructure-as-code, application deployment, production system monitoring, health status tracking, and performance improvements
- Build automation to improve developer experience, infrastructure reliability, and security
