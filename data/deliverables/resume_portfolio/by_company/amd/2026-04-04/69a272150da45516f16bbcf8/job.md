# CPU Core Data Analytics Engineer

**Company:** AMD
**Location:** Austin, TX
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
AMD is a leader in computing products, dedicated to innovation and collaboration. They are seeking a highly motivated professional to design, develop, and deploy pre-silicon design metrics for tracking CPU engineering progress, collaborating with program management and engineering leadership.

## Core Skills
- Silicon design
- Data analytics
- Engineering program support
- Pre-silicon design metrics
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Relevant experience in silicon design, data analytics, or engineering program support
- Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving skills with excellent attention to details
- Self-motivation and commitment to meeting deadlines
- Creativity and innovation
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
- Teamwork skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Ability to work with large, complex data sets and database building
- Advanced Proficiency in SQL (Structured Query Language): Expertise in writing and optimizing complex queries, stored procedures, joins, and views to extract and transform data efficiently from relational databases (e.g.
- PostgreSQL
- SQL Server
- MySQL)
- Skilled in using programming languages like Python (with libraries like Pandas and NumPy) or R to identify inconsistencies, handle missing values, reshape, merge, and clean terabytes of raw data for analysis
- Exceptional analytical and data processing skills using tools like PowerBI
- SQL
- MS Project/Excel
- Knowledge of VLSI design concepts & tradeoffs
- Familiar with programming languages such as Perl
- C, tcl, etc
- Basic understanding of computer architecture
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills

## Responsibilities
- Responsible for driving business processes and analytics that enable effective and efficient engineering of CPUs
- Design, maintain & publish reports, and dashboards utilized as a standard for the engineering team
- Partner and collaborate with stakeholders to inform development and enable program evaluation of design quality and schedule
- Drive adoption and operational rigor across the organization through gap analysis and proactive & creative problem solving
