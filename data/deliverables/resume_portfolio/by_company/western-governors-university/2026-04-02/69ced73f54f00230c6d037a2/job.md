# Data Scientist - Learning Program Analytics

**Company:** Western Governors University
**Location:** Salt Lake City, UT
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Western Governors University is dedicated to expanding access to higher education through innovative online programs. As a Data Scientist on the Learning Program Analytics team, you will leverage your technical skills to optimize curriculum and enhance learning outcomes for students nationwide.

## Core Skills
- Predictive Modeling
- Machine Learning
- Python
- R
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in a related field
- 3+ years of experience building predictive models using machine learning methods
- Expert proficiency in Python or R and advanced proficiency in SQL
- Experience working with large, structured, and unstructured datasets
- Experience building and working with Generative and Agentic AI

## Preferred qualifications
- Familiarity with GitHub
- CI/CD pipelines, and MLOps best practices

## Responsibilities
- Clean, preprocess, and validate data using SQL and Python/R to ensure model inputs are accurate and analysis-ready
- Build predictive models using Python or R, applying machine learning, generative and agentic AI techniques, and advanced statistical techniques
- Analyze course-level performance and identify key trends and equity gaps
- Support experiment design and A/B testing frameworks that assess the impact of interventions and curriculum changes
- Support model monitoring and evaluation efforts, tracking performance over time and flagging unexpected changes
- Collaborate across engineering, product, and program development teams to support data-powered curriculum improvements, including assisting with pipeline development and documentation efforts
- Communicate findings clearly with technical and non-technical stakeholders
