# Data Scientist, LM Simulations Engineering, AMZL Simulations & Analytics Engineering

**Company:** Amazon

**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level

## About the job
Amazon's Last Mile Simulation and Analytics Team is seeking a Data Scientist to help design and optimize the world's most complex delivery network. In this role, you'll own the Data Science/ML roadmap—developing statistical and machine learning models, designing global experiments, and discovering new ways to enhance the customer experience.
