# Data Analyst

**Company:** Lucid Software
**Location:** Salt Lake City, UT
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Lucid Software is the leader in visual collaboration, helping teams see and build the future from idea to reality. As an Analyst on Lucid’s Strategy & Analytics team, you will engage in strategic problem solving, analyze data, present results, and drive change across various business functions.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Statistics
- Hypothesis testing
- Strategic thinking

## Minimum qualifications
- Completed Bachelor's and/or Master's degree, ideally in a technical or quantitative field
- 1.5+ years professional experience
- Proficient with SQL
- Basic understanding of statistics and hypothesis testing
- Great communication skills, both written and verbal
- Able to think strategically and tackle open-ended problems
- Detail-oriented, organized, and a good team player

## Preferred qualifications
- Passion for problem-solving - If you've ever been so absorbed in a problem that your mind couldn't rest until you figured it out, you'll be in good company
- Willing to help and be helped - Our impact comes only through helping others make better decisions using data. We recognize that we're stronger together - there's no shame in asking for help, we're not afraid to say 'I don't know', and we actively seek feedback
- Desire to learn - You'll often be answering questions that have never been answered before, which requires a high level of intellectual curiosity and an eagerness to dive into new problems, domains, tools, and techniques

## Responsibilities
- Work with senior leaders and other stakeholders within various departments at Lucid
- Identify and understand problems and opportunities
- Come up with data-driven recommendations
- Implement new initiatives that help grow the business
- Other duties as assigned
