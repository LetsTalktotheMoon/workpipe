**Company:** Fullsteam
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

Fullsteam is a leading provider of vertical software and embedded payments technology dedicated to helping businesses flourish by providing their customers with seamless experiences. We are seeking a confident Sr. Software Engineer to join our engineering team as a core member, where you will take ownership of major features, contribute to product strategy, and help shape the engineering culture.

## Core Skills
- NestJS
- React
- Web Services
- API
- Microservices

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of concentrated, daily experience with NestJS is required
- 3+ years of concentrated, daily experience with React is required
- Web Services/API experience (.Net Core)
- Experience with Microservices (.Net Core)
- Knowledge of Azure CI/CD pipelines
- Strong database skills (MS SQL)
- Experience with JQuery and legacy Webforms (.Net 4.8)
- Familiarity with AI tooling for DevEx
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Software Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent experience)
- 3–5+ years of experience in full stack development (or equivalent experience demonstrating strong skills in the required technologies)

## Responsibilities
- Be an essential contributor to the engineering team and team culture
- Take full ownership of major features from inception to production
- Lead initiatives independently while leveraging support from the team
- Analyze, troubleshoot, and fix bugs efficiently
- Collaborate across departments to ensure the product meets user needs
