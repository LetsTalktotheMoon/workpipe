# Senior Product Engineer (Ruby) | San Francisco

**Company:** Cleo
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Cleo is a fast-growing fintech company on a mission to change humanity's relationship with money by providing a hyper-intelligent financial advisor. They are seeking a Senior Product Engineer with expertise in Ruby to join a cross-functional team, developing features that enhance users' financial health and mentoring fellow engineers.

## Core Skills
- Ruby on Rails
- React Native
- TypeScript
- Python
- PostgreSQL

## Minimum qualifications
- At least 4 years industry experience of using Ruby on Rails
- Passionate about making a positive difference in society by improving the financial health of users
- Align with company values and engineering principles
- Experience in building data models
- APIs, and business logic services
- Ability to deliver work using agile methodologies and tools like tests, observability
- AB-tests, and feature flags
- Experience in analyzing data to identify problems and generate new ideas
- Ability to mentor colleagues and support their development
- Experience in collaborating with cross-functional teams including engineers, designers, and analysts

## Responsibilities
- Joining a cross-functional product squad and collaborating with a mix of engineers, designers, analysts and other disciplines to develop features that enhance our users' financial health
- Collaborating with other senior peers in your squad and pillar to provide technical insight into upcoming work, and leading the delivery by helping pull everyone together to get it shipped
- Mentoring colleagues to help them grow as engineers and actively supporting their development
- Working on our Ruby on Rails monolith, building data models
- APIs, and business logic services
- Delivering your work using agile methodologies and tools like tests, observability
- AB-tests, and feature flags
- Analyzing data to identify problems and generate new ideas, using various sources such as our database, application logs, and user research data
- Supporting colleagues through in-hours support and optionally joining the compensated out-of-hours on-call rotation
- Contributing to cross-cutting concerns that improve our engineering efforts
- Taking part in shaping the work of your squad beyond technical aspects, participating in product ideation, user research, design reviews, retrospectives, and more
