# Developer - AI & Data Integrations

**Company:** Duke University Health System
**Location:** Durham, NC
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Duke University Health System is dedicated to compassionate care and innovative technology solutions. The Developer role involves contributing to the development of automation and innovative applications while supporting research and prototyping for emerging technologies like AI and ML.

## Core Skills
- Programming
- Debugging
- Software Development
- Full-stack Development
- API Development

## Minimum qualifications
- Level I: Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering or related field OR 2 years relevant work experience
- Level II: Bachelor's degree OR 3-5 years relevant work experience
- Level III: Bachelor's or Master's degree OR 5-7 years relevant work experience
- Level I: Experience in software development through internships, coursework, or entry-level roles
- Level II: 3-5 years of experience in software development, with exposure to emerging technologies
- Level III: 5-7 years development or architecture experience
- 2-3 years designing and delivering automation, innovation and AI-driven efforts
- Level I: Foundational programming and debugging skills
- Level I: Basic collaboration skills within a team
- Level I: Eagerness to learn innovation processes
- Level I: Good communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- API Development
- AI expertise
- Health sciences experience (clinical or research)

## Responsibilities
- Contribute to development tasks under guidance from senior developers
- Assist in testing, documenting, and deploying new automation and innovation solutions
- Learn and apply new frameworks, languages, and tools
- Support research and prototyping for emerging technologies (AI
- ML
- Automation)
- Contribute to the maintenance and monitoring of production systems
- Independently design and implement components of automation platforms and innovative applications
- Collaborate closely with architects and engineers on prototypes and pilots
- Conduct deeper research into emerging technology use cases and best practices
- Mentor Level I staff
- Lead the design and delivery of complex, cross-domain pilot projects using disruptive technologies
