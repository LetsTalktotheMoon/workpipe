# Data Scientist

**Company:** Adobe
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Adobe is a company that empowers everyone to create through innovative platforms and tools. They are seeking a Data Scientist to drive the Document Cloud business by utilizing advanced analytical and machine learning techniques to deliver actionable recommendations.

## Core Skills
- Quantitative analysis
- Statistical modeling
- SQL
- Data visualization
- Tableau

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in quantitative fields, e.g.
- Computer Science
- Math/Statistics
- Economics
- Physics, or equivalent practical experience
- 2-3 years hands-on experience doing quantitative analysis ideally in tech or management consulting
- Demonstrable experience using quantitative analysis/statistical modeling on data sets to tackle intricate business problems
- Phenomenal at telling stories with data and communicating it to partners at all levels to influence critical decision making
- Strong proficiency in querying and manipulating volumes of data sets for analytical purposes using SQL-like languages
- Statistical Modeling in Microsoft Excel or Power Pivot
- Experience building and training statistical/data science models
- Experience with statistics in SaaS or Subscription Business model environment
- Experience in Data Visualization tools, preferably
- Tableau and Power BI
- A solid sense of self with an innate sense of curiosity

## Preferred qualifications
- MBA or a master's degree is preferred

## Responsibilities
- Apply your expertise in quantitative analysis and data modeling to uncover deep insights into customer behavior and trends
- Act as a thought partner to business leads and influence decisions through compelling data-driven storytelling
- Identify business opportunities, build use cases, and recommend data science initiatives to inform our product roadmap
- Tackle critical business challenges with data science models to drive scalable and programmatic business solutions
- Recommend and implement testing ideas based on detailed analysis
