# Data Engineer (Python/Spark)

**Company:** Take-Two Interactive
**Location:** Austin, TX
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Take-Two Interactive is a leading developer, publisher, and marketer of interactive entertainment. They are seeking a dynamic Data Engineer to design, build, and maintain reliable and scalable data solutions while collaborating with business teams to derive insights from complex data.

## Core Skills
- Python
- SQL
- AWS
- Git
- Docker

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of hands-on experience in Python
- 3+ years of hands-on experience in using advanced SQL queries and writing/optimizing highly efficient SQL queries
- 2+ years of working in AWS environment
- Experience with Git
- Experience with Docker
- Comfortable working with business customers to collect requirements and gain a deep understanding of varied business domains
- Knowledge of software coding practices across the development lifecycle, including agile methodologies, coding standards, code reviews, source management, build processes, testing, and operations
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent in an engineering or technical field such as Computer Science
- Information Systems
- Statistics
- Engineering, or similar

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience integrating with 3rd party APIs is highly desirable

## Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain scalable data pipelines and build out new API integrations to support continuing increases in data volume and complexity
- Work with business teams and analysts to understand complex business rules and implement these for deriving insights
- Implement processes and systems to monitor data quality, ensuring production data is always accurate and available for key stakeholders and business processes that depend on it
- Perform data analysis required to troubleshoot data related issues and assist in the resolution of data issues
- Provide thought leadership and collaborate with other team members to continue to scale our architecture to evolve for the needs of tomorrow
- Develop and support continuous integrations build and deployment processes using Jenkins
- Docker
- Git, etc
- Define and implement monitoring and alerting policies for data solutions
