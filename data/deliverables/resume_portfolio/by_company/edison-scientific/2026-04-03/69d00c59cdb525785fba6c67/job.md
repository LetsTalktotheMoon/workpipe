# AI Engineer - Forward Deployed

**Company:** Edison Scientific
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Edison Scientific is focused on building and commercializing AI agents for scientific discovery. As a Forward Deployed Engineer, you will work closely with leading science R&D organizations to maximize the value of Edison's AI agents by understanding their workflows and pain points, and delivering solutions rapidly.

## Core Skills
- Software Engineering
- Production-grade Software Development
- Python
- TypeScript
- JavaScript

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of professional software engineering experience, with a track record of shipping production-grade software in fast-paced or ambiguous environments
- Comfortable operating independently, making decisions with incomplete information, and driving outcomes without close oversight
- Strong problem-solving skills and comfort navigating complex systems, debugging issues in real time, and owning features end-to-end
- Proficiency in Python and/or TypeScript/JavaScript
- Strong communication and relationship-building skills – able to earn trust with scientists, executives, and engineering teams
- Willingness to learn new scientific domains quickly and adapt to rapidly evolving priorities

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in life sciences, pharma, biotech, or scientific R&D – either as a researcher, consultant, or engineer working in these environments
- Experience working with AI/ML systems
- LLM-powered applications, or agent architectures
- Background as a technical founder, forward-deployed engineer, or management consultant
- A science degree or research experience alongside your engineering skills

## Responsibilities
- Be embedded at a client site, understand their research workflows deeply, and take responsibility for maximizing the value Edison delivers to that organization
- Map the customer's scientific and operational landscape to identify where AI agents can have the most impact – not just where they ask for help, but where the real leverage is
- Design, implement, and iterate on production-ready integrations in fast cycles, often owning features end-to-end from architecture to rollout
- Troubleshoot issues in real time, ensure reliability and adoption, and be the go-to technical contact for AI within the client organization
- Capture pain points, unmet needs, and product gaps and work with our internal Applied AI and engineering teams to shape future capabilities
