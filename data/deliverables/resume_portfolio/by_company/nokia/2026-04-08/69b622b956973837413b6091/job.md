# Quality Assurance Software Development Engineer

**Company:** Nokia
**Location:** Indiana, United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Nokia’s IP Network Division is focused on revolutionizing network capacity through innovation and technical expertise. They are seeking a QA Engineer to define and automate tests, drive test strategies, and ensure the quality of cutting-edge IP/DataCenter/Mobile features.

## Core Skills
- Python
- TCL
- Go
- Scripting
- Git

## Minimum qualifications
- More than 3 years of relevant experience
- Genuine interest in computer networking, coding and SW testing
- Strives to deliver top-quality with respect to next-gen IP-routing/DataCenter/Mobile technology
- Self-driven and eager to learn
- Good in programming, networking, debugging
- Experience with scripting, python, tcl, git, json, yang

## Responsibilities
- You follow up on the evolutions of the internet and data center world
- You collaborate with the R&D teams to deliver your feature with exceptional quality to the field and you maintain it during its lifetime
- You fully tests all aspects of the features assigned to you
- This goes from participating in the requirements discussions, creating the test plan, conducting and automating all test cases until final delivery of the feature and product
- Define test strategy
- Develop code (in python
- TCL
- Go other languages) to qualify our Networking
- Security and Data Center products
- Final Go/No Go decision before a software release
- Become an expert (go-to-person) for a number of specific areas (involving features) and gets a good overview of all functionality of our IP range of products
- Close interaction with Product management and SW developers teams
- Continuous integration environment
