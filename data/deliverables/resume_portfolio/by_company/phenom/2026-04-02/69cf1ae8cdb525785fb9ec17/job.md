# Software Developer (Full Stack)

**Company:** Phenom
**Location:** Ambler, PA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Phenom is an AI-Powered talent experience platform that is redefining the HR tech space. They are looking for a Software Developer (Full-Stack) to design and develop scalable and secure backend services, ensuring operational excellence and innovative solutions across their platforms.

## Core Skills
- Full-stack development
- Java
- J2EE
- REST APIs
- JSON

## Minimum qualifications
- 2+ years of software development experience, focusing on full-stack development
- Hands on experience with Java/J2EE and building backend services using REST and JSON
- Strong frontend experience with JavaScript and React.js, building responsive and user-friendly web applications
- Experience designing and implementing scalable, distributed systems in production environments
- Strong understanding of cloud platforms (AWS
- Azure) and hands-on experience with cloud-based deployments

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop end-to-end full-stack systems that are scalable, secure, and maintainable, ensuring seamless integration between frontend and backend components
- Lead full-stack product development efforts, translating business objectives into technical solutions with a focus on SAAS architecture, user experience, and backend system performance
- Develop and maintain frontend user interfaces using JavaScript and React.js, while ensuring backend services in Java/J2EE
- REST APIs, and MongoDB are efficient and scalable
- Ensure systems are compatible with DevOps processes, implementing best practices for CI/CD pipelines, logging, monitoring, and overall operational needs
- Work independently and collaboratively on full-stack projects, delivering high-quality, scalable solutions in a fast-paced environment
- Maintain operational excellence by reducing on-call volume, driving Root Cause Analysis (RCA) for full-stack systems, and fostering system-wide improvements
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including product managers and designers, to ensure successful product delivery and alignment with business requirements
