# Data Analyst Lead

**Company:** Progressive Insurance
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Progressive Insurance is dedicated to helping employees advance in their careers. They are seeking a lead data analyst for the Claims Triage and Automation program, responsible for transforming complex data into actionable insights and driving decisions through structured data models and analytics.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- Data modeling
- Data transformations, dbt

## Minimum qualifications
- A minimum of eight years of analytical work experience
- Bachelor's degree or higher and a minimum of seven years of analytical work experience
- Bachelor's degree or higher in a quantitative field of study and a minimum of five years analytical work experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Strong SQL expertise, including complex joins, window functions, and performance optimization, and prior experience with Python
- Solid understanding of data modeling concepts such as fact and dimension tables, data grain, and scalable design
- Experience building data transformations in a warehouse environment (e.g., dbt, stored procedures
- ETL/ELT tools, etc.), working with Snowflake or a similar cloud data platform, and a willingness to learn dbt if not already familiar
- Demonstrated ability to work with large, unstructured, or third-party datasets and bring structure to ambiguity
- Experience with BI tools (e.g.
- Power BI
- Tableau) for data visualization and reporting
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills

## Responsibilities
- Turn complex, high-volume data into business-ready datasets to drive decisions
- Design data models and transformation pipelines across hundreds of source tables
- Bring structure and clarity where no data dictionary exists
- Define key entities and domains
- Build a curated analytics layer as a single source of truth
- Document datasets, metrics, and business logic to standardize reporting
- Perform investigation analysis to uncover insights, trends, and opportunities
- Guide collaborators on how to act on them
