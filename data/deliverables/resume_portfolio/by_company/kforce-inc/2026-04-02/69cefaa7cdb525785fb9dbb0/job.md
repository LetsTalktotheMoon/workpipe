# Mid and Senior Data Scientists

**Company:** Kforce Inc
**Location:** Salt Lake City, UT
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Kforce Inc is seeking experienced Data Scientists to solve complex, data-driven problems through statistical analysis, predictive modeling, and machine learning. The role involves transforming large datasets into actionable insights that influence product development and business decision-making.

## Core Skills
- Statistical techniques
- Machine learning techniques
- Predictive modeling
- Python
- R

## Minimum qualifications
- Master's degree or higher in Computer Science
- Statistics
- Mathematics, or a related quantitative field is preferred
- Strong foundation in statistical and machine learning techniques such as classification, regression, clustering, dimensionality reduction, and multivariate analysis
- Experience evaluating model performance using methods such as cross-validation, hold-out testing, and similar approaches
- Understanding of algorithmic complexity and performance at scale
- Hands-on experience with predictive modeling using Python and/or R
- Working knowledge of relational databases and SQL
- Ability to code in at least one general-purpose programming language (e.g.
- Python
- Java
- C#, or similar)
- Strong analytical, problem-solving, and written communication skills
- Comfort operating in evolving or ambiguous environments where adaptability is critical
- Excellent interpersonal skills with the ability to communicate effectively across all levels of an organization

## Responsibilities
- Partner with application engineering, product management, and operational teams to design, experiment with, and implement machine learning and analytical solutions
- Analyze large and complex datasets to surface patterns, generate hypotheses, and validate findings through experimentation
- Apply statistical and machine learning techniques to real-world business problems, producing measurable insights to guide product and feature planning
- Translate ambiguous problem statements into structured analytical and mathematical models
- Develop, test, and scale algorithms while ensuring performance, reliability, and interpretability
- Surface analytically driven features and insights within core products and reporting workflows
- Collaborate with product, marketing, and commercial stakeholders as needed to incorporate market and customer feedback
- Communicate findings, trade-offs, and root causes clearly to both technical and non-technical audiences
