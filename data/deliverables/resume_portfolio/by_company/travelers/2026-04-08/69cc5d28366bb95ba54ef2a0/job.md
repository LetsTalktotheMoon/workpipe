# Data Scientist, Applied Research

**Company:** Travelers
**Location:** Hartford, CT
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Travelers is one of the best property casualty insurers in the industry, dedicated to innovation and collaboration. The Data Scientist role involves using advanced statistical techniques, machine learning algorithms, and AI to solve key business challenges and deliver predictive models, while working closely with business partners and stakeholders.

## Core Skills
- Statistical analysis
- Model development
- Statistical software programming
- Advanced statistics
- Regression

## Minimum qualifications
- Master's in STEM related field or equivalent
- 3 years of related experience
- Strong working knowledge of modeling/research/analytics or actuarial required
- Relevant statistical analysis work experience required
- Strong Knowledge of statistical techniques with the ability to read/revise/review statistical software programs
- Ability to develop models and interpret model results
- Understanding of advanced statistics underlying data models
- Ability to apply emerging statistical procedures to work
- Strong knowledge in: Regression
- Classification
- Machine Vision
- Natural Language Processing
- Deep Learning and Statistical modeling
- Master's degree in Statistics
- Mathematics
- Decision Sciences
- Actuarial Science or related analytical STEM field plus two years of experience or any suitable and equivalent combination of education and work experience

## Responsibilities
- Assist in design and development of analytical solutions
- Share knowledge with the community through discussions, presentations, or peer reviews
- Begin to set and manage expectations with business partners for small projects, and with guidance, identify potential conflicts and build consensus
- Communicate analysis, insights, and results to manager, peers and business partners and with guidance, tailor communication to the audience
- Become a mentor or resource for less experienced analytic talent, onboard new employees and interns, and provide support for intern recruiting and talent assessment efforts
- Support various training and skill development initiatives
- Perform other duties as assigned
