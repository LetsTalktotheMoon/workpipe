# Enterprise Data Analyst

**Company:** Shelter Insurance Companies
**Location:** Greater Columbia, Missouri Area
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Shelter Insurance Companies is a company built to serve you, and they are seeking an Enterprise Data Analyst. The role involves leveraging data analysis and visualization to drive informed business decisions and extracting actionable insights from complex data sets.

## Core Skills
- Data analysis
- Data visualization
- Tableau Desktop
- SQL
- Snowflake

## Minimum qualifications
- Proven experience (2+ years) as a Data Analyst, with a focus on data analysis, visualization, and business intelligence using Tableau and Snowflake typically required
- Proficiency in Tableau Desktop, including data connection, dashboard design, calculated fields, parameters, and data blending
- Strong SQL skills and experience working with Snowflake for data extraction, transformation, and manipulation
- Solid understanding of data analysis techniques, statistical concepts, and data visualization best practices
- Experience in data modeling, data validation, and data governance principles
- Strong problem-solving and critical-thinking skills, with the ability to translate business questions into analytical approaches
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively convey complex analytical concepts to stakeholders at various levels
- Ability to manage multiple tasks and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment, with strong attention to detail
- Ability to perform essential functions of the position, with or without a reasonable accommodation

## Preferred qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in data science, statistics, mathematics, computer science, or a related field is preferred
- Familiarity with insurance industry practices and metrics, such as underwriting, claims, and risk management, is preferred
- Experience with Github, dbtLabs is desired
- Experience with Data Catalog & lineage tool is desired

## Responsibilities
- Responsible for leveraging data analysis, visualization, and insights to drive informed business decisions
- Play a key role in extracting actionable insights from complex data sets and communicating them effectively to stakeholders
