# Software Engineer III, Cloud Gaia

**Company:** Google
**Location:** Sunnyvale, CA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Google is a leading technology company that develops next-generation technologies impacting billions of users. They are seeking a Software Engineer III to work on critical projects related to Cloud Gaia, focusing on scalable identity infrastructure and enhancing Google Cloud services.

## Core Skills
- Large-scale infrastructure development
- Distributed systems
- Networks
- Compute technologies
- Storage architecture

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
- 2 years of experience with developing large-scale infrastructure, distributed systems or networks, or experience with compute technologies, storage or hardware architecture
- 2 years of experience with software development or 1 year of experience with an advanced degree in an industry setting
- Experience with Applied AI
- ML Infrastructure
- ML or AI for products, or related fields

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science or related technical fields
- 2 years of experience with data structures and algorithms
- Experience developing accessible technologies

## Responsibilities
- Write product or system development code
- Participate in, or lead design reviews with peers and stakeholders to decide amongst available technologies
- Identify and prove the value of new GenAI features and solutions to advance the value of cloud assist
- Lead initiatives on agentic framework, tooling, quality techniques to help scale multi-modal intent Design coverage across first-party and third-party services and workloads
- Drive exploratory analysis of state of the modeling techniques, user engagement behaviors and product performance to discover important trends, product opportunities, and Google Cloud Assist (GCA) Design quality gaps
