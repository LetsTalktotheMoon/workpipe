# Software Engineer, Shopping Quality

**Company:** Google
**Location:** Pittsburgh, PA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Google is a leading technology company that develops next-generation technologies to enhance user interaction and information retrieval. In this role, the software engineer will manage project priorities and deliverables while designing and enhancing software solutions critical to Google’s commerce needs.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- C++
- Data Quality Evaluation
- Large Language Models
- Data Structures

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
- 2 years of experience with software development in one or more programming languages, or 1 year of experience with an advanced degree
- 2 years of experience using C++
- Experience evaluating and improving data quality for large language models (LLM)

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science or related technical fields
- 2 years of experience with data structures and algorithms
- Experience developing accessible technologies
- Experience with building machine learning models, data pipelines, and feature engineering

## Responsibilities
- Write product or system development code
- Participate in, or lead design reviews with peers and stakeholders to decide amongst available technologies
- Review code developed by other developers and provide feedback to ensure best practices (e.g., style guidelines, checking code in, accuracy, testability, and efficiency)
- Contribute to existing documentation or educational content and adapt content based on product/program updates and user feedback
- Triage product or system issues and debug/track/resolve by analyzing the sources of issues and the impact on hardware, network, or service operations and quality
