# Software Engineer II

**Company:** Ironclad
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Ironclad is the leading AI contracting platform that transforms agreements into assets. As a Software Engineer at Ironclad, you'll work cross-functionally to identify business problems and architect, build, and verify solutions, contributing to a platform that enhances the efficiency of legal professionals.

## Core Skills
- JavaScript
- TypeScript
- Software Engineering
- System Architecture
- Product Shipping

## Minimum qualifications
- At least 2+ years of software experience architecting, building, launching and maintaining complex systems (a formal degree is not required, we are prioritizing industry experience)
- Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript
- Passion for software engineering to build and ship great product
- Hands-on experience shipping valuable products to production with real user feedback
- A high bar for quality
- Strong cross-team communication and collaboration skills
- Experience using AI tools (e.g.
- Claude Code
- Cursor), including writing effective prompts and evaluating outputs

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate cross functionally with product managers, designers, and other engineers to spec, design, and build features and systems
- Partner with quality engineers to define, build and maintain testing strategy
- Contribute to technical discussions, code reviews, observability, ensuring scalability, maintainability, and performance
- Grow your teams by interviewing and recruiting talented engineers to Ironclad
- Work on a modern stack built upon: TypeScript
- ReactJS
- Node.js
- Java, and Docker containers in Kubernetes on Google Cloud Platform (experience with these specific technologies not required)
