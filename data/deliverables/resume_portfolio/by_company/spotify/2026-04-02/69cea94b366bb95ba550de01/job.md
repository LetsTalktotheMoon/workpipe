# Backend Engineer - Music Mission

**Company:** Spotify
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Spotify is a leading audio streaming subscription service that transformed music listening. They are seeking a Backend Engineer to build and operate production backend systems that enhance the listening experience of music videos and support artists in connecting with their fans.

## Core Skills
- Backend development
- Java
- Scala
- Python
- API design

## Minimum qualifications
- Have strong fundamentals in computer science and previous experience building backend services at scale
- You are proficient in at least one backend programming language such as Java
- Scala
- Python, or similar
- You are highly adaptable and enjoy picking up new technologies, moving confidently across different parts of systems and tech stacks
- You are comfortable using AI-powered developer tools such as Claude as part of your development workflow to learn new technologies, accelerate development, and solve complex problems
- You thrive in fast-moving environments, handle change with ease, and communicate clearly with teammates and external partners

## Responsibilities
- Build and operate production backend systems that enrich and improve the listening experience of music videos
- Design, develop, and evolve scalable backend services and APIs that support high-traffic surfaces
- Architect, develop, and operate Java services and Scio pipelines used by millions of listeners every day
- Partner closely with product managers, and engineers to solve complex problems and deliver seamless user experiences
- Contribute within an autonomous, cross-functional squad, contributing to system design, code quality, and operational excellence
