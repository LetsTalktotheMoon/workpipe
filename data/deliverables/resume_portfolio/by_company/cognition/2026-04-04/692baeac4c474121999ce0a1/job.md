# AI Enablement Engineer

**Company:** Cognition
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Cognition is an applied AI lab building end-to-end software agents, including Devin and Windsurf, which aim to enhance collaborative AI capabilities. The AI Enablement Engineer will help teams adopt these tools and integrate AI workflows into their development processes while creating scalable training models for broader global impact.

## Core Skills
- Python
- JavaScript
- TypeScript
- Software engineering
- Technical consulting

## Minimum qualifications
- Degree in a STEM field or equivalent hands-on experience
- 3+ years as a software engineer or technical consultant with strong coding proficiency (Python
- JavaScript/TypeScript, or similar)
- Proven ability to communicate complex technical topics to diverse audiences
- Strong customer-service orientation and ability to thrive in interactive, people-facing environments
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
- Demonstrated ability to learn and adapt exceptionally fast

## Preferred qualifications
- Have experience leading technical workshops, internal enablement programs, or developer onboarding initiatives
- Have deployed or integrated LLM or agent-based systems in production settings
- Are energized by teaching, coaching, and helping others reach 'aha' moments
- Have previously founded or joined early-stage startups where autonomy and execution speed were critical

## Responsibilities
- Lead interactive programs for enterprise engineering teams (live workshops, pair programming sessions)
- Guide customers through installing, configuring, and optimizing Devin and its associated tools (DeepWiki
- MCP integrations, etc.)
- Pair-program with engineers at varying experience levels, helping them build, debug, and automate real projects
- Identify high-ROI workflows for Devin across engineering
- QA, support, data, and product teams
- Create enablement materials, best practices, and shared playbooks based on customer learnings
- Work with leadership to scale AI Enablement into a repeatable and productized offering—through digital learning content, partner certifications, and scalable training models
- Collaborate with Cognition Engagement Managers and Engineering teams to continuously refine onboarding and provide feedback on new features
