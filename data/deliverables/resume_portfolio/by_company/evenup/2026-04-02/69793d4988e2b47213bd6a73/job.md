# Data Migration Specialist

**Company:** EvenUp
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
EvenUp is on a mission to close the justice gap using technology and AI, empowering personal injury lawyers and victims to achieve better outcomes. The Data Migration Specialist is responsible for planning, executing, validating, and optimizing customer data migrations as part of enterprise implementations, ensuring accurate and secure migration of legal data into the EvenUp platform.

## Core Skills
- Data migration
- Data engineering
- ETL tools
- SQL
- APIs

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Information Systems
- Data Engineering, or a related field
- 3 to 8 years of experience in data migration, data engineering, or technical implementation roles within enterprise SaaS environments
- Proven experience migrating large, complex datasets from legacy systems into modern cloud platforms
- Hands-on experience with ETL processes
- APIs
- SQL, and data transformation techniques
- Strong proficiency in SQL and relational data modeling
- Experience with ETL tools, scripting languages (e.g.
- Python), and API-based data transfers
- Understanding of data governance, security, and compliance best practices
- Experience supporting UAT and go-live activities in complex implementations
- Highly detail-oriented with a strong commitment to data accuracy and quality
- Strong problem-solver who can navigate ambiguous data scenarios

## Preferred qualifications
- Relevant certifications (e.g., data engineering, database, cloud, or ETL tools)
- Experience working in regulated industries (legal, financial services, healthcare) strongly preferred
- Bonus: Experience migrating data for legal case management systems or legal operations platforms

## Responsibilities
- Partner with Solution Architects during post-sale discovery to assess customer source systems (e.g.
- CRM
- Accounting
- Processes, spreadsheets) and data readiness
- Analyze legacy data structures, field mappings, data quality, and dependencies to identify risks, gaps, and remediation strategies
- Define migration scope, sequencing, assumptions, and success criteria in collaboration with implementation leadership
- Advise customers on data cleanup, normalization, and preparation activities prior to migration
- Design detailed source-to-target data mappings aligned with EvenUp’s data models, workflows, and reporting requirements
- Collaborate with the migration team to ensure migrations support future-state processes, automations, and analytics
- Define transformation rules, validation logic, and exception handling for complex legal data sets
- Contribute to migration “best practices” and reusable templates for common legal workflows
- Build, execute, and manage data migrations using ETL tools, scripts
- APIs, or database utilities
- Perform trial migrations, incremental loads, and final cutover migrations in accordance with project timelines
