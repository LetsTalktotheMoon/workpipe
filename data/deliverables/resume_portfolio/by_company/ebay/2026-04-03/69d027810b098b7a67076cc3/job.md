# Applied Researcher I

**Company:** eBay
**Location:** Austin, TX
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
eBay is a global ecommerce leader committed to changing the way the world shops and sells. They are looking for an Applied Researcher I to support the risk department by utilizing big data technologies and developing algorithmic solutions to minimize fraud, ultimately impacting millions of buyers and sellers worldwide.

## Core Skills
- Big Data tools
- Hadoop
- Hive
- Spark
- Machine learning

## Minimum qualifications
- Master's degree in a quantitative field such as computer science, math, or statistics
- Experience working with Big Data tools such as Hadoop
- Hive, and Spark
- Expertise in machine learning packages like Python and R
- Strong programming skills in languages such as Python
- Java, or Scala
- Background in various modeling techniques, including GBM, logistic regression, clustering, deep learning, etc
- Excellent analytical skills with a proven problem-solving ability
- Strong presentation and communication skills

## Responsibilities
- Support the risk department by bringing to bear big data technologies to aggregate and structure data
- Conduct statistical analysis and develop algorithmic solutions to minimize fraud and oversee our buyers and sellers
- Research and develop new methodologies and techniques to improve risk management
- Act as the technical owner of projects, customizing analytic tools, techniques, and processes
- Develop tracking and reporting systems to determine the efficiency of models and risk initiatives
- Build and develop systems to transform petabytes of data into statistically significant features for modeling
- Collaborate with Business Unit Partners and Policy Makers to build scalable and repeatable solutions
- Partner with the Data Engineering team to deploy models to the Production environment
