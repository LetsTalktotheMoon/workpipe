# Software QA Engineer II (Full Time) – United States

**Company:** Cisco
**Location:** RTP, North Carolina, US
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Cisco is a leading technology company revolutionizing how data and infrastructure connect. They are seeking a Software QA Engineer II to ensure the highest software quality through systematic testing and automation, while collaborating closely with product managers and development teams.

## Core Skills
- Software Quality Assurance
- Automated Testing
- Software Design
- CI/CD
- Quality Gates

## Minimum qualifications
- Completion within the past 3 years, or current enrollment with expected completion within 12 months, of a certification or relevant degree program (e.g.
- Associate's
- Apprenticeship
- Boot Camp, or Certification in a specialized program + 1 year of relevant experience
- High School Diploma + 2 years of relevant experience) or Bachelor's + 0 years of relevant experience
- Proficient knowledge and hands-on experience with current technologies in the primary technical domain
- Demonstrated expertise in developing and executing software design plans, automated scripts, and quality assurance programs
- Experience in CI/CD environments, including defining and enforcing quality gates
- Ability to work independently, provide mentorship, and collaborate effectively within and across teams

## Preferred qualifications
- Advanced knowledge of secure coding principles and threat modeling for software features
- Proven experience designing and deploying solutions to test reliability, availability, latency, and performance
- Skilled in implementing automation frameworks and conducting complex testing to uncover product defects
- Strong background in root cause analysis and recommending corrective actions for software issues
- Experience leading projects, mentoring team members, and contributing to cross-functional initiatives

## Responsibilities
- Play a vital role in the secure design, build, and testing of software products through systematic testing and automation
- Influence the quality of products by contributing to CI/CD processes and defining quality gates
- Provide risk assessment and root cause analysis to improve product functionality and reliability
- Mentor peers and interns, helping to grow the next generation of technical talent
- Collaborate across teams to implement and document best practices that advance end-to-end product quality
