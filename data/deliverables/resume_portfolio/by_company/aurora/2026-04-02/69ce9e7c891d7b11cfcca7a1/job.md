# Software Engineer - Platform

**Company:** Aurora
**Location:** Boston, MA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Aurora is a company that helps engineers discover opportunities at ambitious startups, and they are seeking a Platform Engineer to design and build the foundational technology for their product. The role involves advancing platform capabilities and collaborating with the Research team to enhance the impact of Aaru's simulation technology.

## Core Skills
- Platform engineering
- Behavioral simulation
- Artificial intelligence
- TypeScript
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- Architectural intuition of a founder
- Technical depth to navigate large-scale behavioral simulation
- High-ownership builder
- Ability to simplify complex problems
- Experience working on products that serve users with a wide range of technical competency
- Ability to ask questions while building
- Experience with the push and pull between building 'right' and 'fast'
- Proficiency in TypeScript
- Python, and Rust (not required to be proficient in all three)

## Preferred qualifications
- Have experience building developer products or platforms
- Have launched or built a great product themselves

## Responsibilities
- Design, build, and advance the platform to force multiply our product capabilities
- Identify patterns across user needs and product to 10x our simulation software’s impact
- Write framework-level code that absorbs complexity for users and agents
