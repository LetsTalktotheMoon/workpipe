# Software Development Engineer 30

**Company:** Adobe
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Adobe is a leading company that empowers everyone to create through innovative platforms and tools. The Software Development Engineer will design, develop, test, deploy, and monitor micro-services that deliver critical product features, ensuring scalability and cohesive behavior across services.

## Core Skills
- Java
- Scala
- SQL
- MySQL
- Snowflake

## Minimum qualifications
- BS or MS in Computer Science or equivalent engineering degree with 3+ years of experience working on enterprise software in distributed environment
- 1+ years working with data processing systems
- Strong proficiency in Java or Scala
- Solid expertise in SQL (MySQL
- Snowflake) and experience with at least one NoSQL store (preference: Mongo
- Redis)
- Experience working with streaming technologies like Kafka
- Experience with CI/CD related technologies, including Docker
- K8s
- Terraform
- Expertise designing and building micro services and RESTful APIs
- Working knowledge of public cloud technologies (Azure
- AWS)
- Working knowledge of data privacy regulations (e.g.
- GDPR
- CCPA) and secure data handling
- Ability to communicate complex technical concepts clearly to technical and non-technical audiences

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Spark
- Databricks
- Airflow
- Practical experience of tuning Spark jobs
- Experience building agentic workflows (LangChain
- LangGraph
- RAG, vector DBs
- MCP)

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, test, deploy, and monitor micro‑services that deliver critical product features end‑to‑end
- Architect solutions that span multiple services and teams, ensuring cohesive behavior and scalability
- Drive every phase of software delivery: design, implementation, reviews, automated testing (unit/integration/e2e), refactoring, debugging, and documentation
- Partner with multi-functional teams to guarantee on‑time delivery of high‑quality, production‑ready product
- Experiment with emergent technologies in a fast‑paced environment and promote takeaways across the team
- Fast prototyping to explore solutions for new and emerging problems
- Design and implement comprehensive test suites covering happy paths, functional flows, error states, user journeys, and edge cases
- Architect and build comprehensive monitoring and alerting to detect anomalies, saturation, and regressions in real time
- Ensure services are highly available, horizontally scalable, and tuned for high throughput/low latency while optimizing for cost efficiency
- Embed secure‑by‑design practices around PII handling, role‑based/attribute‑based access control, and audit trails for agent actions
