# Business Intelligence Engineer, Creators and Social Analytics Team

**Company:** Amazon
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon is building Earth's most desired destination for creators to monetize their unique skills, inspire the next generation of customers, and help brands expand their reach. The Business Intelligence Engineer will develop innovative solutions to enhance creator success, partner with various teams to design analytics tools, and provide insights to inform strategies in critical areas such as compensation and content quality.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- Data visualization
- Tableau
- Amazon Quicksight

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in BI, finance, engineering, statistics, computer science, mathematics, finance or equivalent quantitative field
- 2+ years of analyzing and interpreting data with Redshift
- Oracle
- NoSQL etc. experience
- Experience using SQL to pull data from a database or data warehouse and scripting experience (Python) to process data for modeling
- Experience with data visualization using Tableau
- Quicksight, or similar tools
- Experience with data modeling, warehousing and building ETL pipelines
- Experience developing and presenting recommendations of new metrics allowing better understanding of the performance of the business

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree in BI, finance, engineering, statistics, computer science, mathematics, finance or equivalent quantitative field
- Experience with AWS solutions such as EC2
- DynamoDB
- S3, and Redshift
- Experience in data mining
- ETL, etc. and using databases in a business environment with large-scale, complex datasets
- Experience in e-commerce, digital marketing, customer service delivery or digital sales

## Responsibilities
- Develop innovative solutions to address creator needs and boost their success on Amazon
- Partner with Business
- Marketing
- Product, and Technical teams to design and maintain scalable, user-friendly analytics tools
- Provide actionable insights to senior leaders, informing strategies in critical areas such as creator compensation and monetization
- creator satisfaction
- marketing segmentation
- content quality
- Help define our data acquisition and intelligence strategy for creator-related data
- Tackle challenging problems that directly impact millions of creators and customers
