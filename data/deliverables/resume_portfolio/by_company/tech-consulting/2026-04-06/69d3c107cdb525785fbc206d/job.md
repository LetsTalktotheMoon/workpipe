# Data Analyst

**Company:** Tech Consulting
**Location:** DC-Baltimore Area
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Tech Consulting is seeking a Data Analyst to leverage their passion for big data and analytics to provide insights for the business. The role involves conducting both recurring and ad hoc analysis, as well as developing clear visualizations to convey complex data effectively.

## Core Skills
- Data Analysis
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Statistics or Applied Mathematics or equivalent experience
- 1 - 2 years' Data Analysis experience
- Proficient in SQL

## Responsibilities
- Understand the day-to-day issues that our business faces, which can be better understood with data
- Compile and analyze data related to business' issues
- Develop clear visualizations to convey complicated data in a straightforward fashion
