# Sr Software Engineer

**Company:** Uber
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Uber is a leading technology company that focuses on mobility solutions. They are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to design and build next-generation tools that empower data-driven strategies, while solving complex technical challenges in distributed systems.

## Core Skills
- Software Engineering
- Python
- Go
- Java
- Microservices

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree (or equivalent experience) in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Mathematics, or a related field
- 4+ years of hands-on software engineering experience
- Proficiency in Python
- Go, or a general-purpose language such as Java
- Familiarity with microservices and API design
- Passion for building performant and scalable systems

## Preferred qualifications
- Proven ability to build scalable, reliable, and distributed systems
- Strong understanding of HTTP/REST/GraphQL, and experience with gRPC or Protobuf
- Experience working w/ large datasets and data pipeline orchestrations
- Proficiency in Python

## Responsibilities
- Design and build next-generation tools that power and visualize investment decisions, enabling smarter, data-driven strategies across the business
- Lead cross-functionally with Engineering
- Product, and Operations teams to deliver scalable systems and intuitive user experiences that drive business impact
- Solve complex technical challenges in distributed systems, ensuring performance, reliability, and scalability at global scale
