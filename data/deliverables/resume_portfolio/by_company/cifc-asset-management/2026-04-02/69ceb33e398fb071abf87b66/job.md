# Junior Data Analyst

**Company:** CIFC Asset Management
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
CIFC Asset Management is a global credit manager focused on various disciplines including CLOs and structured credit. The Junior Data Analyst will be responsible for developing internal business applications and maintaining software solutions to support the organization's goals.

## Core Skills
- Java
- C#
- Python
- Data structures
- Multi-threaded programming

## Minimum qualifications
- Proficiency in any one of the programming languages Java
- C#
- Python
- Good understanding of data structures and multi-threaded(multi-core) distributed programming
- Knowledge of SQL / No-SQL database/s, proficient in reading/writing queries and procs
- Familiarity with a data analysis framework in any programming language, preferably Python or Java
- Ability to come up with creative solutions to find errors and patterns in data
- Very good analytical and debugging skills in finding the root cause and fixing it in real-time
- Experience handling multiple requirements at the same time
- Ability to function at both detail and conceptual levels
- Strong client-facing skills are essential
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Exposure to Azure or any cloud infrastructure would be a bonus
- Good understanding of the latest LLMs (ChatGPT
- Claude
- Gemini, etc.) and related trends
- Experience in AI enabled development and use of AI to solve data issues

## Responsibilities
- Participate in the work to determine, develop, test, and deploy new technology solutions to address specific business needs
- Design, analyze, develop, code, test, debug, and document solutions to business problems
- Collaborate with cross-functional team members (Developers
- Managers
- Business Partners, etc.) to enhance our products and services
- Provide technical expertise and dedication to addressing any issues that may affect the use of currently implemented software products and data
- Collaborate effectively with the offshore team through clear communication
