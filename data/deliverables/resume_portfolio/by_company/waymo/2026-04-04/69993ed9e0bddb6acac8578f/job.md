# Machine Learning Engineer, Data & Systems

**Company:** Waymo
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Waymo is an autonomous driving technology company with a mission to be the world's most trusted driver. They are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to develop methods for data mining, create training recipes, and evaluate real-world performance of models in the context of autonomous driving systems.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Computer Vision
- Python
- PyTorch
- JAX

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelors in Computer Science or a similar discipline, or an equivalent amount of deep learning experience
- 3+ years experience in Machine Learning and/or Computer Vision
- Experience with Python
- Experience with ML frameworks like PyTorch or JAX

## Preferred qualifications
- MS or PhD Degree in Machine Learning
- Robotics
- Computer Science or a similar discipline
- Publications at top-tier conferences like CVPR
- ICCV
- ECCV
- ICLR
- ICML
- ICRA
- IROS
- RSS
- NeurIPS
- AAAI
- IJCV
- PAMI
- Experience with C++

## Responsibilities
- Create foundation scale data sets and training recipes, develop methods and recipes for human and machine labeling of foundation scale data sets
- Create methods and recipes for continuous data intake, selection and incremental updates of large models, develop methods for data mining and recipes for automated data collection/model update flywheels
- Develop methods and recipes for evaluating real-world performance of models, and detecting regressions in model updates
- Develop and maintain ground-truth free performance metrics
