# Software Engineer - Simulation Scenario Full Stack

**Company:** Zoox
**Location:** Foster City, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Zoox is developing the first ground-up, fully autonomous vehicle fleet and the supporting ecosystem required to bring this technology to market. They are seeking a full-stack engineer to develop tools for testing and ensuring the safety of autonomous vehicles, focusing on simulation scenario tools for authoring, inspecting, and evaluating performance.

## Core Skills
- C++
- Python
- Web APIs
- TypeScript
- Single-page web applications

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of industry experience in C++ and Python development
- 2+ years of industry experience, with proficiency in Web APIs and TypeScript
- Experience building sophisticated single-page web applications with modern web development frameworks, such as React
- Angular, or Vue
- Experience developing scalable web back-end services
- A BS in Computer Science or related field

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with 3D libraries/engines (e.g.
- Three.js
- OpenGL
- Unity, etc.)
- Experience with simulation, robotics, or autonomous vehicles

## Responsibilities
- Develop autonomous driving simulation scenario tools to author, inspect, and evaluate the performance of autonomous driving software
- Extend a web-based front-end for 3D graphical definition of detailed autonomous driving simulation scenarios
- Extend a web back-end to interact with simulation, autonomous driving, and agentic AI software
- Work closely with internal customers and other stakeholders to gather requirements and design solutions
