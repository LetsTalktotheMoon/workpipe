# Data Science Analyst

**Company:** Tatari
**Location:** San Francisco, California, United States
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Tatari is on a mission to revolutionize TV advertising through the intelligent application of AI and machine learning. They are seeking a Data Science Analyst to work in a fast-paced environment, collaborating with technology teams to build scalable and automated solutions for diverse data challenges.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- Pandas
- Data manipulation
- Data analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelors degree in Business
- Management
- Economics
- Accounting
- Finance, or Computer and Information Science required
- 1-2 years of academic and/or professional experience in Data Manipulation Software (e.g.
- SQL
- Python
- Pandas)
- Demonstrated abilities to manipulate, analyze, and interpret large amounts of data and to organize findings and translate them into actionable insights using original or innovative techniques
- Extremely organized with great attention to detail
- Excellent ability to analyze information and think systematically
- Works well independently and as part of a team
- Ability to handle databases and understand technology-driven business tools
- A sense of curiosity and desire to learn!

## Responsibilities
- Automate data-driven tasks
- design processes that require zero intervention
- Work cross-functionally within the organization to deliver integrated business solutions
- Provide ad-hoc data analysis, utilizing Python
- PySpark, and SQL to identify and forecast trends and conduct deep-dive quantitative analyses to optimize client marketing performance
- Use best-in-class tools like Databricks, and Tableau to empower insightful analysis for advertising partners
