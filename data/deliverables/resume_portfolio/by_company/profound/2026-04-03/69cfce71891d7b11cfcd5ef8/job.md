# Senior AI Strategist

**Company:** Profound
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Profound is on a mission to help companies understand and control their AI presence, and they are hiring Senior AI Strategists to conduct applied analytical work regarding brand visibility in AI systems. This hands-on role involves working with large-scale AI prompt data to provide data-backed recommendations and collaborating with Fortune 500 brands.

## Core Skills
- AI strategy
- Large language models
- Prompt data analysis
- SQL
- Data analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- 4 or more years of experience as a business operations analyst, data analyst, or technical consultant
- Experience building analytics dashboards, including Retool
- Tableau
- Looker, or similar
- Comfort working directly with raw data and writing SQL
- Experience building tools or projects with AI, such as Cursor
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Comfort operating at the intersection of product, engineering, and customer success
- Naturally curious, with a tendency to ask follow-up questions and explore edge cases
- High ownership and proactive by default, you take analyses end-to-end and surface next steps without being asked

## Responsibilities
- Partner with Fortune 500 brands as a strategic guide through the AI discovery landscape
- Perform applied analysis on LLM behavior, prompt patterns, and visibility mechanics
- Write SQL to extract and structure prompt-level datasets
- Use LLM-based workflows to classify, cluster, and synthesize unstructured data
- Produce concise analytical outputs used in senior-level discussions
- Bridge technical complexity and strategic clarity in client-facing work
