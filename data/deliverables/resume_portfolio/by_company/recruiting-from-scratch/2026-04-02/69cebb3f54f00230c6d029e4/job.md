# Fullstack Engineer (Backend Leaning)

**Company:** Recruiting from Scratch
**Location:** San Francisco, CA|Onsite
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Recruiting from Scratch is a specialized talent firm dedicated to helping companies build exceptional teams. They are seeking a Fullstack Engineer (Backend Leaning) to design and build scalable backend systems and APIs, as well as optimize PostgreSQL databases and develop infrastructure for AI agent execution environments.

## Core Skills
- TypeScript
- Node.js
- PostgreSQL
- API development
- Backend development

## Minimum qualifications
- 2–5 years of software engineering experience (startup experience strongly preferred)
- Strong backend expertise with TypeScript/Node.js and PostgreSQL
- Experience building production-grade systems and APIs
- Background in early-stage or high-growth startup environments
- Demonstrated ownership of features or systems end-to-end
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to operate in ambiguity
- Based in or willing to relocate to San Francisco

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with ML infrastructure, model serving, or real-time inference systems
- Familiarity with containerization (Docker/Kubernetes) and distributed systems
- Exposure to AI/LLM systems or agent-based architectures
- Founder or founding engineer experience
- Strong product intuition and ability to move quickly

## Responsibilities
- Design and build scalable backend systems and APIs using TypeScript and Node.js
- Architect and optimize PostgreSQL databases, including schema design and query performance
- Develop infrastructure for containerized AI agent execution environments
- Build and scale real-time ML inference pipelines and recommendation systems
- Own features end-to-end, from problem definition to production deployment
- Collaborate closely with founders to define product direction and technical architecture
- Contribute across the stack as needed to support product and customer needs
- Ship quickly in a fast-paced, high-ownership environment
