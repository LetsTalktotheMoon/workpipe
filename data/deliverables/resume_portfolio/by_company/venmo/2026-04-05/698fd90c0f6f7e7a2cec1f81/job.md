## About the job
PayPal has been revolutionizing commerce globally for over 25 years, and they are seeking a Software Engineer in Test for their Venmo product. ## Core Skills
- Mobile app automation
- Android testing, iOS testing
- Appium

## Minimum qualifications
- 1+ years relevant experience and a Bachelor's degree OR Any equivalent combination of education and experience
- Proven experience in mobile app automation for Android and iOS platforms
- Proficiency in testing frameworks like Appium
- Espresso, and XCTest
- Strong programming skills in Python
- NodeJS
- Kotlin, or Swift
- Familiarity with CI/CD tools such as Jenkins
- GitHub Actions, or similar
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

## Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain automated test scripts for Android and iOS applications
- Build and optimize mobile testing frameworks and CI/CD pipelines
- Perform functional, regression, and performance testing using automation tools
- Identify, analyze, and report bugs, ensuring comprehensive test coverage
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define quality standards and best practices
