# Senior Software Engineer, Engineering Productivity

**Company:** Roblox
**Location:** San Mateo, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Roblox is a platform that allows millions of users to explore, create, and connect in 3D digital experiences. They are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to support and redefine their source code search, storage, review, and CI infrastructure, while improving productivity and user experience for their engineering teams.

## Core Skills
- Source control
- Continuous Integration (CI) tools
- GitHub
- GitHub Actions
- Artifactory

## Minimum qualifications
- A BS in Computer Science or equivalent with 3+ years work experience
- Owned end-to-end development, launch, and support of a tool or system used by other developers at scale (2000+ of engineers)
- Deep knowledge of source control and/or CI tools and infrastructure
- GitHub
- GitHub Actions, and Artifactory experience are a plus
- Experience working with common cloud and hosting technologies, particularly AWS
- Docker
- Kubernetes, and Hashistack (Nomad
- Vault
- Terraform)
- Fluency in at least one common language (C#
- Python
- Go
- Java)
- Passionate about the developer experience, informed by deep technical knowledge, productivity tooling experience, and a customer-focused mindset
- Experienced working cross-functionally at scale, collaborating with teams across a company to bring solutions together
- An emerging team leader excited to mentor junior developers and level the team up through your evangelism of best practices and teamwork

## Responsibilities
- Support, scale, and redefine Roblox’s source code search, storage, review, and CI infrastructure for the next 10 years of growth
- Design software to improve the lives of your coworkers with new local development tools, internal web apps and microservices, reusable CI workflows, productivity metrics toolchains, and integrations across many 1st and 3rd party tools
- Coordinate and lead major efforts improving the productivity and user experience of our Source and CI tools, spanning teams across Roblox engineering and impacting every engineer at the company
- Bring AI and other cutting edge tech into the day-to-day lives of Roblox engineers, revolutionizing their ability to produce better code faster with less toil and reintroducing them to the joy of software engineering
