# Software Engineer, Ridgeline Engineering

**Company:** Google
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Google is a leading technology company, and they are seeking a Software Engineer for their Ridgeline team. This role focuses on front-end development using Angular, contributing to tools for data center capacity planning and enhancing user experience.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Debugging
- Testing
- Java
- C

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
- 2 years of experience with software development
- 2 years of experience with debugging and testing

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's degree or PhD in Computer Science or a related technical field
- Experience in software development in one or more general purpose programming languages (Java/C/C++)
- Experience in front-end software development (e.g.
- Dart/Angular/TypeScript)
- Experience working in front-end and full stack development
- Excellent collaboration skills with product management and UX

## Responsibilities
- Implement UI using Angular
- TypeScript
- RxJS and other technologies
- Design features from the ground up, measuring launches impact
- Implement scalable, reliable, and performant UI pages, components, middleware services, and tests
- Work with UX designers and product managers and design the UI with them by providing feedback on mocks, assessing their feasibility
- Focus on reliability, engineering and product excellence
