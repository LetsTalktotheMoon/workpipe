# Data Integration Engineer

**Company:** Fastenal
**Location:** Winona, MN
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Fastenal is a Fortune 500 company that has grown as a distributor of industrial and construction supplies. As a Data Integration Engineer, you will help build out data pipelines connecting enterprise systems and support enterprise data needs through designing and developing large scale data and analytical systems.

## Core Skills
- Data integration
- ETL
- Agile software development
- Software engineering
- Software development life cycle

## Minimum qualifications
- Possess or are working towards a bachelor's degree in Information Systems
- Computer Science or a related field of study OR possess relevant work experience
- Excellent written and oral communication skills
- Proficient computer skills
- Exhibit strong problem solving, deductive reasoning and decision making skills
- Highly motivated, self directed and customer service oriented
- Work independently as well as in a team environment
- Learn and perform multiple tasks in a fast paced environment
- Demonstrate strong organization, planning and prioritizing abilities
- Demonstrate our core values of ambition, innovation, integrity and teamwork
- Pass the required drug screen and background check (applicable in the US
- Puerto Rico, and Guam ONLY)

## Preferred qualifications
- Possess at least 1 year of experience implementing data integration across enterprise systems
- Knowledge of Agile Software Development methods
- Knowledge of Software Engineering principles, included the software development life cycle and code versioning
- Prior experience using ETL tools
- Prior experience with Oracle and/or SQL Server databases
- Prior experience with Kafka Streams and/or KSQL
- Prior experience with MongoDB and/or Realm Object Server

## Responsibilities
- Designing and developing large scale data and analytical systems
- Designing, implementing, testing, deploying and supporting data models, storage plans, optimization strategies and other data outputs to support enterprise data needs
- Translating complex business requirements into scalable technical solutions that meet data warehousing standards
- Implementing and developing complex automated ETL routines
- Suggesting and implementing improvements and amendments to existing products, tools, database structures, queries and reports
- Conducting critical reviews of existing solutions with a view to achieving efficiency
