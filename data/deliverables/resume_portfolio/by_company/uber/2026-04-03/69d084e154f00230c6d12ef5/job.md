# Applied Scientist - Marketplace Fairness

**Company:** Uber
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Uber is a technology company that focuses on transportation and delivery services. They are seeking an Applied Scientist for the Marketplace Fairness Team to investigate bias in products and machine learning models, collaborating with various teams to ensure fairness and compliance with policies.

## Core Skills
- Python
- SQL
- Data Analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- Deeply proficient in doing data analysis in Python
- Proficient with SQL

## Preferred qualifications
- PhD in Economics
- Exceptional verbal and written communication skills, with ability to collaborate cross-functionally and translate technical findings for non-technical audiences
- Ability to proactively identify research questions relevant to business or policy
- Project management skills and experience working in a cross-functional environment with legal, policy, and business leaders
- Passion for making Uber's platform work better for all types of users

## Responsibilities
- Conduct deep investigations into the platform and across products
- Conduct fairness testing to assess products, projects, or machine learning models for bias
- Present results to lawyers, leadership, and the people who create these products
- Help build AI/ML governance
