# Data Engineer

**Company:** Lamb Weston
**Location:** Eagle, ID
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Lamb Weston is a leading manufacturer in the food industry, known for its innovative approach and global presence. The Data Engineer will play a crucial role in modernizing data assets and analytics, supporting the transition from legacy systems to a modern data environment, and designing efficient data pipelines to drive business value.

## Core Skills
- Data warehouse design
- Data warehouse implementation
- Kimball methodology
- ETL development
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Requires minimum BS in Computer Science or related field or combination of education and experience sufficient to perform duties at an experienced level
- Must have a minimum of 3 years' experience (in addition to education) in a technical environment with data warehouse design and implementation
- 3 Years of programming experience in SQL
- Java or Python
- In depth knowledge of Snowflake
- Knowledge of Informatica
- 1 Year + working as a DBA
- Requires demonstrated ability to work in a team and collaborative environment
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills
- Love of learning new technologies and having fun
- Ability to travel 10% of the time

## Preferred qualifications
- Preferred experience with assisting with SQL environments and migrating to modern platforms like Snowflake
- Preferred experience with AWS Tooling
- Snowflake
- Domo, and Visual Importer
- Experience modeling in Kimball methodology

## Responsibilities
- Design, builds and maintain data warehouses (existing and from scratch) using Kimball and modern data methodologies
- Debug support issues with the legacy environment and support our business users
- Design and implement efficient data pipelines (ETLs) to integrate data from a variety of sources into Data Warehouse as well as data model changes that align with warehouse standards and backfill or other warehouse data management processes
- Evaluates data design, database architecture, metadata and repositories for performance and effective design
- Develop scalable ETL data pipelines with python
- SQL, and AWS cloud tools
- Optimization of existing SQL queries to improve reliability and performance
- Develop and execute testing strategies to ensure high quality warehouse data
- Provide documentation, training, and consulting for data warehouse users
- Work with cross functional stakeholders to design and architect data warehouse and analytics solutions
- Perform requirement and data analysis in order to support warehouse project definition
