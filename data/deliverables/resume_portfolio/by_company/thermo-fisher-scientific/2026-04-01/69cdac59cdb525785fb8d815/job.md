# Data Scientist II

**Company:** Thermo Fisher Scientific
**Location:** St. Louis, Missouri, USA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Thermo Fisher Scientific is a company focused on making the world healthier, cleaner, and safer. As a Data Scientist II, you will apply advanced analytics and AI solutions to solve complex business challenges and contribute to meaningful projects that have real-world impact.

## Core Skills
- Python
- SQL
- Data analysis
- Machine learning
- Statistical modeling

## Minimum qualifications
- Advanced degree with no prior experience required, or Bachelor's degree plus 2 years of experience in applied data science or related role
- Strong proficiency in Python
- SQL, and data analysis tools
- Expertise in machine learning algorithms, statistical modeling, and data visualization
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS
- Azure) and modern data architectures
- Proficient in data engineering and ETL processes
- Experience with Databricks
- Spark, and big data technologies
- Knowledge of generative AI and natural language processing
- Strong understanding of statistical concepts and experimental design
- Experience with data visualization tools (Power BI
- Tableau)
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Preferred Fields of Study: Data Science
- Computer Science
- Statistics
- Mathematics
- Engineering, or related quantitative field
- Additional relevant certifications in machine learning
- AI, or data science a plus

## Responsibilities
- Join our team of data scientists at Thermo Fisher Scientific, where you'll contribute to innovation through advanced analytics and AI solutions
- As a Scientist II in Data Sciences, you'll apply technologies to solve complex business challenges across our organization
- You'll work on diverse projects ranging from predictive modeling and machine learning to generative AI applications, collaborating with teams to deliver solutions that advance our mission of enabling customers to make the world healthier, cleaner, and safer
- In this role, you'll design and implement analytical models, perform exploratory data analysis, and develop data-driven insights that inform strategic decision-making
- You'll have the opportunity to work with advanced tools and technologies while contributing to meaningful projects that have real-world impact
