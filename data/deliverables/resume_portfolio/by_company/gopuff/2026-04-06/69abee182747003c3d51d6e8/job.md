# Senior Data Scientist - Delivery Technology

**Company:** Gopuff
**Location:** Philadelphia, PA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Gopuff is a high-growth startup revolutionizing hyper-local instant e-commerce. As a Senior Data Scientist on the Delivery Technology Team, you will lead the development of data-driven models to optimize the delivery experience for consumers and delivery partners, directly impacting customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

## Core Skills
- Machine learning
- Statistics
- Experimentation
- Causal inference
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- MS or PhD in Operations Research
- Mathematics
- Statistics
- Economics
- Computer Science
- Engineering or a related field with 2+ years of experience working in delivery and/or logistics (or 8+ years of industry experience in data science)
- Strong ability in translating ambiguous business problems into well-defined technical objectives and providing concrete data science solutions
- Expert in machine learning, statistics, and experimentation
- Proficient in Python
- SQL, and source control management tools

## Responsibilities
- Own the end-to-end development and operation of deep learning-based ETA prediction systems—building features, training/testing models, running experiments, monitoring and explaining performance in production
- Collaborate with our business, product, and engineering partners across the organization to vision and deliver data science solutions for practical business problems
- Deeply understand our users, our products, and our data to identify opportunities for creating machine learning, optimization and causal inference solutions to deliver a better customer experience and make our business more efficient
- Build data driven, robust and replicable data science models
- Use experimentation and causal inference to measure the impact of our models on user engagement and business metrics
