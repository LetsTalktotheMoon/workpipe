# Data Scientist I

**Company:** University of Kentucky
**Location:** Greater Lexington Area
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
The University of Kentucky is seeking a Data Scientist I to support cancer-focused research projects through advanced data extraction, natural language processing, and machine learning methods. This role involves developing scalable analytical pipelines and working with multidisciplinary teams to design analytic workflows and validate model outputs.

## Core Skills
- Data Extraction
- Natural Language Processing
- Machine Learning
- Large Language Models
- Analytical Pipeline Development

## Minimum qualifications
- MS
- 3 yrs
- None, lifting, pushing or pulling of objects up to 25 lbs., sitting for long periods of time, repetitive motion
- Knowledge of advance data visualization techniques
- Experience using Tableau
- ArcGIS
- Microsoft Excel
- PowerPoint
- PowerBI, etc. to visually communicate data findings
- Strong written and verbal communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's Degree with 3 or more years of related experience

## Responsibilities
- Support a broad range of cancer focused research projects through advanced data extraction, natural language processing (NLP), and machine learning methods
- Develop and maintain scalable analytical pipelines, including large language model (LLM)–based systems to transform unstructured clinical and research data into high quality, usable datasets for investigators across the Center
- Work with multidisciplinary teams to design analytic workflows
- Validate model outputs
- Integrate multimodal data sources
- Contribute to manuscripts, grant proposals, and collaborative scientific initiatives
