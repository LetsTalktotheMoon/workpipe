# Software Engineer, Public Sector

**Company:** Scale AI
**Location:** NYC Metro Area
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Scale AI is a company focused on developing reliable AI systems for critical decisions. The Public Sector Software Engineer will design and implement scalable backend systems for federal customers, collaborate with cross-functional teams, and contribute to the platform roadmap and product strategy for the Federal business.

## Core Skills
- Full Stack Development
- Modern web development frameworks
- Programming languages
- Databases
- Cloud platforms

## Minimum qualifications
- Proficiency in front-end, back-end development and infrastructure, including experience with modern web development frameworks, programming languages, and databases
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (e.g.
- AWS
- Azure
- GCP) and experience in developing and deploying applications in a cloud-native environment
- Knowledge of ETL (Extract
- Transform
- Load) processes and experience in building data pipelines to integrate and process diverse data sources
- Familiarity with integrating Large Language Models (LLMs) and building agentic workflows
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills to understand complex challenges and devise effective solutions
- Excellent interpersonal and communication skills to effectively collaborate with cross-functional teams, stakeholders, and customers
- Willingness to embrace new technologies, learn new skills, and adapt to defining and evolving project requirements

## Preferred qualifications
- Understanding of containerization (e.g.
- Docker) and container orchestration (e.g.
- Kubernetes) is a plus
- Understanding of data modeling, data warehousing, and data governance principles
- Understanding of prompt engineering, retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), and agent orchestration is beneficial
- Ability to think critically, identify root causes, and propose innovative approaches to overcome technical obstacles
- Ability to clearly articulate technical concepts to non-technical audiences and foster a collaborative work environment
- Ability to quickly grasp and apply new concepts and stay up-to-date with emerging trends in software engineering

## Responsibilities
- Design and implement scalable backend systems for Federal customers using cloud-native AI infrastructure
- Build features for agentic systems including multi-layered guardrails and data retrieval optimization
- Develop data pipelines and machine learning infrastructure to make data sources accessible by agents
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to execute backend solutions for secure environments
- Participate in customer engagements to understand requirements and deliver technical solutions
- Define requirements with stakeholders and implement features until they are accepted
- Contribute to the platform roadmap and product strategy for the Federal business
