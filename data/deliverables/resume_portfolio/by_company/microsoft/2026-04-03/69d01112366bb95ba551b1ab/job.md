# Senior Software Engineer

**Company:** Microsoft
**Location:** Redmond, WA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite

Microsoft is a leading technology company known for its enterprise software solutions, including Dynamics 365. They are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to design and implement intelligent solutions within Dynamics 365, collaborating with cross-functional teams to build scalable systems that leverage AI to address complex business challenges.
