# Data Analyst III

**Company:** One Inc
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
One Inc is a leading company in the insurtech space, providing a full suite of inbound and outbound payment solutions. They are seeking a Data Analyst III to support the Vendor Network data function by building data visualizations and predictive models, as well as conducting statistical analyses to drive business growth.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Power BI
- Power Automate
- Power Query M
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's in Business Analytics
- Finance
- Marketing
- Statistics
- Math, or Computer Science or equivalent combination of experience
- 4+ years of experience working with large datasets using SQL
- 3+ years of experience using Power BI
- Power Automate
- Power Query M for reporting and data visualization
- Proficiency with Python and/or R
- Experience building data pipelines via Fabric
- Snowflake, dbt
- Fivetran
- AWS, or an equivalent
- Ability to connect to multiple data sources
- Ability to self-start and self-direct work in an unstructured environment
- Must be extremely collaborative and comfortable dealing with ambiguity
- Ability to quickly learn new concepts and products
- Attention to detail with ability to think strategically

## Preferred qualifications
- Payments experience
- Insurance experience
- Fabric machine learning tools

## Responsibilities
- Support the Vendor Network data function by building data visualizations, automated reporting tools, dashboards, and presentations
- Build predictive models to target customers for existing and new products
- Produce statistical analyses to identify areas for process optimization and measure intervention impacts
- Maintain and develop operational metrics and define core metrics to measure the impact of enhancements, monitor risks, and drive revenue growth
- Conduct exploratory data analysis to understand user behavior, product trends, and areas of opportunity
- Acquire and clean data from multiple sources, build pipelines, and schedule queries and data refreshes
- Coordinate research requests with internal and external stakeholders and provide deliverables in a timely manner
- Conduct new product/feature opportunity analyses to prioritize development efforts
- Work with stakeholders to scope new project requests and measure success
