# Software Engineer AI

**Company:** Trimble Inc.
**Location:** Lake Oswego, OR
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Trimble Inc. is a global technology company that connects the physical and digital worlds, transforming the ways work gets done. They are seeking a Software Engineer to contribute to implementing agentic AI workflows for their Vista ERP platform, aiming to modernize and enhance features for the construction industry.

## Core Skills
- C#
- Dot NET
- Large Language Models
- Prompt Engineering
- AI Frameworks

## Minimum qualifications
- 2 to 5 years of professional experience in software engineering with a focus on C# and .NET
- Exposure to or a strong interest in Large Language Models (LLMs), prompt engineering, and AI frameworks
- Foundation in cloud concepts and containerization, specifically with Docker or Azure services
- Experience working with SQL
- REST Web APIs, and fundamental unit testing practices
- Strong collaborative skills and a desire to learn within a high-performing engineering team

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Azure DevOps
- GitHub Copilot, or other AI coding assistants
- Familiarity with Kubernetes (AKS) or Infrastructure as Code concepts
- Previous experience or interest in construction industry software or accounting systems

## Responsibilities
- Assist in developing and implementing agentic AI workflows to help automate maintenance and refactor legacy code within the Vista ERP ecosystem
- Utilize AI agents to help automate the generation of boilerplate code, documentation, and unit tests to improve development efficiency
- Support the development of full-stack solutions, including .NET Core services in Azure
- WinForms, and Blazor web applications
- Contribute to the modernization of application architecture by applying AI-driven patterns and ensuring high coding standards
- Participate in team technical sessions to resolve maintenance challenges and support the delivery of high-quality software features
