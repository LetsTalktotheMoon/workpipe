# On Call / Seasonal Software Developer

**Company:** Fermilab
**Location:** Batavia
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory (known as Fermilab) seeks a highly qualified candidate for an On Call Seasonal Software Developer position in the Data Intensive Systems Division of the Technology Directorate. The role involves algorithm development and verification in the areas of machine learning, artificial intelligence, data science, and big data, along with responsibilities such as designing, coding, testing, and maintaining applications.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Artificial Intelligence
- Data Science
- Big Data
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or master's degree in Artificial Intelligence
- Machine Learning, or related computing or physics field and up to 2 years of relevant experience, equivalent combination of education and experience considered in lieu of bachelor's degree
- Experience with Python or a similar programming language for analyzing and interpreting data

## Responsibilities
- Design, code, test, debug, document, and maintain applications, systems, and/or libraries
- Participate in design discussions and authors design notes
- Contribute to design efforts, regularly gathers requirements and information from users for specific projects, and obtains feedback
- Develop and implement specifications for small projects
- Communicate and coordinate with personnel from other departments on a project, including non-computing disciplines
- Communicate effectively with management, peers, and stakeholders in written and oral form
- Perform operational and/or maintenance activities for one or more specific projects, as assigned
- Perform other duties as assigned by supervisor
- Abide by and is responsible for performing all duties in accordance with all environmental, health and safety regulations and practices pertinent to this position
