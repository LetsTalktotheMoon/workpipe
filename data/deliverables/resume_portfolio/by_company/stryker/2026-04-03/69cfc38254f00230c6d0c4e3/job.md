# Data Integrity Analyst (Onsite)

**Company:** Stryker
**Location:** San Jose, California
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Stryker is a leading medical technology company, and they are seeking a Data Integrity Analyst to manage the accuracy of Field Assets for the Endoscopy Division. The role involves troubleshooting data inconsistencies and ensuring sustained data accuracy across various systems, while collaborating with field teams and stakeholders.

## Core Skills
- Microsoft Excel
- PowerBI
- Inventory Tracking
- Data Transformation
- Data Cleansing

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree

## Preferred qualifications
- Demonstrated proficiency in MS Excel (including data analysis tools i.e. queries, pivot tables, etc.)
- Word
- Outlook
- Experience with PowerBI
- Experience with inventory tracking and management principles
- Experience data transformation and cleansing

## Responsibilities
- Act as a primary escalation contact for field asset inquiries within the business, delivering top-tier service levels and rapid response times
- Gather and manage data across multiple platforms (e.g.
- SFDC
- SAP (EWM/S4)
- Oracle
- ServiceMax) for analysis
- Conduct research to identify solutions and recommend next steps for missing or delinquent equipment
- Present delinquency data to stakeholders to guide decisions on contract and transactional customers utilizing the Microsoft Power Platform
- Evaluate contributions from cross-functional partners to data accuracy and proactively escalate issues affecting the business
- Investigate inventory transactions within the Field Asset value stream to verify accuracy
- Collaborate with field teams to ensure timely product returns
- Determine next steps for equipment sent in house without information after doing adequate research
- Research, analyze, and independently determine the best course of action for requests
- Auditing orders and addressing data discrepancies related to orders, continuously providing feedback to reduce risk of loss product
