# Senior Backend Engineer - Streaming

**Company:** Verkada
**Location:** San Mateo, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Verkada is transforming how organizations protect their people and places with an integrated, AI-powered platform. As a Backend Engineer (Video Streaming), you will design backend systems that scale seamlessly while delivering industry-leading reliability and security for video streaming services.

## Core Skills
- Backend development
- Go
- Python
- Distributed systems
- Service-oriented architecture

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of backend development experience in Go or Python
- 4+ years experience designing and building large-scale, resilient distributed systems using service-oriented or microservice architectures
- 3+ years experience with cloud infrastructure, containerization, and deployment automation (AWS
- Docker
- Kubernetes, or similar)
- 3+ years experience with databases, queues, blob storage, and distributed systems fundamentals
- You bring a systems design mindset, able to simplify complex workflows and deliver scalable, efficient solutions
- You care deeply about performance, reliability, and security, and have a track record of building services that excel in all three
- You thrive in a fast-paced environment, demonstrate creativity and sound judgment, and take ownership of problems end to end

## Preferred qualifications
- Bonus: you've worked with IoT devices, real-time streaming technologies, or multimedia formats such as H.264/H.265
- FFmpeg, or similar pipelines

## Responsibilities
- Design and evolve highly scalable, cloud-native services that power video and audio streaming for millions of devices
- Architect and optimize distributed, real-time data pipelines that connect cameras, the cloud, and end users with low latency and high reliability
- Build and operate a high-performance video transcoding and multiplexing service that is fault-tolerant, cost-efficient, and capable of scaling to massive workloads
- Define and refine service-oriented architectures
- APIs, and interfaces that enable streaming to scale seamlessly across our platform
- Develop clean, reliable, and testable backend code that performs under heavy load and ensures a flawless customer experience
- Collaborate cross-functionally with product, hardware, and infrastructure teams to deliver new video capabilities and experiences that raise the bar for the industry
- Champion engineering excellence by driving performance, reliability, and best practices across the organization
