# Business Analyst

**Company:** Dow Jones
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Dow Jones is a global leader in news and information, seeking a Business Analyst to support their Global Business Operations Team. The role involves turning business questions into insights, maintaining Salesforce dashboards, and assisting with data quality and system usage.

## Core Skills
- Salesforce
- Excel
- Google Sheets
- Data analysis
- Data visualization

## Minimum qualifications
- Willingness to work in our Midtown Manhattan office 3 days a week
- 0–2 years of experience (internships or academic projects in business analysis, data analysis, or similar fields are welcome)
- Bachelor's degree in Business
- Economics
- Information Systems, or a related field preferred
- Familiarity with Salesforce, or a willingness to learn quickly
- Working knowledge of Excel and/or Google Sheets (e.g., pivot tables, basic formulas, data organization)
- Comfortable working with data and learning how to interpret and present it clearly
- Strong problem-solving mindset with a willingness to ask questions and learn new tools
- Good organizational and time management skills
- able to handle multiple tasks with direction
- Clear and professional communication skills, both written and verbal

## Preferred qualifications
- Exposure to data visualization tools (e.g.
- Tableau) through coursework or internships
- Experience working on team projects involving data analysis or reporting
- Interest in emerging technologies, including AI tools and automation

## Responsibilities
- Support the creation and maintenance of Salesforce reports and dashboards based on stakeholder needs
- Assist in building “dashboard-style” reporting in Excel and Google Sheets, including combining and organizing data from multiple sources
- Help monitor Salesforce usage to identify data quality issues and support teams in improving accuracy and consistency
- Perform basic Salesforce tasks such as data entry, record updates, and supporting ongoing operational workflows
- Contribute to process improvement initiatives by gathering data, documenting workflows, and supporting implementation efforts
- Assist in preparing presentations and slide decks by organizing data, creating visuals, and summarizing key insights
- Support testing and evaluation of new tools and systems (including AI add-ons), documenting findings and helping with rollout efforts
- Manage multiple tasks and requests with guidance, prioritizing effectively and communicating timelines clearly
- Collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders to gather requirements and provide updates on work progress
