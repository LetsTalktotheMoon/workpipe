# Software Engineer - Maps Infrastructure

**Company:** Applied Intuition
**Location:** Sunnyvale, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Applied Intuition, Inc. is a leader in physical AI, creating the digital infrastructure for intelligent machines. They are seeking a senior engineer to own and maintain their HD maps infrastructure, focusing on map storage formats, data pipelines, and collaborating with engineers and customers on future designs and features.

## Core Skills
- Maps Infrastructure
- Mapping
- Localization
- Autonomous Vehicles
- Simulation Products

## Minimum qualifications
- Experience building software components or (sub) systems that address real-world mapping and localization challenges
- Experience working in either autonomous vehicles or simulation products
- Working knowledge of C++ and/or Python
- 2+ years of experience within software engineering

## Responsibilities
- Own our maps infrastructure across all of our products
- Define our map storage format to handle more features and distribute map data focusing on UIs, data pipelines and storage, and SDKs
- Collaborate with engineers and customers on existing and future designs and features
