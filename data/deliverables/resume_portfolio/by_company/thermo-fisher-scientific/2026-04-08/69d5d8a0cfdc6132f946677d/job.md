# Data Scientist I

**Company:** Thermo Fisher Scientific
**Location:** Morrisville, NC
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Thermo Fisher Scientific is dedicated to enabling customers to make the world healthier, cleaner, and safer. As a Finance Data Scientist I, you'll develop analytics models and generate predictive business insights using statistics and machine learning, while collaborating with cross-functional teams to analyze complex datasets and drive data-driven decision-making.

## Core Skills
- Statistical analysis
- Machine learning
- Generative AI
- Python
- R

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree required, no prior experience required
- Strong proficiency in statistical analysis, machine learning algorithms, and programming, particularly in Generative AI applications
- Expertise in Python
- R
- SQL, and data visualization tools (Power BI
- Tableau)
- Experience with deep learning frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Proficiency in exploratory data analysis techniques and data preprocessing
- Knowledge of modern data management systems including relational databases and various data stores
- Strong data engineering skills, including ETL processes and data pipeline development
- Experience with big data frameworks and cloud platforms (e.g.
- Databricks
- AWS
- Azure)
- Proven ability to translate complex technical concepts for non-technical stakeholders
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical thinking skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with CRM systems and process improvement methodologies

## Responsibilities
- Develop analytics models and generate valuable predictive business insights through the application of statistics, machine learning, and advanced mathematics
- Analyze complex datasets and develop innovative solutions
- Contribute to data-driven decision-making that impacts global health outcomes
- Explore structured and unstructured data, implement machine learning algorithms, and create effective visualizations that communicate insights from data
- Collaborate with diverse teams across the organization
