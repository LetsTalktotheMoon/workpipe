# Machine Learning Engineer

**Company:** Meltwater
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Meltwater is a pioneer of media intelligence that provides businesses with insights to manage their brands effectively. They are seeking a Machine Learning Engineer to enhance their AI assistant, MIRA, by developing innovative features and improving the multimodal semantic layer, while working on large-scale machine learning systems.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Data Science
- Applied Research
- Experimentation
- Modeling

## Minimum qualifications
- 2+ years experience applying machine learning techniques through academic, professional, or personal projects, ideally with exposure to experimentation, modeling, or applied research
- Solid foundation in machine learning concepts such as feature engineering, model selection, training, validation, and error analysis
- Ability to write clear, well-structured code, with motivation to grow software engineering skills and contribute to production ML systems
- Experience reasoning about metrics, tradeoffs, experiments, and uncertainty in applied ML problems
- You are comfortable learning new tools, concepts, or techniques as needed, and enjoy expanding beyond your initial area of strength
- Advanced knowledge in at least one programming language (Python or other e.g. Rust
- Scala
- Java)
- Passion for AI (in particular Generative AI), with a constant drive to stay updated and ahead of the curve
- You are a team-player, self-learner and autonomous
- MS
- PhD, or equivalent practical experience in machine learning, data science, or a related technical field
- Demonstrable experience with the full lifecycle of machine learning models - from development to deployment and monitoring

## Responsibilities
- Drive the development of industrial ML projects from ideation to deployment, contributing across early prototyping, research, evaluation, and progressively to production, scalability, and monitoring with support from the team
- Collaborate closely with product teams in their domains, building domain expertise, and leveraging ML to solve critical business problems
- Keep up-to-date with the latest advancements in machine learning to stay current on emerging techniques to solve novel problems
- Design, run, and iterate on machine learning experiments to improve model quality and product impact
- Develop features, online and offline evaluation metrics, and validation strategies for large-scale ML systems
- Analyze model errors and failure modes to understand limitations, biases, and opportunities for improvement
- Drive the development of new industrial level projects from ideation to deployment
- This includes deep expertise in areas such as early ideation & prototyping, research, infrastructure, scalability, monitoring, and evaluation of AI systems
