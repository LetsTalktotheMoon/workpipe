# Software Dev Engineer, Developer Agents and Experiences

**Company:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon Web Services (AWS) is at the forefront of AI-driven transformation in the software industry. They are seeking engineers to develop a new AWS service that integrates AI into the software development process, enhancing developer productivity and software quality.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Software Design
- System Architecture
- Design Patterns
- Reliability Engineering

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- Experience programming with at least one software programming language

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- You will be responsible for developing and driving the future direction of a new AWS service
- You'll meet with customers to help understand their use cases and make sure we maintain Amazon's commitment to start from the customer and work backwards
- You'll help manage and operate our systems, using best-in-industry continuous-integration-and-deployment tools
- Participate in the team's Weekly Operations Review and understand the key metrics that drive our product
- Gain experience building and releasing features
- Work with your manager to understand how to grow your career at AWS and how to build a career growth plan
- Become an active participant in all stages of our software development cycle, including planning meetings and customer briefings
- Work with other engineers to improve our operations effectiveness by designing new systems, and making our current ones more effective
- Help onboard new engineers and guide them in their first weeks at AWS
