# Software Engineer I - Ads Demand Enablement

**Company:** Twitch
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Twitch is the world’s biggest live streaming service, with global communities built around gaming, entertainment, music, sports, cooking, and more. The role involves working within the Twitch Ads team to design, develop, and operate interactive experiences and products that enhance advertising quality and user satisfaction. The engineer will collaborate with cross-functional teams to solve customer problems and drive business impact.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Consumer-facing Product Development
- Programming Languages
- Algorithms
- Data Structures

## Minimum qualifications
- 1+ Years experience developing software in a professional environment
- Prior experience building consumer-facing products at scale
- Proficient coding skills in modern languages and frameworks
- Proven ability with algorithms, data structures, data modeling, and schema design
- Minimum of Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with languages/frameworks we use at Twitch: Golang
- Typescript/React
- Experience building software and services using AWS technologies such as ECS
- DynamoDB
- Lambda
- SQS, and Step Functions
- Familiarity with Twitch, gaming, and/or streaming on Twitch

## Responsibilities
- Work with product managers, technical leads, and other engineers to design, develop, launch, and operate interactive experiences and products
- Build distributed applications at scale
- Collaborate with both your team and related teams - fostering shared understanding of how to solve problems and deliver solutions
- Work backwards from customer problems to drive business impact
