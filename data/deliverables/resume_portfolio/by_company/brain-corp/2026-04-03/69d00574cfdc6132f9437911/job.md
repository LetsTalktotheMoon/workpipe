# Software Engineer I, Pilots

**Company:** Brain Corp
**Location:** San Diego, CA
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Brain Corp is a San Diego, California-based AI company creating transformative core technology for the robotics industry. The Software Engineer I will work on pilots of a newly launched product, focusing on solving complex problems in retail automation and improving product performance through software development and collaboration with cross-functional teams.

## Core Skills
- C++ (11+)
- Python
- Embedded systems
- Computer vision
- Object detection

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science, software engineering, or a related field
- 0-2 years of demonstrated experience in software development
- Experience writing C++ (11+) and/or Python applications for embedded systems
- Experience building and evaluating computer vision models for object detection and recognition
- Solid understanding of design patterns, data structures, and advanced programming techniques
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills in a multiple internal and external stakeholder environment
- Strong analytical skills that enable data-driven decisions and prioritization
- Experience with soft real-time and multi-threaded systems

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience designing and developing robotic systems using robotics frameworks, including ROS, and existing libraries and tools
- Experience with path planning
- AI algorithms, navigation algorithms, or robot behavior systems
- Experience with continuous integration, deployment and release management tools
- Experience with Agile and Scrum methodology
- Desire to constantly learn, receive and act on feedback, and share knowledge

## Responsibilities
- Build and review performance metrics and dashboards of the production and/or pilot fleet as a key indicator of customer experience, and product performance
- Analyze trends and identify recommendations in product performance for improvements in our model training pipeline and operations
- Develop code and deliver software features/functionality that increases customer value and product scalability
- Increase the agility/ software release frequency of the team and organization through code refactoring, simplification, and removal
- Coordinate with cross-functional areas of expertise including hardware, firmware, platform, user experience, quality, cloud, autonomy and Applied ML engineering to deliver world-class customer value
- Remain up to date with developments across platform
- AI, autonomy, navigation, and other robots, in order to leverage and contribute towards software innovation across all products
