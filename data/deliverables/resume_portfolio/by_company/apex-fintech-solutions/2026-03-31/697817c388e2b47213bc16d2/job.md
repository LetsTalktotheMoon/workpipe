# Software Engineer I (Java)

**Company:** Apex Fintech Solutions
**Location:** Austin, TX
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Apex Fintech Solutions is a company that powers innovation in digital wealth management by processing millions of transactions daily. They are seeking a Software Engineer I to work closely with software development and business teams to build products and services for their financial back office solutions.

## Core Skills
- Java
- Go
- Python
- RDBMS
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Software Engineering
- Electrical or Computer Engineering, or a related field
- 2+ years of prior work experience preferred
- Experience of software development skills in a modern object-oriented programming language ideally Java / Go
- Python considered a plus
- Experience with an RDBMS or SQL
- Ability to learn new languages and technologies quickly
- Excellent problem-solving skills with high attention to detail
- Effective communication skills
- Adaptability: while you enjoy establishing process and standards, you understand the need to be flexible and enjoy trying new things

## Preferred qualifications
- Previous knowledge in financial markets isn't required, but interest is a plus

## Responsibilities
- Working with a variety of languages/frameworks, such as Java
- Go
- Python and technologies such as RDBMS
- Kubernetes, and Docker
- Google Cloud Platform
- Designing, developing, testing, deploying, maintaining and improving software
- Building resilient, self-scaling, self-healing services that allow you to sleep through the night
- Writing high-quality, well-tested code
- Maintaining high code-quality standards across your team via code reviews
- Attending training and expand domain knowledge on Accounts creation and Maintenance
- Encouraging innovation by questioning business processes throughout the company
