# Senior Data Management Analyst

**Company:** Wells Fargo
**Location:** Irving, TX
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Wells Fargo is seeking a senior data management analyst to support the Consumer Banking & Lending Lines of Business in policy implementation requirements for the Data Management Policy and Procedures. The role involves leading data quality initiatives, conducting analysis to identify data quality issues, and consulting with clients on data and metadata quality.

## Core Skills
- Data Management
- Business Analysis
- Data Quality
- Data Governance
- Metadata Management

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of Data Management
- Business Analysis
- Analytics, or Project Management experience, or equivalent demonstrated through one or a combination of the following: work experience, training, military experience, education

## Preferred qualifications
- Knowledge of Consumer Banking & Lending products, applications
- Risk Assessable Units, and controls
- Experience navigating policies, procedures, and regulation requirements in alignment with role
- Ability to communicate effectively with multiple business partners and lines of businesses
- Ability to develop partnerships and collaborate with other business and functional areas
- Experience with Visio and Microsoft tools including Excel and PowerPoint highly desired
- Strong attention to detail with organizational skills

## Responsibilities
- Lead or participate in moderately complex programs and initiatives for data quality, governance, and metadata activities
- Design and conduct moderately complex analysis to identify and remediate data quality, data integrity, process, and control gaps
- Identify data quality metrics and execute data quality audits to benchmark the state of data quality
- Develop recommendations for optimal approaches to resolve data quality issues and implement plans for assessing the quality of new data sources leveraging domain expertise and data, business, or process analysis to inform and support solution design
- Drive planning and coordination on moderately complex remediation efforts acting as central point of contact
- Consult with clients to assess the current state of data and metadata quality within area of assigned responsibility within Consumer Banking & Lending
- Provide input into communication routines with stakeholders, business partners, and experienced leaders
