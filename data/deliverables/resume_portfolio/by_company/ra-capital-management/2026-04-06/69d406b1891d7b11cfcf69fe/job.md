- JavaScript

- Expert-level Python programming skills
- Strong proficiency in JavaScript and Java
- Deep understanding of machine learning fundamentals (regression, classification, clustering)

- Proven expertise with AWS AI platforms (SageMaker
- Bedrock)

- Design and develop end-to-end AI applications, from concept through deployment
