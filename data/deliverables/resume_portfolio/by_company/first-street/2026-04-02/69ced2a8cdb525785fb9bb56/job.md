# Software Engineer

**Company:** First Street
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
First Street is the standard for Climate Risk Financial Modeling, aiming to connect climate and financial risk. The Fullstack Software Engineer will be responsible for delivering end-to-end features across frontend and backend systems, collaborating with product managers and designers to create intuitive user experiences.

## Core Skills
- Frontend fundamentals
- UI architecture
- Performance optimization
- Accessibility
- API integration

## Minimum qualifications
- Professional experience working as a software engineer on production systems
- Strong frontend fundamentals, including UI architecture, performance optimization, and accessibility
- Experience building and integrating with APIs
- Comfort working across the stack, even if your background leans frontend or backend
- Experience collaborating with cross-functional partners such as product managers and designers
- Ability to debug complex systems and reason about issues across frontend, backend, and data layers
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience in Engineering
- Computer Science, or a related field

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience working in product-driven engineering environments
- Familiarity with GraphQL and modern frontend data-fetching patterns
- Experience debugging or working with data-heavy or geospatial systems
- Prior experience in roles that required close collaboration with non-engineering stakeholders

## Responsibilities
- Build and ship product features across the frontend and backend
- Develop user-facing experiences with attention to UI architecture, performance, and accessibility
- Integrate frontend applications with backend APIs and services (primarily written in Go)
- Collaborate closely with product managers and designers, actively contributing ideas, tradeoffs, and technical perspectives
- Debug and reason about issues across the stack, including identifying whether problems originate in the frontend, backend, or underlying data
- Work with platform engineers and data partners to ensure product behavior aligns with real-world data and customer expectations
- Contribute to code quality, documentation, and shared engineering practices
- Deliver reliable, maintainable software that advances First Street’s mission to connect climate and financial risk
