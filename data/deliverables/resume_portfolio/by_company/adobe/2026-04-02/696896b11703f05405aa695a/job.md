# Business Intelligence Analyst

**Company:** Adobe
**Location:** Lehi, UT
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Adobe is a leading company empowering creativity and productivity through innovative platforms and tools. They are seeking a Business Intelligence Analyst to design and scale analytics solutions that provide actionable insights across customer experience orchestration, focusing on advanced analytics, data engineering, and AI/ML.

## Core Skills
- Advanced analytics
- Data science
- Business intelligence
- Data engineering
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or graduate degree in Computer Science
- Data Science
- Business, or a related field
- 3+ years of hands-on experience in advanced analytics, data science, business intelligence, or data engineering roles
- Strong expertise in SQL
- Python
- Spark, and cloud platforms (Azure
- AWS, or GCP)
- Demonstrable experience in building scalable data pipelines, advanced dashboards, and executive-level reporting (Power BI
- Tableau
- Adobe Workspace)
- Demonstrated track record of developing and deploying ML/AI models in production environments
- Solid understanding of SaaS business models and Customer Success domains (renewals, adoption, churn, value realization)
- Superb communication skills with the ability to clearly explain complex analysis to senior and non-technical audiences
- Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple initiatives and deliver outcomes in fast-paced environments

## Responsibilities
- Own end-to-end development of advanced analytics, dashboards, and data products focused on customer success metrics such as renewals, adoption, churn risk
- NDR, and value realization
- Translate complex datasets into clear, compelling narratives and actionable recommendations for senior leaders and partners across multiple functions
- Design, build, and maintain scalable data pipelines integrating large datasets across subscription, services, usage, and customer health domains
- Ensure data quality, consistency, and governance to support reliable, enterprise-grade analytics and reporting
- Develop and deploy predictive and prescriptive models to surface churn risk, expansion signals, and consumption health
- Build and operationalize ML- and LLM-powered insight workflows and agents that automate analysis and enable next-best-action recommendations
- Create executive-ready dashboards and reports using Power BI (and related tools), optimized for clarity, performance, and decision-making
- Embed advanced metrics, forecasting, and insights generated by machine learning directly into analytics experiences
- Partner closely with Customer Success
- Engineering
- Professional Services
- Sales Operations, and Product teams to align analytics solutions with business priorities
- Act as a trusted analytics advisor, influencing decisions through data rather than formal authority
