# Analytics Engineer

**Company:** FanDuel
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
FanDuel is the premier mobile gaming company in the United States and Canada, and they are seeking an Analytics Engineer responsible for designing, building, maintaining, and improving data models and visualizations. The role involves collaborating with data scientists and engineers to optimize data pipelines and support business performance impacting decisions.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- R
- C#
- Data pipeline development

## Minimum qualifications
- Minimum 3 years of experience in a similar role
- Minimum 2 years building, maintaining and optimizing complex data pipelines with real world applications
- Advanced SQL coding and experience of manipulating large datasets
- Proficient in at least one programming language (e.g. Python
- R
- C# etc.)
- Strong problem solving ability and attention to detail are important
- Experience building data pipelines using Python and Databricks an advantage
- Experience working with data visualization tools like Tableau an advantage
- Undergraduate degree in a coding heavy or technical discipline

## Preferred qualifications
- Interest in Sports an advantage

## Responsibilities
- Work with Data Scientists and Data Engineers to optimize data pipelines and feature engineering for reporting, analysis, classification and prediction models
- Manage data transformation jobs for relevant data, ensure completion on schedule, optimize code, troubleshoot any issues
- Optimize modeled metric pipelines, streamline execution times and prioritizations, reduce query execution times where possible, remove unnecessary or unused metrics
- Build a strong understanding of Sportsbook Product, our customers behaviors and preferences, and your stakeholders objectives, to support collaboration, a focus on delivering impactful output and ideation
- Through idea generation and exploratory analysis, develop new and innovative ways to turn behaviors into features for use in analysis, segmentation and predictive modeling
- Maintain business rule definitions, and code implementations
- Design and implement data quality checks and alerts to manage accuracy of underlying data, and ensure modeled metrics are fit for purpose
- Build strong knowledge in how to optimally use our data platforms, and assist in educating team members in optimal platform usage
- Build and maintain strong working relationships with key stakeholders in relevant teams within the Data Engineering and Data Product departments
- Build and maintain dashboards, optimize code, troubleshoot issues
