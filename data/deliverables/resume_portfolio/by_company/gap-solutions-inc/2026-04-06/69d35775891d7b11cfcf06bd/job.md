## About the job
GAP Solutions, Inc. is seeking a Digital Health And Behavior Data Scientist to provide advanced computational and data science expertise for digital health and behavioral research within the NIH Intramural Research Program supporting the National Institute on Drug Abuse. The role involves leading multimodal data integration, machine learning, and simulation modeling, as well as supporting human subjects research to enhance understanding of substance use and treatment outcomes.
