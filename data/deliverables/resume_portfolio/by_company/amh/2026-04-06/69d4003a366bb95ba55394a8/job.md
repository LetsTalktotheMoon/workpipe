# Senior Enterprise Data Analyst

**Company:** AMH
**Location:** Las Vegas, NV
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
AMH is one of the leading single-family rental companies and homebuilders in the country, recognized as a top employer. The Senior Enterprise Data Analyst will analyze and report on data from data warehouses, create reports and dashboards, and engage with stakeholders to inform business decisions.

## Core Skills
- Business Intelligence
- Financial Reporting
- Data Warehousing
- Requirements gathering
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- High school diploma or GED required
- Minimum three (3) years' experience working with Business Intelligence
- Analytics
- Financial Reporting Data Warehousing technologies, and/or related required
- Minimum three (3) years' experience utilizing proven requirements gathering and business analysis techniques required
- must have a complete understanding of the business intelligence development life cycle including the ability to conduct interviews and gather the needs for desired outcomes for the BI solutions being built
- Intermediate experience querying relational databases and data warehouses (such as SQL Server
- Databricks
- Snowflake, etc.) required
- Intermediate Microsoft Excel experience required including writing and using advanced functions and formulas (e.g., lookup & reference, nested logical expressions, aggregation, etc.) as well as advanced Excel features (e.g., conditional formatting, data validation, pivot tables, external data connections)
- Working knowledge of Microsoft Office (Outlook
- Word) required
- Excellent verbal and written communication and problem-solving skills
- Strong business analysis skills and technical aptitude

## Preferred qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science, mathematics, finance, accounting, economics, statistics, business management, information systems and/or related preferred
- Working knowledge of Tableau or Power BI highly preferred

## Responsibilities
- Utilizes business intelligence tools (Power BI
- Tableau
- SQL
- DataBricks, etc.) to develop analytical content including reports, dashboards, semantic models, and data sets. Supports end-user adoption of reporting and creation of documentation as needed
- Utilizes relational database and data warehouse design skills to codify business logic in SQL to meet reporting requirements for all areas of operation
- Collaborates with business unit owners to elicit requirements using interviews and data analysis, develop and/or improve work product, and create new features in existing report packages
- Works to proactively identify data quality issues, debug erroneous logic and perform QA validations
