# Senior Web Engineer

**Company:** Typeface
**Location:** Palo Alto, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Typeface is a company that helps major brands transition to personalized marketing campaigns quickly. The Senior Web Engineer will be responsible for owning and optimizing the marketing website, ensuring a fast, reliable, and visually appealing user experience while collaborating with design and engineering teams.

## Core Skills
- Full-stack web development
- Frontend development
- Backend development
- React
- Typescript

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field
- 7+ years of experience in developing scalable, reliable, and secure full-stack web applications
- Strong proficiency in frontend and backend development technologies
- Experience building or integrating with Contentful (or similar headless CMS)
- Deep knowledge of React
- Typescript, and Next.js
- Ability to translate high-level requirements into a detailed execution plan
- A product-minded builder who's comfortable partnering with Product and Growth teams to experiment, iterate, and move quickly
- Prior experience working at a high-growth startup or tech company

## Responsibilities
- You’ll own the marketing website from infrastructure to interface, leading its architecture
- CI/CD pipeline, and performance optimization to ensure a fast, reliable, and scalable experience
- Build and maintain the Typeface.ai marketing website, ensuring every page is fast, stable, and visually stunning
- Turn Figma designs into pixel-perfect, responsive experiences that delight and engage our audience
- You’ll collaborate closely with our design team to translate high-fidelity wireframes into polished, production-ready work
- Develop and maintain components in Contentful, integrating content models, and reusable sections aligned with design system standards
- Own the web CI/CD pipeline by managing build and deployment automation through Docker
- Azure Pipelines, and Cloud Run
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Product and Engineering to help shape Typeface’s Web Agent roadmap using real-world insights from the marketing website
- Drive site performance and SEO best practices, ensuring pages are optimized for speed, accessibility, and conversion
- Improve and refactor the codebase to move toward best-practice Next.js Server Components and unify styling systems
