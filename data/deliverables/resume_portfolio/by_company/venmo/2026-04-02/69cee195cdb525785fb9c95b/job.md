# Senior Software Engineer in Test - Mobile

**Company:** Venmo
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
PayPal has been revolutionizing commerce globally for over 25 years, empowering consumers and businesses in approximately 200 markets. The Senior Software Engineer in Test - Mobile will deliver complete solutions across the Software Development Lifecycle, guiding junior engineers and making technical decisions with minimal supervision.

## Core Skills
- Mobile app automation
- Android testing, iOS testing
- Appium
- Espresso

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years relevant experience and a Bachelor's degree OR Any equivalent combination of education and experience
- Proven experience in mobile app automation for Android and iOS platforms
- Proficiency in testing frameworks like Appium
- Espresso, and XCTest
- Strong programming skills in Python
- NodeJS
- Kotlin, or Swift
- Familiarity with CI/CD tools such as Jenkins
- GitHub Actions, or similar
- Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

## Responsibilities
- Delivers complete solutions spanning all phases of the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) (design, implementation, testing, delivery and operations), based on definitions from more senior roles
- Advises immediate management on project-level issues
- Guides junior engineers
- Operates with little day-to-day supervision, making technical decisions based on knowledge of internal conventions and industry best practices
- Applies knowledge of technical best practices in making decisions
- Develop and maintain automated test scripts for Android and iOS applications
- Build and optimize mobile testing frameworks and CI/CD pipelines
- Perform functional, regression, and performance testing using automation tools
- Identify, analyze, and report bugs, ensuring comprehensive test coverage
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define quality standards and best practices
