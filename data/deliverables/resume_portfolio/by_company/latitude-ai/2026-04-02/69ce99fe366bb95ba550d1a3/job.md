# Software Engineer II

**Company:** Latitude AI
**Location:** Palo Alto, CA
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Latitude AI is a subsidiary of Ford Motor Company that develops automated driving technologies. The Software Engineer II role involves designing and implementing offboard L3 map stores, developing software products, and collaborating with internal teams to meet project requirements.

## Core Skills
- Java
- Python
- C++
- Automated testing infrastructure design
- Debugging large scale systems

## Minimum qualifications
- Requires a bachelor's or foreign equivalent degree in Computer Engineering
- Computer Science
- Electrical Engineering
- Robotics, or a related field
- Requires 2 years of experience in the position offered or 2 years of experience performing web application design and development in Java
- Python and C++
- Requires 2 years of experience in: Designing and building scalable end to end automated testing infrastructure and solutions
- and Debugging large scale systems
- Requires experience or graduate-level coursework in: Designing scalable solutions for big-data storage and retrieval
- and Development and visualization of business-critical metrics
- In lieu of a bachelor's degree in the aforementioned fields and 2 years of experience as stated, employer will accept a master's or foreign equivalent degree in the aforementioned fields and experience or graduate-level coursework as stated
- Candidates for positions with Latitude AI must be legally authorized to work in the United States on a permanent basis. Verification of employment eligibility will be required at the time of hire

## Responsibilities
- Help design and implement the offboard L3 map store and map build pipeline to ingest collected map data
- Apply updates to highway maps based on the needs for expansion or detected changes
- Develop rigorous software products within various software programs such as C++
- Python and Java
- Collect map data while working on storage systems
- Be responsible for fusing and mining data while integrating into a cloud-based service
- Utilize complex software in resolving issues that may arise
- Collaborate with internal teams to understand needs and define requirements and interfaces
