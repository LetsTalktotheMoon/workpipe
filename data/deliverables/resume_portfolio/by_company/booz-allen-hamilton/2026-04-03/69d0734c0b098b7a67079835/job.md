# MLOps Engineer

**Company:** Booz Allen Hamilton
**Location:** San Antonio, TX
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Booz Allen Hamilton is seeking an experienced MLOps Engineer to support the development of the Agentic AI platform. The role involves training, testing, deploying, and maintaining machine learning models, collaborating with various engineering teams, and guiding clients in ML strategy.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Statistical Analysis
- ML Model Deployment
- AWS Cloud
- AWS Bedrock

## Minimum qualifications
- Experience deploying production grade ML models onto cloud environments like AWS bedrock
- Experience in building and maintaining CI/CD pipelines for ML
- Experience in managing ML workloads to design elastic infrastructure to scale as needed to provide cost effective resource management for clients
- Knowledge of MLOps Frameworks and Container Systems, such as Kubernetes or Docker
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or software engineering
- AWS Cloud Certification

## Responsibilities
- Train, test, deploy, and maintain models that learn from data
- Lead the direction of critical solutions by applying best-fit ML algorithms and introducing leading-edge technologies
- Share your knowledge with a large community of machine learning engineers across the company
- Collaborate with AI software engineers
- DevSecOps, and Data Engineers to deliver world class solutions
- Guide clients as they navigate the landscape of ML algorithms, tools, and frameworks
