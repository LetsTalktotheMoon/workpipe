# Engineering Program Manager, OS Build Train Management & Integration

**Company:** Apple
**Location:** San Diego, California, United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Apple is a leading technology company, and they are seeking an Engineering Program Manager for their OS Build Train Management & Integration team. This role involves coordinating complex build systems, ensuring seamless code integration, and driving continuous improvement initiatives to enhance build reliability and developer experience.

## Core Skills
- Build
- Release Engineering
- Release Management
- Software Development Life Cycle
- Unix programming

## Minimum qualifications
- BS/MS EE/CS/CE or equivalent
- 3-5+ years experience as a Project or Program Manager

## Preferred qualifications
- Build and Release Engineering/Release Management experience highly desirable
- Project management experience in software development life cycle highly desirable
- Unix programming/scripting experience highly desirable
- Self-motivated and proactive with demonstrated creative and critical thinking capabilities
- Proven track record of defining processes and executing on them

## Responsibilities
- Managing software builds to support releases to beta users and customers
- Identifying, escalating, and communicating key issues
- Developing strategies for improving processes
- Driving resolutions for issues with engineering teams
- Helping to manage risk in the endgame leading up to software launch
