# Senior Data Scientist

**Company:** Outdoorsy
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Outdoorsy Group is a leader in the outdoor travel ecosystem, providing innovative solutions for RV rentals and outdoor experiences. The Data Scientist will be responsible for developing advanced analytics models, including pricing and recommendation engines, to enhance business processes for Outdoorsy and Roamly.

## Core Skills
- Machine Learning
- Travel Industry Experience
- Insurance Industry Experience
- SQL
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years data science experience
- Proven track record of standing up Machine Learning algorithms within consumer-facing products
- Proven ability to understand business imperatives and trade-offs (Bookings vs. Revenue
- Premium vs. Risk)
- Ability to tailor outputs to align with company goals
- Ability to work effectively across departments, e.g. Product
- Engineering, and Finance
- Ability to thrive in fast-paced environments where priorities shift quickly
- Advanced expertise in SQL
- Python/R, and relevant machine learning libraries
- Demonstrated experience with statistical methods such as regression, hypothesis testing, clustering, classification, tree-based methods, neural networks, and anomaly detection
- Familiarity with ELT tools such as FiveTran and DBT
- Ability to collaborate with data counterparts on data repositories and models

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in Travel, marketplace, or insurance
- Expertise with business intelligence tools such as Tableau

## Responsibilities
- Develop Lead Scoring algorithms that predict the LTV of qualified insurance leads, and pass it back to our ad partners to optimize bidding strategies, as well as to our internal Sales team to prioritize outreach
- Develop models to predict churn and claims losses across our insurance products. Work closely with the Underwriting team to ensure these models are integrated into business processes
- Assist in the development of a dynamic pricing model to help RV marketplace hosts adjust prices seamlessly in response to seasonal and vehicle-based factors, as well as surges in demand
- Develop models to predict what ancillary products should be recommended for upsell to a given Outdoorsy / Roamly customer
- Use advanced statistical methods to conduct audience clustering and segmentation on both RV Rental and Insurance customers, and evangelize these findings across stakeholders in Marketing and Product
- Collaborate closely with Data Analysts and Engineers to ensure that data pipelines and models are accurate and optimized
