# Software Engineer

**Company:** Uber
**Location:** Seattle, WA

Uber Technologies, Inc. is seeking a Software Engineer to join their team in Seattle, Washington. The role involves designing, developing, and testing software applications, while also supporting business operations and improving engineering productivity.

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and test software applications
- Design, develop, and/or test software applications using company technology stack
- Apply technical knowledge to evaluate complex data, developments, and projects in order to provide recommendations to resolve technical issues
- Support the company's business operations and improve engineering productivity
