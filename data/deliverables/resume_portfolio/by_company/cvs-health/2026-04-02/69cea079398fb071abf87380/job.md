# Senior Data Scientist

**Company:** CVS Health
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
CVS Health is committed to delivering a simpler, more meaningful, and personal health care experience. As a Senior Data Scientist, you will be working on the Medicare Analytics team, developing advanced machine learning models and collaborating with cross-functional teams to improve member experience and support business decisions.

## Core Skills
- Data Science
- Python
- SQL
- Cloud Technology
- Model Deployment

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of relevant Data Science experience
- 3+ years' experience programming in Python
- 3+ years' experience programming in SQL
- 2+ year experience working with Cloud Technology (GCP
- AWS or Azure)
- 1+ year experience deploying models in production
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience in Business Analytics
- Economics
- Physics
- Engineering
- Mathematics
- Statistics
- Computer Science, or related discipline

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in Google Cloud/BigQuery
- 2 plus years of healthcare or health insurance experience
- Strong problem-solving experience with a history of making business impact
- Knowledge of Time Series forecasting techniques, and Machine learning methods including classics like SARIMA, exponential smoothing, time series regressions, and machine learning methods like LSTM
- XGBoost, etc

## Responsibilities
- Model Development: Develop and implement advanced machine learning models for forecasting and predictive analytics. As a key member of our team, you will be responsible for developing and standardizing forecasting applications using time series techniques to provide forward-looking insights to our members, to improve Member experience
- ML-Ops: Develop, deploy and maintain machine learning models in production, ensuring robust and scalable solutions
- Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including product managers, engineers, and business analysts, to understand business needs and deliver data-driven solutions
- Innovation: Stay up-to-date with the latest advancements in data science and machine learning, and apply them to improve existing processes and models
- Reporting: Create and present detailed reports and visualizations to communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders
- Analytical Skills: Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to work with complex datasets
- Communication Skills: Excellent verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to explain complex technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders
