# Development Manager-Data Integration Platform

**Company:** Mastercard
**Location:** O'Fallon, MO
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Mastercard is a global technology company in the payments industry. The role involves leading a team of software engineers to manage the CDIP platform's deployment, operation, and enhancement while ensuring compliance with organizational standards and data governance requirements.

## Core Skills
- Data management operations
- Data governance
- Data protection
- Privacy regulations
- Software engineering management

## Minimum qualifications
- Strong understanding of data management operations
- Familiarity with data governance principles
- Knowledge of data protection best practices
- Awareness of privacy regulations that govern data usage and exposure
- Demonstrated experience leading and developing technical teams
- Comfortable managing and mentoring engineering talent
- Conducting performance assessments and supporting ongoing growth and development
- Excellent communication skills
- Ability to translate complex technical concepts clearly for both technical teams and business leadership
- Collaborates effectively across teams and organizations

## Responsibilities
- Lead and manage a team of software engineers, including internal developers and external SOW contractors, responsible for building, deploying, operating, and enhancing the CDIP platform
- Oversee development and delivery activities to ensure platform enhancements and releases are executed efficiently, reliably, and in alignment with business objectives
- Partner closely with architecture and technical leadership to ensure development teams clearly understand system requirements, technical standards, and design expectations
- Coordinate across internal and external stakeholders to ensure all development activities comply with Mastercard policies, security requirements, and engineering standards
- Proactively identify, escalate, and resolve risks, issues, and blockers, ensuring leadership has clear visibility and teams remain aligned toward delivery goals
