# Senior Analyst, Enterprise Analytics (Austin, TX - Based)

**Company:** Zendesk
**Location:** Austin, Texas, United States of America
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Zendesk is a company that focuses on enhancing customer service experiences. They are seeking a Senior Analyst in their Enterprise Analytics team to deliver high-impact analytics that drive strategic decisions across business functions, translating complex datasets into actionable recommendations for leadership.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Tableau
- Looker
- Python
- R

## Minimum qualifications
- Minimum 3+ years of experience in data analytics
- Expert proficiency in SQL
- Advanced experience with BI tools (specifically Tableau or Looker)
- Foundational knowledge of Python or R for statistical modeling and data manipulation
- Experience with dbt (data build tool) or similar ETL/ELT frameworks would be preferable
- Hands-on experience using LLMs for prototyping & deployment
- Strong ability to structure problems, perform deep-dive analysis, and synthesize large volumes of data into a cohesive narrative
- Proven track record of presenting complex technical findings to non-technical audiences and influencing senior stakeholders

## Responsibilities
- Act as a strategic partner to leadership in Operations
- Sales, and Finance domains to identify business challenges and define data-driven solutions
- Lead the design, development, and delivery of complex analytical projects, from initial data requirements gathering to final executive presentation
- Create, maintain, and automate high-visibility dashboards in Tableau/Looker that track global KPIs and business health
- Bring a forward thinking mindset to all your work to drive quality & velocity
- Develop, optimize & deploy sophisticated SQL queries and dbt models
- Apply statistical methods to identify trends, perform variance analysis, and build forecasting models to predict business outcomes
- Provide technical guidance and peer reviews for junior analysts, ensuring best practices in SQL optimization and data storytelling
