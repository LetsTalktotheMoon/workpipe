# Senior Data Analyst, People Analytics

**Company:** Uber
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Uber is seeking a Senior Data Analyst for their People Analytics team, who will leverage data to provide insights that influence business decisions. The role involves acting as an analytics consultant, analyzing employee survey data, and communicating findings to stakeholders.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Data analysis
- People analytics
- HR data
- Employee listening

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in a quantitative or analytical field (e.g.
- Economics
- Psychology
- Business
- Statistics
- Data Analytics, or related)
- Strong SQL expertise with the ability to independently write complex, efficient queries on large datasets
- 2+ years of experience analyzing data and delivering insights in a business environment

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience using tools such as Python
- R
- Excel, or similar for data analysis
- Experience in people analytics
- HR data, or employee listening (survey) analysis
- Familiarity with Qualtrics is strongly preferred, including building dashboards in Qualtrics
- Experience in statistical modeling and data science
- Experience with survey design, measurement, or organizational research concepts
- Demonstrated ability to leverage Generative AI tools to automate or augment data analysis and insight generation
- Ability to manage multiple priorities and work effectively in a fast-paced, ambiguous environment
- Strong problem-solving skills and ability to translate business questions into structured analysis

## Responsibilities
- Act as an analytics consultant to the business
- Partner with stakeholders to quickly understand business questions and translate them into clear, actionable analyses
- Deliver timely, high-quality insights that inform decisions on engagement, retention, performance, and organizational health
- Balance speed and rigor to support both rapid questions and deeper dives
- Analyze Uber-wide employee survey data to identify trends, risks, and opportunities
- Support survey reporting, interpretation, and storytelling to drive action across teams
- Contribute to improving how we measure and understand employee experience
- Query, clean, and analyze data from multiple data sources (e.g.
- HRIS, surveys, behavioral data)
- Build survey dashboards, reports, and repeatable analyses that enable self-service and scalable insights
- Translate data into clear narratives, visualizations, and recommendations for both technical and non-technical audiences
