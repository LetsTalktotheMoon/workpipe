# Data Analyst (Entry-Level / Fresher) – Non-C2C

**Company:** Emonics LLC
**Location:** New York, United States
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Emonics LLC is seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Entry-Level Data Analyst to join their team. This opportunity is ideal for fresh graduates or candidates starting their careers in analytics, where you will work with real-world datasets to generate insights and support business decisions.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Microsoft Excel
- Statistics
- Data analysis
- Power BI

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Data Science
- Statistics
- Computer Science
- Mathematics
- Economics, or related field
- Basic knowledge of SQL
- Proficiency in Microsoft Excel
- Understanding of statistics and data analysis concepts
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Good communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Python or R (Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib)
- Tableau or Power BI
- Basic knowledge of machine learning
- Internship or academic project experience

## Responsibilities
- Collect, clean, and validate data from multiple sources
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Build dashboards and reports using Excel
- Power BI, or Tableau
- Write SQL queries to extract and analyze data
- Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies
- Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business requirements
- Present insights using clear visualizations and reports
