# Senior Software Engineer - Bloomberg Finance & CFO

**Company:** Bloomberg
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Bloomberg is a global leader in financial data and analytics, and they are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to join their Finance Development team. The role involves collaborating with business users to create and enhance custom applications that support Bloomberg's Finance community, focusing on web-based applications and system integrations.

## Core Skills
- Object-oriented programming
- C#
- Java
- Software development lifecycle
- Web services

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years experience programming and systems design utilizing object-oriented languages (e.g. C#
- JAVA)
- A degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or a similar field of study or equivalent work experience
- Experience in all phases of the software development lifecycle
- Experience creating and working with web and Windows services
- Experience with relational databases design (e.g. SQL
- PostgreSQL)
- Understanding of fundamental networking concepts (e.g. firewall, proxy traversal)

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience in full web-stack development (i.e. JavaScript
- CSS
- HTML)
- Significant experience with SQL syntax and stored procedure development
- Experience programming in C# or Python
- Experience of working within an IT organization
- Comfortable working closely with users to understand and solve business problems

## Responsibilities
- Create and enhance custom applications which eliminate inefficiencies or provide brilliant solutions to seemingly intractable problems
- Build and maintain web/desktop applications
- Office Add-Ins, and back-end system automation tools
- Interact with numerous existing systems and their supporting teams to utilize Bloomberg infrastructure and tools resulting in bespoke solutions to solve complex business problems
