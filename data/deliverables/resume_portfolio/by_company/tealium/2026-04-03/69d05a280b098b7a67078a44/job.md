# QA Engineer (Remote)

**Company:** Tealium
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Tealium is a leader in real-time Customer Data Platforms, dedicated to helping organizations unify their customer data for personalized experiences. They are seeking a QA Engineer to uphold high standards of quality through testing methodologies, collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure software reliability and efficiency.

## Core Skills
- QA Engineering
- Software Quality Assurance
- Playwright
- TypeScript
- REST API Testing

## Minimum qualifications
- 3–5 years of QA experience, with a strong track record in ensuring software quality and reliability
- Proven experience delivering high-availability enterprise software products in a fast-paced SaaS environment
- Proficiency in Playwright
- TypeScript, and REST API testing, with a focus on automation and scalability
- Experience with AWS and GraphQL is a plus, demonstrating familiarity with modern cloud and API technologies
- Comfortable with continuous integration tools, distributed version control systems (e.g.
- GitHub), and virtualization technologies, supporting streamlined development workflows
- Quick learner with a passion for adapting to new technologies and tools, eager to grow and expand technical expertise
- Strong problem-solving skills and meticulous attention to detail, ensuring high-quality deliverables
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills, able to clearly convey ideas and collaborate effectively
- Team-oriented mindset, thriving in collaborative, cross-functional environments
- Experience with automated testing tools and frameworks is a plus, enhancing efficiency and coverage

## Responsibilities
- Develop and execute comprehensive test plans for new features using TypeScript and Playwright, building on existing QA frameworks
- Document test cases and results clearly in the QA tracking system to ensure transparency and reproducibility
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams, communicating issues and findings clearly and proactively
- Contribute to the development and continuous improvement of QA strategies to enhance overall product quality and efficiency
- Lead and advocate for QA processes across development teams, ensuring adherence to best practices
- Deploy and manage builds in QA environments, leveraging appropriate tools to support testing workflows
- Analyze and evaluate automated testing framework results, identify issues, and drive actionable insights
- Adapt smoothly to shifting priorities, maintaining focus and productivity under changing project requirements
