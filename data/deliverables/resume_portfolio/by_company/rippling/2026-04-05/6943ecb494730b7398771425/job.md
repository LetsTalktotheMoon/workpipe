# Forward Deployed Engineer

**Company:** Rippling
**Location:** Austin, TX
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Rippling is a company that provides a unified platform for managing HR, IT, and Finance functions. The Forward Deployed Engineer will work closely with customers to understand their business processes, design, and deliver custom solutions on the Rippling Platform, ensuring seamless integration into their operations.

## Core Skills
- TypeScript
- JavaScript
- Python
- Go
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science or a related field
- 2+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong proficiency in at least one of TypeScript
- JavaScript
- Python, or Go, with experience in SQL and data modeling
- Ability to operate independently in fast-moving, ambiguous environments
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to build trust with customers
- Customer-centric mindset
- prior experience in a customer-facing or consultative role is a plus
- Solid project ownership and execution skills
- Willingness to travel to customer sites as needed (up to ~25%)

## Responsibilities
- Partner directly with customer stakeholders — from individual contributors to executives — to understand business processes, pain points, and success criteria
- Own the full lifecycle of customer solutions, from discovery and scoping through build, deployment, and post-launch iteration
- Build custom solutions on the Rippling Platform, including data connectors, custom apps, functions, workflows, data models, reports, and analytics by writing code in TypeScript
- JavaScript
- Python
- Go, and SQL, alongside Rippling’s no-code tooling
- Translate ambiguous business requirements into clear technical designs and working software
- Provide technical guidance and support throughout implementation, including troubleshooting, optimization, and adoption support
- Identify opportunities where customer solutions can inform or extend native Rippling product capabilities
- Collaborate closely with account teams, product managers, and engineers to align customer needs with platform capabilities
