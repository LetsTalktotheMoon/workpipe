# Software Quality Assurance Analyst

**Company:** SS&C Technologies
**Location:** Fort Worth, TX
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
SS&C Technologies is a leading financial services and healthcare technology company headquartered in Windsor, Connecticut. They are seeking a Software Quality Assurance Analyst responsible for ensuring the quality and reliability of trust accounting and asset management software products through test planning, execution, and documentation.

## Core Skills
- Database Query Languages
- Data Validation
- Manual Testing
- Test Planning
- Regression Testing

## Minimum qualifications
- Extensive experience with database query languages and data validation
- Extensive experience with manual testing of complex and legacy software systems
- Proven ability to create technical documents and training materials, including user guides, process documentation, and reference manuals
- Strong critical thinking and problem-solving skills, with the ability to analyze issues from multiple angles
- Excellent written communication skills, with the ability to convey technical concepts clearly and effectively

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with multivalue databases (such as UniData or UniVerse)
- Experience with oil and gas accounting software or related industry processes
- Broad knowledge of financial markets and securities operations, with exposure to Trust and Fiduciary products

## Responsibilities
- Develop, document, and execute comprehensive test plans, test cases, and regression tests for legacy software applications
- Conduct detailed manual testing to identify defects, inconsistencies, and performance issues across functional areas
- Write and execute complex database queries to validate data integrity and troubleshoot issues
- Collaborate with product managers, developers, and business stakeholders to understand requirements and confirm acceptance criteria
- Produce high-quality technical documentation, user guides, and training materials for internal and external audiences
- Participate in requirement reviews, design walkthroughs, and release planning activities
- Contribute to process improvement efforts and advocate for quality throughout the development lifecycle
