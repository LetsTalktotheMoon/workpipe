# AI Software Development Engineer

**Company:** Intel
**Location:** Folsom, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Intel is a leading technology company focused on semiconductor manufacturing and AI solutions. They are seeking an experienced AI Software Development Engineer to enhance their capabilities in AI system development and DevOps practices, particularly for Large Language Model (LLM) integration and scalable AI applications.

## Core Skills
- DevOps
- Containers
- Kubernetes
- Large Language Model (LLM) evaluation
- Agent AI

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree with 3+ years of relevant experience, master's degree with 2+ years of relevant experience, or PhD with 1+ years of relevant experience in Computer Science
- Data Science, or any related STEM discipline
- 2+ years of experience with the following technical skills:
- Hands-on experience with DevOps, containers, and Kubernetes
- Experience with LLM evaluation
- Agent AI, and backend API development
- Experience managing SQL/NoSQL databases via DBaaS
- Experience with Linux and virtualization

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience building ML applications using Python and/or R
- Knowledge of semiconductor manufacturing or related domains
- Experience deploying and maintaining production-grade systems

## Responsibilities
- Productize and integrate LLM and Agent AI solutions into PBG business systems
- Evaluate, benchmark, and select LLMs for enterprise use cases
- Develop backend APIs using Python and /or C#
- Lead and enhance DevOps practices, including automation of AI/ML pipelines
- Deploy and operate containerized, microservices-based applications using Kubernetes
- Support CI/CD and production deployment of AI-enabled systems
