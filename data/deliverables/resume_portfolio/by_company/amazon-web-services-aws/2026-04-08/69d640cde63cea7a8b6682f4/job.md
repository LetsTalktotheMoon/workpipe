# Software Development Engineer, Training & Certifications

**Company:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon Web Services (AWS) is seeking a talented Software Development Engineer to join their engineering team. The role involves designing, developing, and operating distributed systems and services that enhance the learner experience for AWS Training and Certification programs.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- System Design
- Architecture
- Distributed Systems
- Object Oriented Design

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- 1+ years of designing and developing large-scale, multi-tiered, multi-threaded, embedded or distributed software applications, tools, systems, and services using: C#
- C++
- Java, or Perl experience
- 1+ years of Object Oriented Design experience
- Bachelor's degree or foreign equivalent in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Mathematics, or a related field

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience

## Responsibilities
- Design and implement scalable, reliable, and secure services and features
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
- Own operational excellence for your team's services, including on-call responsibilities
- Participate in system design, code reviews, and architectural discussions
- Identify and resolve bottlenecks, performance issues, and technical debt
- Contribute to team processes, mentoring, and engineering best practices
- Work across the stack (front-end, back-end, infrastructure) as needed
