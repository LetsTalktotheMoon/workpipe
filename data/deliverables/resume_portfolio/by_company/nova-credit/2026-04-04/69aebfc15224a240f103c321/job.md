# Mid Level Software Engineer

**Company:** Nova Credit
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Nova Credit is on a mission to power financial inclusion globally by building a unified platform for credit and financial data. The Mid-Level Software Engineer will contribute to high-impact initiatives, implement robust solutions across products and platforms, and collaborate with experienced engineers to enhance their technical skills.

## Core Skills
- Node.js
- React
- TypeScript
- Python
- PostgreSQL

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of relevant experience, ideally in a fast-paced or product-driven environment
- Proficiency with modern software technologies, with ability to quickly learn and contribute within our tech stack
- Growth-minded and eager to learn, adapting quickly to new challenges and priorities in a dynamic environment
- Effective communicator and team player, able to collaborate with technical and non-technical stakeholders

## Responsibilities
- Developing and enhancing features for our enterprise-grade APIs, web applications, and dashboards
- Working on the implementation of new data supplier integrations
- Improving and deploying features based on existing data models for new attributes and scores
- Take ownership of defined features, delivering with quality and support
- Actively participate in team norms (e.g., project planning, code reviews
- QA, on-call rotation)
- Collaborate effectively within your engineering team as well as with product and design to ensure successful execution on team projects
- Deepen your knowledge of specific product areas and take action on identified improvements
- Understand how your work directly contributes to our business goals and products
- Successfully deliver multiple features, from implementation to deployment
- Become proficient in your product vertical and related technical artifacts
