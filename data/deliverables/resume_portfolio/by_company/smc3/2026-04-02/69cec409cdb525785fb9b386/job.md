# Data Analyst

**Company:** SMC³
**Location:** Peachtree City, GA
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
SMC³ is a company that focuses on data solutions, and they are seeking a Data Analyst to evaluate and manipulate data for their products. The role involves performing data verification, collaborating with customers, and maintaining data accuracy while following established procedures and guidelines.

## Core Skills
- Microsoft Office
- Excel
- Word
- Database
- MS SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Minimum 2-year Programming or Data related college degree, professional certification or equivalent related work experience
- Microsoft Office (Excel
- Word)
- Accuracy
- Adaptability
- Attention to Detail
- Dependability
- Initiative
- Interpersonal
- Logical Thinking
- Oral Communication

## Preferred qualifications
- Database (MS SQL
- MySQL and/or Access)
- Programming language(s) (i.e.
- C#
- VB.net
- VBA, etc.)
- VBA Script (MS Access)

## Responsibilities
- Perform assigned duties with appropriate supervision and guidance
- Follow established procedures to run programs and processes used to build, maintain, and verify products
- Execute existing procedures, programs, and processes to build and validate products in accordance with customer requirements
- With guidance, modify existing procedures, programs, and processes to create or update customer data
- Perform all aspects of data verification, including regression testing, to ensure data accuracy and integrity
- Collaborate with customers to gather, clarify, and document project requirements
- Develop and maintain productive working relationships with customers to support data continuity and accuracy
- Stay current with the software tools and systems used to build and maintain SMC³ products and data content
- Identify potential alternatives or improvements and present recommendations to the direct manager/supervisor and the Data Services team
- Perform other related duties as assigned
