# Business Intelligence Analyst II

**Company:** The Trade Desk
**Location:** Ventura, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
The Trade Desk is changing the way global brands and their agencies advertise to audiences around the world. They are seeking a Business Intelligence Analyst II who will own the development of cross-functional data models and scalable data solutions, while partnering closely with various teams to drive data-driven decision making.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Relational databases
- Data warehouse methodologies
- Snowflake
- Databricks

## Minimum qualifications
- A quantitatively focused education (minimum BSc in Statistics
- Economics
- Computer Science etc.)
- 5+ years professional experience in Analytics Engineering
- Data Engineering, or Business Intelligence roles
- SQL proficiency
- Deep hands-on expertise with relational databases and data warehouse methodologies (Snowflake
- Databricks
- Vertica, etc.)
- Experience translating complex business problems into elegant, scalable data models leveraging modern data tools like dbt
- Experience with multiple programming languages (Python
- R, etc.)
- Experience with BI tools such as Tableau
- Power BI, or Looker
- Strong understanding of enabling self-serve analytics
- Genuinely curious and a lifelong learner
- Fearless advocate of positive change

## Preferred qualifications
- AdTech experience

## Responsibilities
- You will own the development of cross-functional data models, core business metrics, and self-service analytics capabilities
- You will design and maintain scalable data solutions that enable reliable, timely access to business-critical insights
- You will develop analytical frameworks that support data-driven experimentation and business health monitoring across the organization
- You will anticipate and solve common data access patterns across product, marketing, finance, and operations teams
- You will partner closely with Business Analysts and Data Engineers to develop deep expertise in the business and serve as the bridge between business needs and data infrastructure
- You will work with engineering teams to understand impacts to existing analytics products when changes to existing data models occur
- You will champion data standards, governance, and best practices across the organization
