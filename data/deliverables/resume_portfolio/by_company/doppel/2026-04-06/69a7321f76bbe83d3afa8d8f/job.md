# Software Engineer, Infrastructure

**Company:** Doppel
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Doppel is a company focused on combating digital threats such as social engineering and deepfakes, aiming to make the internet a safer place. They are seeking an experienced backend engineer to build and scale the infrastructure necessary for their AI-native social engineering defense platform, which involves monitoring billions of digital entities and deploying adaptive AI agents.

## Minimum qualifications
- Experience with backend engineering
- Experience building infrastructure on Kubernetes
- Experience with Elasticsearch
- Experience with Terraform (IaC)
- Experience in setting up staging environments for safer releases
- Experience with observability and tracing (metrics, logging, distributed tracing)

## Responsibilities
- Build out the infrastructure required to rapidly scale up the engineering organization
- Built self-hosted Elasticsearch infrastructure on Kubernetes, enabling real-time search across millions of alerts and associated metadata
- Defined core infrastructure using Terraform (IaC), allowing reproducible, version-controlled environments and faster onboarding for new engineers
- Stood up a dedicated staging environment, enabling safer releases, feature validation, and automated integration testing before production rollouts
- Introduced observability and tracing (metrics, logging, distributed tracing), improving our ability to debug performance issues and maintain reliability at scale
