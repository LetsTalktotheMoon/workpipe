# Full Stack Engineer

**Company:** Vorto
**Location:** Denver, CO
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Vorto is on a mission to improve America’s Supply Chain efficiency and sustainability. The Full Stack Engineer will design, build, and operate mission-critical services embedding AI technologies to automate workflows and enhance productivity in logistics.

## Core Skills
- Software engineering
- Component-driven front-end engineering
- Angular
- Vue
- React

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of professional software engineering experience delivering production systems at scale
- Strong background in component-driven front-end engineering (Angular
- Vue
- React, or Svelte) and modern UX patterns
- Fluency with SQL schema design and data modeling for transactional and analytical workloads
- Demonstrated ability to leverage LLMs/AI agents to boost velocity and solve novel problems
- Superior product reasoning skills
- Exceptional written and verbal communication
- able to align technical decisions with business outcomes

## Preferred qualifications
- Proven track record of owning problems end to end
- Experience stabilizing and scaling large code bases

## Responsibilities
- Design, build, and operate services and UIs used daily by shippers, carriers, and drivers
- Embed AI/LLMs (Vertex AI, open-source models, agentic tools) to automate decision-making and supercharge developer productivity
- Stabilize back end services
- Own problems end-to-end
