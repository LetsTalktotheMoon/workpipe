# IT Software Engineer

**Company:** CommonSpirit Health
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
CommonSpirit Health is one of the nation’s largest nonprofit Catholic healthcare organizations, focused on building a healthier future through integrated health services. The Software Engineer is responsible for improving user experience by enhancing applications, managing application builds, and collaborating with development teams to meet business objectives.

## Core Skills
- Apex Development
- Lightning Web Components
- Aura Components
- SOQL
- SOSL

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent knowledge and skills obtained through a combination of education, training, and experience in a Healthcare environment
- 2-4 years' experience working in IT as an Application Systems Analyst or Technical analyst type role
- Proven proficiency in writing robust, scalable, and efficient Apex code (classes, triggers, batch Apex, scheduled Apex
- Queueable Apex)
- Experience developing custom user interfaces using LWC or Aura frameworks
- Expert-level knowledge of querying Salesforce data
- Ability to maintain and enhance existing Visualforce pages (though LWC/Aura is preferred for new development)
- Experience consuming and exposing web services (SOAP and RESTful APIs) for integrations with external systems
- Familiarity with event-driven architecture for integrations
- Proficiency with Git for source code management
- Understanding of continuous integration and continuous deployment pipelines

## Preferred qualifications
- Salesforce Certified Platform Developer I
- Salesforce Certified Platform Developer II

## Responsibilities
- Strives to improve the user experience by implementing and enhancing applications
- Perform application build and configuration management functions or work directly with Software Engineers by writing technical specification documents for development
- Perform testing, configuration management for the implementation, enhance or upgrade of solutions on premise or in a cloud hosted environment
- Developing Robust
- Scalable, and Maintainable Salesforce Solutions Aligned with Business Requirements and Best Practices
- Designs, configures, tests, implements, and maintains solutions with limited direction
- Responsible for delivering project assignments of medium to high complexity which includes application design, and configuration build
- Interacts with the customer user community in a courteous and professional manner to secure business requirements
- perform technical requirements translation and work directly with Software Development engineers when development is needed to meet business objectives
- May lead Product Release management functions which may include developing test plans, perform test plans, facilitating with end user for end user acceptance testing and may develop application validation scripts for ongoing operations team
- Interacts with business teams to develop application requirements and installation plans
- Contribute to and maintain a thorough understanding of application standards, policies, and procedures and Participates in ensuring transition to operations functions have occurred to Technical Analysts resources for successful Leads and coordinates peer reviews of single facility or application focused configuration work to ensure it aligns to the business and technical requirements
