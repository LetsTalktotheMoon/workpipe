# Data Analytics Senior Associate

**Company:** Baker Tilly US
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Baker Tilly is a leading advisory, tax and assurance firm, providing clients with a genuine coast-to-coast and global advantage. The Data Analytics Senior Associate will support FP&A with insights, management reports, and analysis for decision-making processes, while developing business-critical analysis and reporting to enable data-driven decisions.

## Core Skills
- Data Analytics
- Financial Planning
- Analysis
- Forecasting
- Budgeting

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree required
- emphasis in data analytics, data science, computer science, management information systems, finance, statistics or related field
- 3-5 years of experience performing data analytics in a financial setting (financial statement analysis or development, forecast analysis, trending analytics, understands key finance metrics, chart of accounts, etc.) with strong focus in Financial Planning and Analysis
- Experience and strong understanding of forecasting, budgeting, variance analysis, and financial modeling
- Hands on practical experience and proficiency utilizing Microsoft Power BI
- Azure Databricks
- Databricks SQL and SQL Server
- Experience with consumer-level and industry-level data
- Ability to synthesize complex data into clear, actionable insights for senior leadership consumption
- Experience utilizing cloud-based technologies (preferably Azure) and data lakehouse architecture
- Be an excellent communicator and collaborator, engaging with multiple technical and business stakeholders and leaders and be able to translate business questions and analysis into simple briefings for use by various parties
- Ability to work independently in a fast-paced, growth-oriented environment without sacrificing quality

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with professional service industry or CPA and advisory firms preferred

## Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain data models, reports, and dashboards for FP&A function and firm overall with a focus on reporting forecasting data, budgeting and planning data, and trends, and providing insights for leaders
- Own end-to-end forecasting, budgeting, and planning reporting to support FP&A function
- Translate business needs into analytics/reporting requirements to support FP&A decisions and workflows
- Support overall firm initiatives from an FP&A perspective
- Proactively analyze and mine data lakehouse to identify additional insights, data quality concerns, and opportunities for additional data modeling
- Support users with ad hoc training, troubleshooting, etc. related to FP&A data and reporting
