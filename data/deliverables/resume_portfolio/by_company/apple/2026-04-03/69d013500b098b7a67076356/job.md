# Senior Software Engineer, Apple Cash

**Company:** Apple
**Location:** Cupertino, California, United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Apple is a company that transforms innovative ideas into extraordinary products and services. In this role, you will architect and build innovative solutions and features for Apple Cash, while collaborating with various teams to understand and meet their needs.

## Core Skills
- Object-oriented analysis
- Java
- Client-server development
- API design, implementation

## Minimum qualifications
- Experience with object-oriented analysis and design with languages like Java
- Experience in client-server development
- API design and implementation
- Experience with Relational and NoSQL Databases
- BS degree in computer science or equivalent field plus minimum 3 years of software development experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience building high availability, high volume, distributed web applications and services
- Good understanding of cloud technologies, tools and frameworks

## Responsibilities
- Write bullet-proof Java code (thread-safe, performant, object-oriented, and inherently testable)
- Engage with internal stakeholders including product, fraud, security, legal, compliance, privacy and operations to understand their needs and requirements
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams on feature definition and development
- Work with external partners and vendors to ensure quality deliverables
