# Data Scientist, Customer Support Analytics

**Company:** Etsy
**Location:** Brooklyn, NY
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Etsy is the global marketplace for unique and creative goods. They are seeking an experienced Data Scientist to support product and operations teams in delivering exceptional customer support through insights and analytics. The role involves partnering with various teams to improve user experience and drive decision-making using data.

## Core Skills
- Data Science
- Data Analysis
- SQL
- Statistical Concepts
- Hypothesis Testing

## Minimum qualifications
- 2+ years of proven success as a data scientist or analyst focused on supporting product teams with data access, reporting, and insights
- Successful track record of distilling highly complex problems into narratives that are concise, action focused, and memorable
- High proficiency with SQL
- Strong grasp of advanced statistical concepts (hypothesis testing, statistical power, time series), statistical scripting languages (e.g. Python
- R)
- Ability to communicate insights to senior executives verbally, visually, and in writing
- Curious mentality to drive creative problem-solving and business impact
- A trust in and commitment to Etsy's vision, mission and values

## Responsibilities
- Partner with operations leaders and product managers within our Customer Support operations & product teams
- combine support and Etsy user behavioral data to shed light on opportunities for improving the effectiveness and user experience of Etsy Support’s self-service solutions, improve the accuracy of our support ticket forecasts, and develop robust reporting for support ticket trends
- Identify impactful metrics to understand the performance of your partner teams, and ingrain these metrics into teams’ day-to-day decision-making
- Lead proactive analyses to uncover opportunities, help teams set strong hypotheses, and deliver robust analysis
- Partner with product, engineering, design teams
- use behavioral and transactional data to shed light on our users and our support product experience, identify areas for improvement, and drive decision-making
- Lead experimentation, help teams set strong hypotheses, and deliver robust analysis of experiment results
- Continually evaluate and refine your technical toolkit
- teach what you learn to the team
