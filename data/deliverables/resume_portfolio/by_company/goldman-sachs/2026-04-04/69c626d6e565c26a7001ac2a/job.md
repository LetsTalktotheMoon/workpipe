# GBM, Public, ETF One Delta Strat - Quantitative Engineering (NYC)

**Company:** Goldman Sachs
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Goldman Sachs is a leading global investment banking, securities and investment management firm. They are seeking a Quantitative Engineer to handle operational aspects of algorithmic trading, ensuring robust and high-performing systems while optimizing trading technology and data stacks.

## Core Skills
- Java
- Python
- Data Structures
- Algorithms
- Systems Design

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science
- Computer Engineering, or a related technical field
- A strong foundation in CS fundamentals (data structures, algorithms, and systems design) is essential
- Expert-level proficiency in Java and Python
- Experience or strong interest in working with proprietary languages and cross-language integration (specifically JSI)
- Deep understanding of software engineering lifecycles, including CI/CD, version control (Git), and performance profiling
- Exceptional debugging skills and the ability to navigate complex, distributed systems under time-sensitive conditions

## Preferred qualifications
- Prior experience in a systematic trading group
- HFT firm, 'Strat' or Engineer team at a major investment bank
- Experience building and maintaining large-scale ETL processes and time-series data pipelines
- Familiarity with ETF market microstructure, delta-one products, or systematic hedging strategies is a significant plus but not a substitute for strong engineering skills
- Previous experience with Slang or similar proprietary financial languages (e.g.
- Athena
- Quartz)

## Responsibilities
- Proactively identify and resolve technical bottlenecks in the trading lifecycle
- build automated solutions to enhance desk efficiency and system reliability
- Provide expert-level technical troubleshooting for production trading systems, ensuring 24/7 high availability and performance in volatile market conditions
- Design, build, and maintain the high-performance tech and data stack powering our systematic block market-making business
- Translate complex quantitative pricing models and business logic into robust, production-grade code for real-time pricing and risk management
- Develop and optimize the JSI layer to ensure seamless, low-latency communication between our Java-based trading infrastructure and our proprietary Slang environment
