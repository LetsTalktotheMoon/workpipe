## About the job
Brex is an AI-powered spend platform that assists companies in managing their financial operations efficiently. They are seeking a Software Engineer II to develop new products from concept to launch, focusing on enhancing customer experiences and driving business impact.

## Core Skills
- Full-stack application development
- Client-side code development
- Software architecture
- Software testing

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of professional experience designing, developing, and deploying full-stack applications
- Experience architecting, developing, and testing client-side code end-to-end
- A high bar for engineering standards and a willingness to learn

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate with engineers across the company to build new features and products end-to-end
- Work alongside user-facing teams like Sales and Support to deeply understand our customers' needs
- Design and implement solutions to improve our customers' banking experiences
- Own problems end-to-end, from user experience and data models to scalability, operability, and ongoing metrics
- Tune and polish features to a high degree of excellence
