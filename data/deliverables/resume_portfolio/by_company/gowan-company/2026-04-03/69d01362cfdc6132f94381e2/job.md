# Forward Deployed Engineer

**Company:** Gowan Company
**Location:** Gowan Co Arizona Salaried, Yuma, AZ, US
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Gowan Company is seeking a Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE) to design, deploy, and support advanced technology solutions within their global organization. This role requires collaboration with internal stakeholders to translate business requirements into technical solutions while participating in the full solution lifecycle.

## Core Skills
- Software Engineering
- Systems Engineering
- Production Deployment
- Customer-facing Solutions
- Manufacturing Industry Experience

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Information Systems, or a related field, or equivalent experience
- Minimum of 3 years of professional experience in software engineering, systems engineering, or related technical roles
- Experience deploying and supporting solutions in production or customer-facing environments
- Strong proficiency in one or more programming languages
- Experience with cloud platforms
- APIs, system integrations, and enterprise applications
- Working knowledge of databases, data pipelines, and analytics tools
- Familiarity with DevOps practices, version control systems, and CI/CD pipelines
- Strong problem-solving and analytical skills
- Ability to communicate technical concepts clearly to non-technical stakeholders
- Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple priorities

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience working in manufacturing, life sciences, chemicals, agriculture, or other regulated industries is preferred
- Familiarity with Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools and/or AI assisted development software like Claude Code
- Bilingual preferred: English
- Spanish

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, deploy, and maintain software and system solutions that meet business and operational needs
- Work directly with business users and functional teams to gather requirements and understand workflows
- Customize and integrate solutions with existing enterprise systems and platforms
- Participate in the full development lifecycle including design, coding, testing, deployment, and support
- Troubleshoot, diagnose, and resolve complex technical issues across applications, systems, and integrations
- Collaborate with cross-functional IT teams, vendors, and partners to deliver effective solutions
- Ensure solutions adhere to security, compliance, and regulatory standards
- Document system designs, configurations, deployment procedures, and support processes
- Provide technical guidance and mentorship to other IT staff as needed
- Stay current with emerging technologies and recommend improvements or innovations
