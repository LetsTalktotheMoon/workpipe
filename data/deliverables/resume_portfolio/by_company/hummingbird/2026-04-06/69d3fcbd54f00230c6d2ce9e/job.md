# Senior Software Engineer, Infrastructure

**Company:** Hummingbird
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Hummingbird is a remote-first, fully distributed team united by the shared mission of helping fight financial crime. The Senior Software Engineer, Infrastructure will be responsible for building a platform that can scale to meet customer needs, while working collaboratively with a passionate team to tackle complex challenges in a high-security environment.

## Core Skills
- Cloud hosted web applications
- AWS
- Terraform
- Developer tooling
- Monitoring tools

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of experience with cloud hosted web applications, preferably with some startup experience
- You love digging into complex problems and determining both the root cause and the best solution to fix
- You're happy spelunking logs and running diagnostic tools to make sure you understand the problem
- You sail through ambiguity and enjoy jumping around the stack, touching everything from low level networking and file systems to database configuration and high level architecture
- Iteration is part of the fun. You're not married to specific ideas or solutions
- You want your impact to go beyond the lines of code you write
- Safety first. You care about quality, testing, and security
- You like digging deeply into new technologies, new domains, or new ideas
- You want to build something exceptionally valuable and have a real impact on the world. You're excited to get your hands dirty in pursuit of that goal

## Preferred qualifications
- (Bonus) Experience in the financial sector, especially around compliance or fraud

## Responsibilities
- Support the underlying infrastructure that enables our platform to help fight financial crime. You’ll build developer tooling, manage our AWS environment using Terraform, make cross-cutting code changes, analyze scaling issues, setup and configure monitoring tools, and identify performance bottlenecks
- Work with a small, passionate, and experienced team: We're a small team of mostly senior engineers and we love to do great work together. We're fully remote, but we make a strong effort to prioritize, design, and code collaboratively
- Grapple with the challenges of working in a high security, high stakes environment: We work with some of the most sensitive data you can imagine and provide a critical service to our customers. Security, reliability, and correctness must always come first
- Influence the overall architecture and hosting strategy: Great ideas come from everywhere. Everyone on the team has a voice in technical discussions, and is trusted to make significant decisions
- Have the opportunity to grow into leadership: Hummingbird is growing rapidly. As we do, there will be opportunities for engineers to take on new roles in product, technology, or people leadership positions
