# Data Analyst, Client Insights

**Company:** Madhive
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Madhive is the leading independent operating system for local media professionals. They are seeking a Data Analyst to serve as a strategic consultant for enterprise clients, engaging directly with them to provide insights that drive growth and retention.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- R
- Presentation tools
- PowerPoint

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field (Economics
- Finance
- Stats
- CS, etc.)
- 2-5+ years of experience in analytics, preferably in a client-facing capacity (e.g.
- Media Agency
- Sales Engineering, or Consultative Analytics)
- Strong SQL proficiency is required
- Experience with Python/R is a plus
- Expertise in presentation tools (PowerPoint
- Google Slides) and the ability to design aesthetically pleasing client deliverables
- Experience with BI tools (Looker
- Tableau) for creating external-facing views
- Clear communicator with the ability to explain findings to non-technical audiences

## Responsibilities
- Partner with Sales and Client Success to act as the dedicated data consultant for specific enterprise accounts, attending client meetings and presenting insights to external executives
- Create bespoke, client-ready presentations that go beyond 'what happened' to explain 'why it matters' and 'what to do next.'
- Proactively monitor client campaign performance to identify trends and opportunities for optimization, then pitch these recommendations directly to the client
- Build tailored Looker dashboards or reports that address specific client KPIs, ensuring they have transparency into their media spend and performance
- Provide clients with vertical-specific insights (e.g.
- Auto vs. Retail trends) to help them shape their media strategy using Madhive data
