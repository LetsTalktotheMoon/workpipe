# Data Conversion Analyst

**Company:** Grant Street Group
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Grant Street Group is seeking a Data Conversion Analyst to join their team. In this role, you will work closely with project managers, developers, and data analysts to migrate clients to the TaxSys software, focusing on data preparation, QA, and client communication.

## Core Skills
- Data Mapping
- Specifications Writing
- SQL
- ETL Tools
- Customer Interaction

## Minimum qualifications
- At least 1 year of experience in working directly with customers
- Experience with data mapping, specifications, and/or other deliverables for developers
- Analyzing data using SQL tools
- Experience with SQL
- Outstanding planning and time management skills
- Willingness to travel about 15% annually for face-to-face meetings with clients and colleagues
- Project management experience and desire to lead projects

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with ETL tool and/or dbVisualizer a plus

## Responsibilities
- Prepare data for customer training and verification by customer subject matter experts
- Write specifications for conversion into TaxSys and QA the results
- Track down bad customer data and analyze how it got that way
- Communicate effectively with developers and management regarding documentation and project status updates
- Guide clients in verifying their data in TaxSys and help them understand tradeoffs in data management
