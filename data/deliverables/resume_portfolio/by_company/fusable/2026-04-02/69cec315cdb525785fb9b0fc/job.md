# Data Specialist

**Company:** Fusable
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Fusable is a company that provides analytics and benchmarking sources for off-highway equipment and on-highway vehicles. The Data Specialist will support the maintenance of internal databases by collecting and organizing data, cleansing existing data, and running bi-monthly data sprints while collaborating with Data Analysts and Senior Data Specialists.

## Core Skills
- Data entry
- Data accuracy
- Data cleansing
- Data collection
- Data management

## Minimum qualifications
- Minimum 1-2 years of experience. The ideal candidate has specific industry experience in similar industries
- Strong interpersonal and communication skills with the ability to consult, partner, and work effectively with analyst leaders and business partners across cultures and functions
- Process improvement skills to identify opportunities for advancement of data collection and management
- Desire to advance in understanding data collection and organization
- Associate's degree or equivalent required
- Software: Required – strong working knowledge of MS Excel and internet research. Interest in learning new skills such as SQL
- R, and others

## Responsibilities
- Maintains and assists in the development of scaled collection and review processes to keep equipment databases in line with industry trends
- Develops expertise and innovation for quality control processes supporting EquipmentWatch and Price Digests
- Clearly communicates needs with analyst and management staff
- Reports weekly progress to analysts and managers
- Runs bi-monthly sprint processes and assists the other team members
