# Data Scientist

**Company:** Citadel Securities
**Location:** Miami, FL
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Citadel Securities is a next-generation capital markets firm and a leading global market maker. As a Data Scientist, you will analyze complex datasets to extract insights, build predictive models, and collaborate with various stakeholders to inform strategic business decisions.

## Core Skills
- Python
- R
- Pandas, scikit-learn
- TensorFlow

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of full time experience in a fast-paced financial or similar setting
- Strong programming skills in Python and R, with experience using libraries such as Pandas, scikit-learn, and TensorFlow
- Proficiency in tools like Tableau for creating compelling visualizations
- Solid understanding of statistical methods and their application in data analysis
- Ability to write complex SQL queries to extract and manipulate data from databases
- Experience with cloud platforms such as GCP
- Familiarity with big data tools like Spark and Kafka
- Understanding of ETL processes and data pipeline design
- Bachelor's degree in a STEM field

## Responsibilities
- Conduct thorough data analysis and create visualizations to understand data patterns and trends
- Develop and implement machine learning models to predict outcomes and optimize business processes
- Design and execute experiments to test hypotheses and validate model performance
- Present findings and insights to stakeholders in a clear and actionable manner
