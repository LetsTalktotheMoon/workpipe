# Data Engineer

**Company:** SafeRide Health
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
SafeRide Health is a technology and services company dedicated to reducing barriers to care by improving the delivery of non-emergency medical transportation. They are seeking a Data Engineer to design, build, and optimize scalable data systems that support data-driven decision-making and enhance operational workflows across the organization.

## Core Skills
- Data modeling
- ETL development
- Data warehousing
- Analytics datastores
- Cloud warehousing solutions

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Mathematics, or related field
- 3+ years of professional experience in data engineering or related roles
- Hands-on experience with data modeling
- ETL development, and data warehousing
- Experience with analytics datastores such as Redshift
- Snowflake
- SQL Server, etc
- Experience working with Cloud warehousing solutions such as AWS Glue
- Azure Data Factor
- GCP BigQuery, etc
- Experience with BI and reporting tools such as QuickSight
- Tableau, or PowerBI
- Proficiency in at least one modern programming language (e.g.
- Python
- Java
- Scala)
- Strong problem-solving, troubleshooting, and analytical skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Strong understanding of system design and data architecture principles
- Experience supporting operational workflows or compliance-driven processes (e.g., complaints, claims, or regulatory reporting)
- Experience working with multiple database platforms and very large-scale datasets
- Strong communication skills with the ability to work cross-functionally

## Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain scalable ETL pipelines and data workflows to support analytics and operational use cases
- Develop and optimize data models and warehouse structures to enable efficient reporting and insights
- Partner with cross-functional teams (Operations
- Compliance
- Product
- BI) to translate business requirements into data solutions, including support for complaints and regulatory processes
- Improve and maintain data quality, integrity, and governance standards across systems
- Optimize query performance, dashboards, and reporting infrastructure for scalability and efficiency
- Contribute to system design discussions, ensuring data architecture supports long-term business needs and growth
- Troubleshoot and resolve data issues, ensuring reliability and accuracy of datasets used across the organization
