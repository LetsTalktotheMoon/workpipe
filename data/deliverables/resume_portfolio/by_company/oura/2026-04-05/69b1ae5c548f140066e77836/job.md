# Data Scientist - Movement

**Company:** ŌURA
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
ŌURA is a company dedicated to empowering individuals to understand and improve their health through innovative products. They are seeking a Data Scientist to develop movement algorithms and translate sensor data into actionable insights, collaborating closely with hardware and engineering teams to enhance product offerings.

## Core Skills
- Data Science
- Machine Learning
- Algorithm Development
- Time-Series Data Analysis
- Sensor Data Analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- MSc degree with 2+ years of relevant industry experience or a PhD in a related field (e.g., data science, biomechanics, signal processing, or machine learning)
- Experience developing and deploying data science or machine learning solutions to production as part of a cross-functional team
- Hands-on experience working with time-series data from multi-axis accelerometers, gyroscopes, or similar MEMS sensors, ideally in wearables, biomechanics, or motion analysis contexts
- Strong understanding of sensor data characteristics, noise sources, and real-world constraints, and experience designing algorithms that are robust to these challenges
- Expert-level proficiency in Python, including deep knowledge of scientific/ML libraries, and experience building robust, scalable, and production-ready code
- Expert-level proficiency in SQL and data wrangling, and comfort contributing to and maintaining the data pipelines for the features you develop
- A solid understanding of experimental design, causal inference, and validation methodologies for ML-powered product features
- Familiarity with end-to-end development using AWS (or a similar cloud service)
- Experience working with international and distributed teams
- Prior experience in the athletic performance technology space or wearable sensing

## Preferred qualifications
- A background in biomechanics, kinesiology, or sports science
- Experience with MLOps principles and tools for model deployment and monitoring
- Experience with hardware development
- Experience with deep learning frameworks (e.g.
- PyTorch
- TensorFlow)

## Responsibilities
- Develop Movement Algorithms: Contribute to the research, prototyping, and development of movement and activity algorithms, with a focus on leveraging existing and emerging hardware sensing capabilities
- Translate Sensor Data into Insights: Design, build, and deploy production-grade models and analytical pipelines that translate raw sensor data into accurate and actionable movement metrics and personalized insights for Oura members
- Collaborate on HardwareAlgorithm Co-Design: Partner closely with Hardware
- Firmware, and Engineering teams to evaluate new sensing modalities, understand sensor limitations and tradeoffs, and influence how hardware capabilities translate into reliable movement algorithms
- Influence Product Direction: Collaborate with Product
- Engineering, and Design to shape the technical vision and roadmap for movement sensing and tracking, ensuring solutions are scientifically sound and deliver meaningful member value
