# AI Product Engineer [Accounting]

**Company:** Bridge Legal
**Location:** Greater Chicago Area
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Bridge Legal is a hyper-growth tech startup that provides an AI-powered platform to help plaintiff law firms streamline casework. The AI Product Engineer [Accounting] will architect intelligent financial systems, automate accounting processes, and leverage AI to transform financial data into strategic insights.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Python
- Data Automation
- Data Pipelines
- AI Tools

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Data Analytics
- Computer Science
- Engineering, or related field
- 1+ years of hands-on experience with SQL
- Python, or comparable data tools
- Demonstrated ability to build automation workflows or data pipelines
- Deep familiarity with and experience using AI tools and AI Agents
- Strong analytical mindset with the ability to translate finance problems into technical solutions
- Highly organized, detail-oriented, and capable of managing multiple initiatives
- Mandarin language proficiency required
- Comfortable communicating with engineers
- Fast learner

## Preferred qualifications
- Strong understanding of GAAP principles and financial reporting structures preferred
- Experience applying AI/ML techniques to structured financial data is strongly preferred

## Responsibilities
- You will work with product and technology teams to automate our manual accounting processes
- You will work with our technology teams to form a datalake for our financial products, establishing multiple integrations with our operation systems to sync data on a daily basis
- Build data validations to cross-check data accuracy and timeliness
- Automate financial reporting from our centralized data source
- Leverage AI to find insight and drive actionable changes to our operations/technology
- Architect and implement a centralized financial data infrastructure that unifies ERP, accounting, product, and operational systems into a single source of truth
- Transition finance from manual, reactive reporting to automated, real-time financial intelligence
- Design and deploy scalable data pipelines and integrations to ensure accurate, daily synchronization of financial and operational data
- Establish rigorous data validation, governance, and audit controls to ensure accuracy, integrity, and compliance across all financial systems
- Automate core accounting workflows, reconciliations, and reporting processes to materially reduce close cycle time and manual workload
