# Software Engineer, Early Career

**Company:** Aquatic Capital Management
**Location:** Chicago, New York
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Aquatic Capital Management is a quantitative investment manager focused on the global financial markets. They are seeking an Early Career Software Engineer to collaborate with experienced researchers and technologists, building systems that contribute to the firm's profitability.

## Core Skills
- Python
- C++
- Event-driven architectures
- Distributed systems
- Quantitative skills

## Minimum qualifications
- A bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Software Engineering, or equivalent
- A history of building software solutions in Python or C++
- No more than 3 years of professional (post-education) experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Stronger candidates will have knowledge of event-driven architectures
- Stronger candidates will have knowledge of distributed systems
- Strong quantitative skills preferred

## Responsibilities
- Work 1-on-1 with senior engineers who will mentor and guide you in each tech rotation
- Apply hands-on software development using Python or C++
- Learn software design and testing techniques, such as Refactoring and Test Driven Development
