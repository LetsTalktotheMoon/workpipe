# Senior Analyst - American Football, Data and Insights (D&I)

**Company:** Electronic Arts (EA)
**Location:** Orlando, FL
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Electronic Arts (EA) creates next-level entertainment experiences that inspire players and fans around the world. The Senior Analyst will be part of the Data & Insights team, leading critical insights into the operations, processes, and tools that drive the creation of American Football games.

## Core Skills
- Data Science
- Quantitative Analysis
- SQL
- Python
- R

## Minimum qualifications
- A degree in a quantitative discipline (Analytics
- Data Science
- Business
- Statistics
- Mathematics
- Economics)
- 3+ years of experience in an analytics role performing quantitative analysis with big data to help guide decisions
- 1+ years of experience independently managing projects and building analysis plans with clear goals & timelines
- Expertise in SQL and ability to extract data from large data sources with differing structures
- Proficiency analyzing large datasets in programmatic analysis tools (e.g. Python
- R)
- Experience with data visualization software (e.g. Tableau
- PowerBI
- Looker)
- Experience in statistical modeling (e.g. regression, clustering, predictive modeling)
- Passion for playing and thinking about games

## Responsibilities
- Report to the Director of Sports Analytics for the Tiburon Studio
- Collaborate with the Live Service team to establish key metrics and targets to measure program and content performance against expectations
- Receive questions from our partners, determine how our data can help answer them, and provide insights through reports, presentations, or dashboards
- Lead presentations to diverse audiences including senior management
- Help to shape and implement analytics best practices and team vision
