# Applied AI ML Associate - Payments Operations

**Company:** JPMorganChase
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
JPMorgan Chase is one of the oldest financial institutions, providing innovative financial solutions globally. The Applied AI ML Associate will develop and implement machine learning applications for the Payments Operations team, focusing on enhancing business insights and model performance through collaboration and innovation.

## Core Skills
- Python
- R
- Machine Learning Frameworks
- Scikit-Learn
- TensorFlow

## Minimum qualifications
- BS degree in Computer Science
- Mathematics
- Engineering, or related field with 3+ years of experience, or MS in a related field
- Proficient in Python
- R, or other equivalent languages, including experience using machine learning frameworks such as Scikit-Learn
- TensorFlow
- Pytorch, as well as version control, testing and object-oriented programming
- Strong mathematical and statistical skills, including knowledge of exploratory data analysis, statistical models
- GLM, decision trees, clustering, and bootstrapping
- Experience with deep learning for language and image classification, image to text extraction, and knowledge of CNNs
- RNNs and Transformers
- Ability to manage AI/ML project from inception to production, including extraction of insights, planning and completion of milestones, iteration on solutions and evaluation of business tradeoffs
- Eagerness to work with large and complex datasets, including preprocessing raw and unlabeled multimodal data
- Excellent communication and presentation skills that enable collaboration and transparency with non-technical senior leaders as well as technical counterparts
- Self-motivated and comfortable working with a geographically distributed team

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Language Models, and Computer Vision
- Ability to design intrinsic and extrinsic evaluations of a model's performance aligned with business goals

## Responsibilities
- Identify automation opportunities by gathering business knowledge from stakeholders, exploring large volumes of complex data, and testing hypothesis using traditional and cutting-edge machine learning models
- Propose and implement innovative approaches to process multimodal data to enhance business insights and power machine learning models
- Design and build machine learning models in partnership with different parts of the business, by understanding key challenges and metrics of success, communicating results, and gathering feedback to improve model performance and expectations
- Productionize and evaluate models, ensuring compliance with relevant processes and governance
