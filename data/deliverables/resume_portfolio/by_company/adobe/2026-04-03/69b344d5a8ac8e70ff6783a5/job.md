# Software Development Engineer

**Company:** Adobe
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Adobe is a leading company that empowers everyone to create through innovative platforms and tools. The Senior Software Engineer will play a pivotal role in building scalable systems that democratize access to data, focusing on transforming how businesses derive insights from customer data.

## Core Skills
- Software development
- SaaS product development
- Data insights
- Data science
- Data structures

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of software development experience at major technology companies and/or software startups building SaaS products and Agents
- Real passion for solving complex customer problems using data insights, data science and analytics
- General understanding of data structures, algorithms and distributed computing concepts
- Proficiency in Python
- Spark
- SQL, etc and Apache Airflow
- Databricks
- Snowflake
- DAX and ETL
- Experience in visualization tools such as Tableau
- Power BI, and Adobe Analytics
- B.S. in Computer Science (or equivalent experience)

## Preferred qualifications
- A total of 7 to 10 years of hands-on experience in advanced analytics and data science, machine learning/statistics
- Comfort with ambiguity, adaptability to evolving priorities, and the ability to work autonomously
- Proficient understanding of Statistical Modeling
- Machine Learning
- Predictive Analytics
- A/B Testing
- Customer Segmentation
- A relentless pursuit of great customer experiences and continuous improvements to the product

## Responsibilities
- Build technical specifications, prototypes and presentations to communicate your ideas
- Design, build, improve and maintain highly performant and highly scalable services and data processing pipelines
- Work in a small, collaborative, highly productive, and high output team environment
- Champion standard methodologies and process improvements within the team
- Learn new technologies and technical solutions
