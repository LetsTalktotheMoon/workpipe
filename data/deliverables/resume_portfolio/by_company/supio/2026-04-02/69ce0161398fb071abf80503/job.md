# Software Engineer, Core Product (Mid–Senior+)

**Company:** Supio
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Supio is looking for product-minded engineers who enjoy building across the stack and owning problems end-to-end. The role involves crafting user experiences, designing scalable systems, and applying LLMs and AI in real-world products.

## Core Skills
- Front-end development
- React
- TypeScript
- Back-end development
- Python

## Minimum qualifications
- Experience shipping production software and owning meaningful systems or product areas
- Strong proficiency in either modern front-end development (e.g.
- React
- TypeScript) or backend systems (e.g.
- Python
- APIs, data pipelines), with the ability to work across the stack when needed
- Experience designing scalable, reliable systems and thinking through architecture, performance, and long-term maintainability
- Solid engineering fundamentals, including testing, code review, and iterative development practices
- Ability to translate ambiguous problems and customer needs into clear technical solutions
- Strong collaboration and communication skills in cross-functional environments

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience working with LLMs
- AI/ML systems, or data-intensive applications
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS
- GCP, or Azure) and modern infrastructure patterns
- Experience in startup or high-growth environments

## Responsibilities
- End-to-end product experiences that define how customers interact with our platform - from polished, intuitive interfaces to the scalable systems behind them
- AI-driven capabilities powered by LLMs, turning research and experimentation into production-grade features used in high-stakes, real-world workflows
- The core backend services
- APIs, and data pipelines that power evaluation, orchestration, and intelligent decision-making at scale
- High-performance, reliable systems designed to handle growing volume, complexity, and customer demands
- The internal tooling, monitoring, and infrastructure that ensures our AI workflows are accurate, observable, and continuously improving
