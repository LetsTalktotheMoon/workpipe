# Data Management & Quantitative Analyst I

**Company:** SYNCREON
**Location:** PA-51, Pittsburgh, PA, USA
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
SYNCREON is a company that provides recruitment and staffing services across various industries. They are seeking a Data Management & Quantitative Analyst I to partner with data owners, perform data analysis, and support data quality initiatives.

## Core Skills
- Data Management
- Metadata Analysis
- Microsoft Excel
- Data Quality Frameworks
- Data Quality Controls

## Minimum qualifications
- Strong data management and metadata analysis skills
- Proficiency in Microsoft Excel
- Experience with data quality frameworks, controls, and issue management systems
- Familiarity with logical data models and data environments
- Strong analytical, organizational, and documentation skills
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
- Ability to work under pressure in a fast-paced environment

## Responsibilities
- Partner with source system data owners to understand, document, and analyze data requirements, including scope, limitations, and data relationships
- Perform data analysis, querying, and manipulation based on defined business rules and procedures
- Conduct root cause analysis for data issues, assess impact, and recommend remediation steps
- Support data quality initiatives, including implementation and monitoring of data quality controls
- Prepare ad-hoc reports and provide insights to management and stakeholders
- Ensure accuracy, completeness, and reliability of data outputs
- Work in a deadline-driven environment while managing multiple priorities effectively
- Handle inquiries professionally and provide timely responses
- Identify business impact levels for data quality issues and recommend long-term solutions
