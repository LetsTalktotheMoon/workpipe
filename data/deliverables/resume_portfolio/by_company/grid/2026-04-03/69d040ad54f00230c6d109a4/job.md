# Software Engineer | On-site | San Francisco

**Company:** Grid
**Location:** San Francisco, California
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Grid is a fast-growing company focused on leveling the financial playing field by building innovative financial products. They are seeking a Software Engineer to develop data pipelines for their AI-powered financial systems and maintain the backend logic driving their AI agent.

## Core Skills
- Large language models
- Prompt engineering
- Backend development
- Data processing
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Degree in Computer Science
- Hands-on experience with large language models (GPT
- Claude, etc.)
- Strong technical writing and analytical skills with attention to precision and systematic testing methodologies
- Detail-oriented approach to documentation, performance measurement, and systematic experimentation in a fast-paced startup environment
- Ability to work out of our San Francisco office

## Preferred qualifications
- Candidates who can start immediately

## Responsibilities
- Write backend features end to end — data models, business logic, and API integrations that make our product work reliably for customers
- Design, version, and systematically test prompts that drive our backend AI agent's financial reasoning and decision-making — measuring accuracy, consistency, and reliability across diverse inputs
- Build and maintain production tools that process financial data at scale
- Use SQL and our analytics infrastructure to evaluate prompt performance, scraper reliability, and system behavior
- Own your implementations end to end, participate in our CI/CD process, and respond to production incidents as needed
