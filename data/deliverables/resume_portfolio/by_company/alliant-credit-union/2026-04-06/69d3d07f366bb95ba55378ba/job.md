# Senior Business Intelligence Analyst

**Company:** Alliant Credit Union
**Location:** Chicago, IL
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Alliant Credit Union is seeking a Senior Business Intelligence Analyst to perform data analysis that drives actionable insights supporting business strategies. The role involves creating reports, developing dashboards, and collaborating with various departments to ensure data quality and support decision-making.

## Core Skills
- Data Analysis
- Statistical Methods
- Data Modeling
- Descriptive Analytics
- Business Intelligence Tools

## Minimum qualifications
- Graduate Degree in Business
- Business Analytics
- Data Science
- Statistics
- Mathematics
- Computer Science or Related
- 1 Year Business Intelligence
- Data Analysis or Related
- 3 Years Business Intelligence
- Data Analysis including a Bachelor's Degree or 7 years without a degree

## Responsibilities
- Perform complex data analyses to uncover insights, trends, and patterns that drive business strategy, utilizing statistical methods, data modeling, and descriptive analytics to support decision-making
- Design, develop, and maintain interactive dashboards and reports using business intelligence tools such a Tableau
- Power BI, or similar platform
- Ensure reports are comprehensive, accurate, and aligned with business needs
- Work with multiple data sources to integrate and consolidate data into a unified view
- Assist in the development of data pipelines and ETL processes to ensure data availability and accuracy
- Create and refine data visualizations to effectively communicate insights and support business objectives, applying best practices in data visualization to enhance clarity and impact
- Collaborate with business units and stakeholders to understand their data needs and provide actionable insights
- Translate complex data findings into clear and actionable recommendations
- Monitor and ensure data quality, accuracy, and consistency
- Implement and adhere to data governance practices to maintain standards of data integrity
