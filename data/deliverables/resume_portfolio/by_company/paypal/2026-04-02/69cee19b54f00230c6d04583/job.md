# Software Engineer - SDET

**Company:** PayPal
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
PayPal has been revolutionizing commerce globally for more than 25 years. The role involves implementing tasks within the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC), collaborating with peers, and contributing to code and design reviews.

## Core Skills
- Mobile Test Automation
- Android, iOS
- Appium
- Espresso

## Minimum qualifications
- 1+ years relevant experience and a Bachelor's degree OR Any equivalent combination of education and experience

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with mobile test automation for Android and iOS platforms
- Hands-on knowledge of testing frameworks such as Appium
- Espresso, and XCTest
- Programming experience in Python
- NodeJS
- Kotlin, or Swift
- Familiarity with CI/CD tools such as Jenkins
- GitHub Actions, or similar
- Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to clearly communicate findings and collaborate with cross-functional teams

## Responsibilities
- Implements tasks within the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC), receiving structure and oversight from more experienced staff
- Follows well-established internal conventions and standard procedures
- Understands internal standards & processes an applies them to make technical decisions
- Collaborates with peers, manager, and project lead to gain understanding of tasks and review solutions
- May contribute to code & design reviews
