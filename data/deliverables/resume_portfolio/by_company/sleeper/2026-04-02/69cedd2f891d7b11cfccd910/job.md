# Data Scientist- Platform Integrity

**Company:** Sleeper
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Sleeper is a sports-focused games platform driven by a passion for sports and innovation. They are seeking a Data Scientist to enhance user experience and platform integrity by analyzing player behavior, detecting fraud, and collaborating with cross-functional teams to improve onboarding and user journeys.

## Core Skills
- Data Science
- Economics
- Machine Learning Engineering
- Python
- R

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of industry experience in data science, economics, analytics, or machine learning engineering
- Advanced Degree in computer science, statistics, or a related field
- Proficiency in scripting languages (Python
- R, or similar) and big data query/processing languages and tools such as SQL
- Hive
- Spark, and Airflow
- Experience building or working with event-based analytics systems
- Familiarity with machine learning methods for classification, clustering, or anomaly detection
- Ability to work cross-functionally with engineering and product

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience working in Fraud/Risk/Trust & Safety teams
- Experience developing internal tooling for analytics, operations, or user lifecycle workflows (e.g., onboarding, review systems, or risk signals), using platforms like Retool
- Appsmith, or comparable frameworks
- Experience working in real money gaming or other high-velocity transaction environments
- Comfortable writing light JavaScript in data/UI workflows

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate with product leaders to optimize our onboarding funnel to identify friction and areas for improvement
- Build scalable analytics systems for continuous evaluation of user journeys and defining key metrics
- Partner with engineering to implement real-time event tracking (Firebase
- Amplitude etc)
- Define schemas and instrumentation for user activity (account creation, sessions, device/IP data etc)
- Collaborate on systems that balance user friction with platform integrity
- Build analytical frameworks to surface signals that inform adaptive user flows (dynamic verification requirements etc)
- Establish prevalence measurement methodologies to quantify fraudulent activities on the platform
- Partner with our operations teams to triage and mitigate any ongoing threats to the platform
- Classify transaction risk (payment method, account-level signals)
- Apply clustering, anomaly detection etc to large scale behavioral data
