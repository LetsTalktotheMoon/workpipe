# Salesforce Developer

**Company:** SS&C Technologies
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
SS&C Technologies is a leading financial services and healthcare technology company headquartered in Windsor, Connecticut. They are seeking a Salesforce Developer to design, develop, and maintain custom Salesforce applications while collaborating with stakeholders to deliver scalable solutions.

## Core Skills
- Salesforce development
- Apex
- Visualforce
- Lightning Web Components
- JavaScript

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Information Technology, or a related field
- 3+ years of hands-on experience as a Salesforce Developer
- Proven expertise in Apex
- Visualforce
- Lightning Web Components (LWC), and JavaScript
- Strong understanding of Salesforce platform architecture, data model, and security model
- Experience with Salesforce APIs (REST
- SOAP
- Metadata API) and integrating Salesforce with other systems
- Proficiency in SOQL and SOSL
- Familiarity with Salesforce development best practices, including version control (e.g.
- Git) and CI/CD pipelines
- Salesforce Platform Developer I certification is required
- Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and communication skills
- Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced environment

## Preferred qualifications
- Salesforce Platform Developer II certification is a plus
- Experience with Agile development methodologies is preferred
- Experience with CPQ is preferred

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain custom Salesforce applications
- Apex classes
- Visualforce pages
- Platform Events
- Lightning Components, and triggers
- Implement and configure Salesforce features, including custom objects, fields, flows and validation rules
- Develop and maintain integrations between Salesforce and other internal and external systems using APIs, web services, and middleware
- Perform data migration, data quality analysis, and data cleansing activities
- Collaborate with business analysts and stakeholders to gather requirements, translate them into technical specifications, and propose effective Salesforce solutions
- Write and maintain technical documentation, including design specifications, test plans, and release notes
- Conduct unit testing, integration testing, and support user acceptance testing (UAT)
- Provide ongoing support, troubleshooting, and maintenance for existing Salesforce applications
- Stay up-to-date with new Salesforce releases, features, and best practices
- Participate in code reviews to ensure adherence to development standards and best practices
