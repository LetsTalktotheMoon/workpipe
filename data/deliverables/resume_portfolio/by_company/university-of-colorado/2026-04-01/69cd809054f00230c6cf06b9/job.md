# Data Scientist

**Company:** University of Colorado
**Location:** Aurora, CO
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
University of Colorado Anschutz Medical Campus is seeking a Data Scientist to join the Language, Reasoning and Knowledge Lab (LARK). This role involves developing advanced AI solutions to improve medical diagnostics and patient care documentation through innovative natural language processing techniques.

## Core Skills
- Natural language processing
- Artificial intelligence
- Python
- NLP frameworks - Transformers
- NLP frameworks - SpaCy

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science, management/computer information systems, computer engineering, information technology, or a directly related field from an accredited institution
- 1 years of related natural language processing and artificial intelligence experience
- An advanced degree (Masters or PhD) may be substituted for experience on a year-for-year basis if the degree is in a field of study directly related to the work assignment

## Preferred qualifications
- Masters Degree in Computational Linguistics
- Analytics
- Search, and Informatics
- Documented opensource contributions
- Experience with NLP/deep learning frameworks such as pytorch and tensorflow
- Experience with NLP state-of-the-art models and agentic workflow (Llama
- Claude
- LangGraph, etc)
- Experience with training models on GPUs
- Experience working with Unix and/or Linux, including shell scripting
- Experience implementing NLP/AI algorithms and models for real-world applications

## Responsibilities
- Conduct comprehensive literature reviews to identify existing solutions and their limitations, and develop advanced NLP algorithms for multimodal data
- Design and conduct experiments to validate hypotheses using applied statistics, clean and preprocess data for model training, and debug code to analyze complex datasets
- Develop reproducible research pipelines and maintain thorough documentation on GitHub
- Assist the Principal Investigator (PI) with grant writing and the development of research proposals
- Present research findings and progress at lab and collaborative meetings, also prepare and submit manuscripts and abstracts for publication in scientific journals and conferences
- Support lab members and collaborators with research tasks and manage lab logistics, including the lab website and server infrastructure
- Adhere to responsible conduct of research guidelines, participate in ethics training sessions, and monitor compliance with research ethics
