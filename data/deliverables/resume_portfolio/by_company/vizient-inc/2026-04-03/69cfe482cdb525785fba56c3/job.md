# Data Scientist

**Company:** Vizient, Inc
**Location:** Chicago, IL 60607
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Vizient, Inc is committed to creating an inclusive environment for its employees. In this role, you will develop and deliver analytic solutions that support client decision-making and drive improvements in clinical, operational, and economic outcomes.

## Core Skills
- Python
- SAS
- SQL
- Healthcare data
- Tableau

## Minimum qualifications
- 2 or more years of relevant experience required
- Experience with Python
- SAS, and SQL required
- Experience working with healthcare data required
- Knowledge of data modeling, statistical analysis, and machine learning techniques
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Effective written and verbal communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Relevant degree preferred
- Experience with Tableau
- Apache Spark, and Azure Databricks preferred

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate with product and development teams to deliver data analytics capabilities
- Develop data science insights across single and multiple domains
- Communicate analytical findings to clinical and administrative client audiences
- Apply data assessment techniques including discovery, cleaning, validation, and enrichment
- Build and implement machine learning and statistical models
- Support data modeling, exploratory analysis, and visualization development
- Utilize programming tools including Python
- SAS, and SQL
- Leverage data platforms such as Azure Databricks and Apache Spark
- Troubleshoot and optimize complex datasets from multiple sources
- Produce recurring reports and support analytics deliverables
