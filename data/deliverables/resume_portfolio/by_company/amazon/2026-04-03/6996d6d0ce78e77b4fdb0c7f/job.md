# Software Dev Engineer II, Conversational Ad Experiences

**Company:** Amazon
**Location:** Palo Alto, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon is re-imagining the advertising landscape through generative AI technologies, revolutionizing how customers discover products and engage with brands. The role involves developing conversational ad experiences powered by large language models, driving product and engineering initiatives, and building large-scale systems that impact millions of shoppers worldwide.

## Core Skills
- Generative AI
- Large Language Models
- Software engineering
- Software programming languages
- Software design patterns

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- Experience programming with at least one software programming language
- Passionate about Generative AI
- Large Language Models, and their real-world applications
- Strong software engineering skills
- Creative problem-solver who thrives in ambiguity and loves tackling complex challenges
- Track record of delivering innovative solutions that drive business impact
- Enthusiasm for staying current with the rapidly evolving AI landscape

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- Design and develop net new conversational ad experiences on the Search page
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate AI-driven solutions across our advertising ecosystem
- Pioneer new approaches to conversational AI and natural language interactions in advertising
- Shape the future of how businesses connect with customers through advertising
- Create scalable systems to deliver explicitly personalized ad experiences
