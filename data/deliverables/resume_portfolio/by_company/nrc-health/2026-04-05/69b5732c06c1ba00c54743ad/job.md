# AI Platform Engineer

**Company:** NRC Health
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
NRC Health is dedicated to bringing Human Understanding to healthcare. As an AI Platform Engineer, you will build, stabilize, and evolve the production AI platform, collaborating with product engineering teams to implement AI capabilities effectively and responsibly.

## Core Skills
- Software engineering
- LLM-based systems
- Model lifecycle management
- Production system reliability
- Operational excellence

## Minimum qualifications
- 4 + years of software engineering experience building and operating production systems
- Hands-on experience developing or supporting LLM-based systems (e.g.
- RAG pipelines, orchestration layers, inference services)
- Familiarity with model lifecycle concepts, including evaluation, deployment, monitoring, and regression testing
- Experience building scalable backend or platform systems
- Experience working with AWS ecosystem
- Comfort working in collaborative, cross-functional engineering environments
- Strong problem-solving skills with a focus on production reliability and operational excellence

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience working in regulated or high-trust environments
- Experience with modern data platforms (e.g.
- Snowflake)
- Experience contributing to shared internal platforms or developer tooling

## Responsibilities
- Help build, stabilize, and evolve NRC Health’s production AI platform under the leadership of the Senior AI Platform Lead
- Play a hands-on role in transitioning AI capabilities from an external vendor to full internal ownership
- Partner with product engineering teams to enable safe, scalable, and governed AI-powered features across NRC Health’s portfolio
- Contribute directly to LLM-based application systems, platform reliability, and responsible AI implementation, ensuring our AI solutions remain reliable, observable, and ready to scale
