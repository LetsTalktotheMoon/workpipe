# (Senior) Corporate Data Analyst

**Company:** Origami Risk
**Location:** Atlanta, GA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Origami Risk is a company that provides single-platform SaaS solutions for risk management. They are seeking a (Senior) Corporate Data Analyst who will collaborate with stakeholders to develop a new internal data warehouse, manage data analysis, and present insights in an accessible format.

## Core Skills
- Data Analytics
- Dashboard Building
- Python
- SQL
- Data Pipelines

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree or equivalent experience in a Computer Science
- Data Analysis or business-related field
- Corporate Data Analyst: 1+ years' experience working with and providing data analytics, reporting and dashboard building for company consumption
- Senior Corporate Data Analyst: 3+ years' experience working with and providing analytics and reporting to leadership including executive level
- Technical experience using data manipulation tools and development languages, including Python and SQL
- Superior communication skills
- Strong project ownership and attention to detail skills with the ability to be a self-starter
- Knowledge of and experience with data pipelines and ETL processes
- Prior experience working with data engineers and understanding of technical requirements

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Databricks
- Thoughtspot, and other data lake / warehouse and analytics tools

## Responsibilities
- Develops, manages, and optimizes BI solutions, including data models, dashboards, and reports
- Acts as owner of company wide data warehouse in regard to maintenance, availability of systems data, and coordination of data integration engineers when needed
- Works with stakeholders, including cross departments, to gather and document requirements for BI projects, enhancements, and reporting
- Utilizes data visualization, statistical analysis, and reporting skills to present data to stakeholders in a meaningful way
- Uses data analysis techniques to extract information and identify patterns or trends
- Builds reports and reconcile to system data, on both a regular cadence and ad-hoc requests
- Develops and maintains documentation of data sources, models, and processes
- Provides training and support to end-users on BI tools and reports
- Stays current with BI industry trends and best practices
- Applies basic development skills working with Python
- SQL Queries, etc. to extract business insights around finance, business operations and more
