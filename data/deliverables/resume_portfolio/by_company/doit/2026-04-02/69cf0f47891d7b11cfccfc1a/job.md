# Applied AI Engineer

**Company:** DoiT
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
DoiT is a global technology company that works with cloud-driven organizations to leverage the cloud to drive business growth and innovation. The Applied AI Engineer will be responsible for embedding AI-driven workflows into core business functions, focusing on automating processes and improving efficiency through modern AI tooling.

## Core Skills
- AI coding assistants
- Claude Code
- Gemini CLI
- Codex
- LLM APIs

## Minimum qualifications
- The Solver Mindset: You can research a problem, design a solution, code it, and launch it. You use AI coding assistants heavily to multiply your own output
- The AI-Native Builder: You don't just know about AI. You build with it daily. You instinctively reach for tools like Claude Code
- Gemini CLI and Codex to multiply your output. You are fluent in modern AI coding environments and autonomous agents like Claude Code
- Strong Software Fundamentals: You produce production-quality code, not just brittle scripts
- AI Integration Experience: Hands-on experience with LLM APIs, prompt engineering
- RAG
- MCP, and agent-based architectures
- Extreme Empathy: Translating messy, real-world business workflows into clean technical solutions requires deep listening and communication skills. You can speak 'Engineering' and business language equally well
- Self-Direction: There is no established playbook for this role. You will need to find the work, scope it, build it, and prove it mattered

## Responsibilities
- Embed & Discover: Audit real workflows in your assigned department. Find the 'Human API' moments where smart people are doing robotic work (copy-pasting, manually reconciling data)
- Provide the 'Golden Start': Build default, pre-configured AI setups for your department teammates. Don't make an accountant figure out how to connect an LLM to NetSuite
- build the integration, secure the endpoints, and hand them the keys
- Ship Foundational Wins: Build and deploy initial AI-powered automations using DoiT's standard stack (Next.js
- TypeScript
- Firestore
- Cloud Run, and Claude API) to build trust and show what's possible
- Educate & Enable: Train your department teammates on the tools you build. Foster their growth so they can start writing their own prompts, configuring their own agents, and automating their own workflows
- Measure Outcomes: Track the time and cost savings for every automation shipped in your department. No vanity metrics, just real business outcomes
