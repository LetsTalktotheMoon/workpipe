# Associate, Developer

**Company:** Lone Pine Capital LLC
**Location:** Greenwich, CT
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Lone Pine Capital LLC is a private investment management company with over 25 years of experience. They are seeking an Associate, Developer to automate and streamline operations, build and maintain tools, and troubleshoot issues in various environments.

## Core Skills
- API integration
- Python scripting
- SQL scripting
- PowerShell scripting
- Microsoft Excel

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Information Technology, or a related field, or equivalent practical experience
- A minimum of four years of experience as an Associate Developer or in a similar role
- Background working with vendor-provided API's
- Scripting skills in languages such as Python
- SQL, or PowerShell
- Knowledge in Microsoft Excel and VBA
- Working knowledge of AI tools and platforms, with the ability to apply them effectively in day-to-day workflows
- Demonstrates a general understanding or curiosity about financial markets, stocks and broader business trends
- Strong problem-solving and troubleshooting skills
- Excellent communication and collaboration abilities
- Self-motivated and eager to learn

## Preferred qualifications
- Proficiency with automation tools
- Familiarity with containerization and orchestration tools
- Experience with monitoring and logging tools (e.g.
- Prometheus
- Grafana
- OpenTelemetry)
- Background working with Redis and/or Hashi Corp Vault
- Comfortable working across Windows and Linux environments

## Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain automation scripts and tools to manage infrastructure
- Design, implement, and manage continuous integration/continuous deployment pipelines to enable rapid software development and deployment
- Set up and maintain monitoring and logging solutions to ensure system performance, reliability, and availability using tools
- Collaborate effectively across teams to streamline repetitive tasks
- Implement and manage security best practices, including vulnerability assessments, patch management, and incident response
- Troubleshoot and resolve issues in development, test, and production environments
- Analyze system performance and identify bottlenecks
- recommend and implement performance improvements
