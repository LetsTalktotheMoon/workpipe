Sony Interactive Entertainment is a global leader in entertainment producing the PlayStation family of products and services. They are seeking a Software Systems Engineer II to implement data quality and validation measures, ensuring the accuracy and integrity of data stored in Snowflake.

## Core Skills

- Information Systems or related field or equivalent, three (3) years of experience developing high scale and distributed systems, implementing functions, stored procedures, complex SQL, and performance tuning, conducting data mining and extract to transform and load data using databases with large-scale and complex datasets, assessing queries and visualizations for ad-hoc requests and ongoing reporting, optimizing ongoing processes to reduce data processing time and monthly expenditures, convert full data load to incremental, troubleshooting systems to reconcile revenues, taxes, and product profitability in the data-warehouse against heterogeneous source systems, utilizing MySQL and Oracle databases
