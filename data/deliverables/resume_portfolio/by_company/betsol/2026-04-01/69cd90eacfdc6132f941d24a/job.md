# Associate – AI & Automation (Early Career / New Graduate)

**Company:** BETSOL
**Location:** Broomfield, CO
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
BETSOL is a cloud-first digital transformation and data management company offering innovative products and IT services. They are seeking an early-career professional to support AI-driven automation initiatives, utilizing AI tools to analyze workflows and identify opportunities for efficiency and cost reduction.

## Core Skills
- AI tools usage
- Automation concepts
- Process analysis
- Applied AI
- Scripting

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree (or final‑year student) in Engineering
- Computer Science
- Information Systems
- Data Science
- Business Analytics, or a related field
- Strong interest in AI, automation, and applied problem solving
- Comfort using AI tools as part of real work (not just academic exposure)
- Analytical mindset with the ability to break down processes and identify inefficiencies
- Clear written and verbal communication skills

## Preferred qualifications
- Hands‑on exposure to Claude
- AI copilots, generative AI tools, or automation platforms
- Basic understanding of automation concepts (workflows
- APIs, scripting
- RPA, low‑code tools)
- Familiarity with data analysis, reporting, or systems integration
- Basic programming or scripting knowledge (Python
- JavaScript
- SQL, etc.)
- Academic, internship, or personal projects involving AI, automation, or process improvement

## Responsibilities
- Actively use AI tools such as Claude
- Co‑worker
- OpenClaw, and other AI platforms as part of day‑to‑day work
- Apply AI to support analysis, documentation, workflow design, decision support, and solution development
- Develop a strong understanding of where AI adds value vs. where traditional approaches are more appropriate
- Analyze workflows and processes to identify automation, simplification, and cost‑reduction opportunities
- Support the design and implementation of AI‑enabled or automated solutions that reduce manual effort and improve turnaround time
- Help assess and articulate the impact of automation (effort saved, cycle time reduced, complexity eliminated)
- Contribute to internal AI and automation programs that improve how BETSOL operates
- Support external client projects by assisting with AI‑enabled analysis, solution prototyping, and implementation
- Work alongside senior engineers, consultants, and leaders on real delivery initiatives—not just experiments
- Stay current on emerging trends in generative AI, copilots, agents, and automation platforms
