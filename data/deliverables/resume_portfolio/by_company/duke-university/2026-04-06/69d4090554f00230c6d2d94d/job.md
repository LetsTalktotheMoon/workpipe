# Developer - AI & Data Integrations

**Company:** Duke University
**Location:** Durham, NC, US, 27710
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite

Duke University is committed to compassionate care and innovation in healthcare technology. They are seeking a Developer for AI & Data Integrations to contribute to the research, development, and delivery of emerging technologies, while supporting IT and automation best practices.

## Core Skills

- Debugging
- Software development

- Bachelor's degree in Software Engineering or related field OR 2 years relevant work experience for Level I
- Bachelor's degree OR 3-5 years relevant work experience for Level II
- Bachelor's or Master's degree OR 5-7 years relevant work experience for Level III
- Experience in software development through internships, coursework, or entry-level roles for Level I
- 3-5 years of experience in software development, with exposure to emerging technologies for Level II
- 5-7 years development or architecture experience
- 2-3 years designing and delivering automation, innovation and AI-driven efforts for Level III
- Foundational programming and debugging skills for Level I
- Basic collaboration skills within a team for Level I
- Eagerness to learn innovation processes for Level I
- Good communication skills for Level I

## Preferred qualifications
- API Development
- AI expertise
- Health sciences experience (clinical or research)

- Contribute to development tasks under guidance from senior developers
- Assist in testing, documenting, and deploying new automation and innovation solutions

- ML
- Automation)
