# Software Engineer, Data and APIs, Quantum AI

**Company:** Google
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Google is a leading technology company, and they are seeking a Software Engineer for their Quantum AI team. In this role, you will design and build foundational APIs and data layers for quantum software products, collaborating with various teams to advance the capabilities of quantum computing.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Rust
- C++
- Software Design
- Software Architecture

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent practical experience
- 3 years of software development experience or 1 year with an advanced degree
- 2 years of experience programming in Rust or C++
- 1 years of experience with software design and architecture

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Kubernetes and Google Distributed Cloud
- Experience designing data processing pipelines or job execution systems
- Experience building distributed services and resource-oriented APIs
- Familiarity or education in quantum computing with enthusiasm to learn more

## Responsibilities
- Analyze problems and recommend solutions for both short and long-term needs, taking ownership and roadmapping to influence other engineers, and implementing solutions or delegating to colleagues
- Deliver outcomes as a key contributor by designing and implementing multi-quarter projects without supervision, consistently generating the ideas required to solve ambiguous problems
- Write and review code to ensure best practices are met, contributing to data preparation, optimization, and performance enhancements confirming all aspects of technical work are high-quality
- Incorporate feedback on designs to determine when to enhance existing systems or build new systems, and contribute to existing documentation or educational content
- Partner with research groups and collaborate with teams of different backgrounds, including hardware engineers, electronic engineers, or research scientists
