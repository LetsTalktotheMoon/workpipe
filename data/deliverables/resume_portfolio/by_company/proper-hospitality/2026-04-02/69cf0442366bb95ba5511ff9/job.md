# Founding Full Stack Engineer - AI Focused

**Company:** Proper Hospitality
**Location:** Los Angeles, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Proper Hospitality is seeking a Founding Full-Stack Engineer (AI-focused) to build the production systems behind their hospitality OS. The role involves designing and implementing backend systems, data pipelines, and orchestration to enhance operational efficiency in hospitality.

## Core Skills
- Full stack development
- Backend development
- API design
- Data modeling
- Distributed systems

## Minimum qualifications
- 3–6 years of engineering experience building and operating production systems
- Strong backend fundamentals. APIs, data modeling, distributed systems basics, and pragmatic infrastructure
- Experience building data pipelines in messy real-world environments
- Experience integrating with legacy systems and uncooperative third parties
- Practical understanding of AI systems. How models fail, how tool-use breaks, and how to design around brittleness
- Strong engineering judgment. You can explain tradeoffs, keep scope tight, and choose simple approaches that scale

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience operating systems with multi-tenant requirements
- Experience with evaluation frameworks for AI/agent workflows
- Experience building internal platforms or shared primitives that multiple teams build on
- Hospitality, travel, or other 'messy ops' domain experience

## Responsibilities
- Build backend systems that power agent workflows in production
- Orchestrate jobs, manage state, design idempotency, and handle retries
- Design and scale the data layer. Pipelines, schemas, quality checks, and freshness from legacy property systems through to live inference
- Take on technically challenging architecture. Multi-tenant design, integration abstractions, permissions models, and robust failure handling
- Own quality end to end. Define evals, catch regressions, and raise the bar under deadline pressure
- Ship v0s fast without creating future traps. Know which decisions are reversible and which foundations must be correct
