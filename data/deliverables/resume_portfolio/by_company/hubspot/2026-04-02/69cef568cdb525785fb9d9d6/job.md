# Senior Software Engineer II

**Company:** HubSpot
**Location:** Cambridge, MA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
HubSpot is an AI-powered customer platform that connects marketing, sales, and service. The Senior Software Engineer II role focuses on individual contributors who demonstrate technical excellence, leadership, and the ability to design scalable solutions while mentoring team members.

## Core Skills
- Technical Expertise
- System Design
- Scalable Software Architecture
- Cross-Platform Development
- Code Review

## Minimum qualifications
- Demonstrated technical excellence and leadership on their team
- Ability to quickly adapt to understand complex issues
- Design elegant, scalable solutions
- Balance short-term and long-term needs
- Serve as one of the team's/group's technical experts
- Strong, autonomous problem solver
- Lead a team's complex projects with many moving parts
- 3-5 years of experience as a Sr SWE I or similar
- Point out long-standing technical problems without prompting
- Propose solutions and advocate for their resolution

## Preferred qualifications
- Expand focus into other problem areas and work on multiple teams
- Master additional technical platforms
- Take on broader system design work
- Own larger areas of the product
- Dig into framework-level technical architecture and design
- Work closely with TL
- PM, and PD to guide team direction
- Serve as the cross-team technical leader for big projects
- Demonstrate early signs of leadership for promotion to Tech Lead II
- Proactively take ownership of projects
- Communicate effectively with TL, manager, and peers

## Responsibilities
- Lead a team's complex projects with many moving parts (technologies, teams)
- Take on the team's most challenging problems with minimal guidance
- Point out long-standing technical problems without prompting, propose solutions, and advocate passionately for their resolution
- Help set team direction and priorities by partnering with their TL
- PM, and other engineers
- Step in as TL for an interim period (e.g. during a transition, vacation, sabbatical, or parental leave)
- Embrace a growth mindset and consistently get deeper or broader technical skills
- Provide experienced guidance and feedback on technical designs, decisions, and projects
- Demonstrate consistent multiplicative impact through presentations and creating core reusable libraries/services
- Embrace and promote quality in engineering best practices
