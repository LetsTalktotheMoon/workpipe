# Data Scientist II

**Company:** Seismic
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Seismic is the global leader in AI-powered enablement, empowering go-to-market leaders to drive strategic growth and deliver exceptional customer experiences. As a Data Scientist II, you will design, build, and ship AI-powered product features, working closely with product managers, engineers, and other data scientists to develop end-to-end AI solutions that provide real customer value.

## Core Skills
- Python
- LangChain, pandas
- Generative AI
- Machine Learning

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's
- Master's, or higher degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or related fields
- 2–4 years of industry experience in data science, machine learning, or applied AI (or 1–2 years with an advanced degree), with experience contributing to production systems or product features
- Strong knowledge of Python is a must
- Ideally comfortable with LangChain, pandas and other GenAI related python libraries
- Strong hands-on skills in AI & ML service development, end-to-end product engineering, and data analytics
- Experience with GenAI and cloud technologies
- Ability to translate business and customer problems into technical solutions, and to iterate based on user feedback and data
- Exceptional communication skills, with the ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams
- Strong problem-solving and decision-making abilities, with a strategic mindset and a passion for driving product vision and business outcomes through technology
- Experience thriving in dynamic, fast-paced environments, preferably within SaaS or technology-driven companies focused on customer and product innovation

## Responsibilities
- Contribute to the development of AI-powered product features, from prototyping and experimentation through production delivery, deployment and iteration
- Build and improve AI/machine learning, especially LLM-based systems (e.g., copilots, conversational workflows, or agent-style experiences) using established frameworks and internal platforms
- Work with product and design teams to understand user needs and translate them into practical AI-driven solutions
- Partner with engineers, designers, and other data scientists to build, test, and refine AI features, balancing model performance, system reliability, and user experience
- Help define and analyze metrics to evaluate model performance, product usage, and customer impact, and use data to guide improvements
