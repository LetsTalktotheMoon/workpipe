# Platform Engineer

**Company:** Five9
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Five9 is a leading provider of cloud contact center software, committed to enhancing customer experiences. They are seeking a Platform Engineer to join their Cloud Operations team, focusing on building and maintaining scalable cloud infrastructure and developer tooling.

## Core Skills
- Software Development Life Cycle
- Agile
- Scrum
- Public Cloud
- Google Cloud Platform

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of Software Development Life Cycle experience, preferably in Agile/Scrum environments
- 3+ years of public cloud experience (GCP or AWS)
- 3+ years of systems and networking experience
- Strong proficiency with Kubernetes
- Helm
- GKE, and Docker
- Experience designing and operating large-scale distributed systems
- Proficiency in Python and/or other scripting languages
- Experience with Go (Golang) is a strong plus
- Infrastructure as Code experience with Terraform
- Experience with Incident Management processes and SRE best practices
- Demonstrated ability to independently scope, prioritize, and execute complex infrastructure projects with minimal oversight

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with SIP telephony (Freeswitch
- Asterisk
- Kamailio
- WebRTC) is a plus
- Experience with ArgoCD and GitOps workflows is essential
- Experience with CI/CD tooling including GitLab
- Jenkins
- Spinnaker, or similar
- Familiarity with config management tools such as Ansible
- Puppet, or Chef

## Responsibilities
- Design, build, and maintain public and private cloud infrastructure with a focus on reliability, scalability, and developer productivity
- Build and maintain internal developer environment tooling, primarily in Go and Python, to accelerate engineering workflows and reduce friction across the SDLC
- Drive automation initiatives that improve availability, reduce toil, and increase operational efficiency across Five9’s cloud platform
- Collaborate with Engineering teams to deliver high-quality software in a fast-paced environment, contributing technical depth to architecture and implementation decisions
- Monitor production and development environments, proactively identifying and resolving issues before they impact customers
- Contribute to incident response
- SRE practices, and continuous improvement efforts to lower MTTD and MTTR
- Help define and evolve the platform engineering roadmap as Five9 scales its infrastructure for the next phase of growth
