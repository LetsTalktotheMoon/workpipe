# Information Management - College Co-Op

**Company:** Selective Insurance
**Location:** Charlotte, NC
**Seniority:** New Grad
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Selective Insurance is a midsized U.S. domestic property and casualty insurance company with a history of strong financial performance. They are seeking a motivated college student for a co-op position in their Information Management department, where the student will contribute to projects that enhance customer service and data security.

## Core Skills
- Object-oriented programming
- Python
- C#
- Java
- Data analytics

## Minimum qualifications
- Must be a current college student majoring in Computer Science or a related field to qualify (rising seniors, preferred)
- Must be enrolled in, or plan to enroll in, an accredited internship or co-operative education course during the fall 2026 semester
- Basic to intermediate experience with object-oriented programming languages such as Python
- C#
- Java, or similar
- Strong analytical, critical-thinking, and problem-solving skills, with an interest in applying technology to real-world business challenges
- Demonstrated interest in Data
- Analytics, and AI, through relevant coursework, academic projects, or hands-on experience (e.g., data analytics, machine learning, or AI concepts/tools)
- Demonstrate an interest in learning about the insurance industry
- Currently enrolled in a college/university pursuing a bachelor's degree in computer science or related field

## Responsibilities
- Develop, enhance, and support Data and Analytics solutions
- Provide production support for the solutions as needed
- Research new technology capabilities under the direction from existing team members
- Ensure adherence to IT standards and best practices
- Collaborate effectively with team members and communicate clearly to support business processes and resolve issues
