# AI-Enabled Software Engineer

**Company:** ALTEN Technology USA
**Location:** Raymond, OH
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
ALTEN Technology USA is an engineering company helping clients bring groundbreaking ideas to life. The AI-Enabled Software Engineer will focus on developing and delivering production-ready AI solutions, collaborating with cross-functional teams and integrating AI tools into operational workflows.

## Core Skills
- Applied AI
- Software Engineering
- Python
- AI tools integration
- AI libraries

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Software Engineering, or related field
- 2–4 years of experience in software engineering or applied AI
- Proficiency in Python for production-level code
- Experience integrating AI tools, libraries, or model APIs
- Strong communication skills to work effectively with teams
- Ability to work onsite full-time
- Familiarity with structured development environments

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with AWS services (EC2
- Lambda
- S3
- Bedrock)
- Exposure to Docker
- CI/CD
- MLOps
- Familiarity with enterprise data pipelines or responsible AI practices
- Experience with ETL
- PySpark, or similar data engineering tools

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain Python-based AI tools, scripts, and automation solutions
- Integrate AI libraries, model APIs, or existing AI services into operational workflows
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand requirements and deliver reliable solutions
- Participate in code reviews
- CI/CD pipelines, and MLOps practices for production deployment
- Document processes, architecture, and AI implementations for long-term maintainability
- Monitor and troubleshoot deployed AI solutions to ensure quality and performance
