# Software Development Engineer II, Specialized Selection

**Company:** Amazon
**Location:** Bellevue, WA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon is a leading technology company focused on optimizing its supply chain. They are seeking a Software Development Engineer II to design and build scalable systems that enhance Amazon's transportation network, utilizing innovative technologies and methodologies.

## Core Skills
- Software development
- Software design, architecture
- Design patterns, scaling

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- Experience programming with at least one software programming language

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- Experiment with whatever technology we need to get the job done
- Use Test-Driven Development and Continuous Deployment
- Work with senior engineers and leadership across multiple technical teams and business stakeholders to design and build highly available and scalable systems for optimizing and managing Amazon’s transportation network
- Expand and optimize our world-class supply chain systems to support a variety of emerging business initiatives
