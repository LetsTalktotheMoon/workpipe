# Sr. Reporting and Data Analyst, Operations and Innovation

**Company:** DeVry University
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
DeVry University is committed to closing society’s opportunity gap by preparing learners for careers shaped by technological change. The Sr. Reporting and Data Analyst will work collaboratively to support system and business processes, develop standardized reports, and deliver key findings to the organization.

## Core Skills
- Data Analysis
- Business Process Support
- CRM Systems
- Telephony Systems
- Workforce Management Systems

## Minimum qualifications
- 4-6 years' experience reporting on data and working in an analytical capacity
- Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience required
- Experience working with CRM
- Telephony
- Workforce Management
- SIS systems processes and/or call center technologies or similar applications
- Power BI DAX programming/Tableau (required)
- SQL (required)
- Ability to deliver insights and be able to present data findings using PowerPoint
- Experience forecasting and modeling data and presenting to leadership
- Capacity to learn technical systems and environments, interested in developing their technical skills
- Detail oriented with the ability to work independently, problem solve and drive to solutions
- Ability to work in a fast-paced work environment and excel with little guidance and supervision

## Preferred qualifications
- R or Python (strongly preferred)
- Advanced Analytics/Statistical Analysis (preferred not required, though must have demonstrated math background due to role)
- Experience using Salesforce CRM preferred
- Experience with project and/or change management a plus
- Some experience with coding and basic programming languages for advanced analytical tools such as Cognos or Crystal Reports is a plus

## Responsibilities
- Providing insights on the student Journey including the driving indicator of change within reporting. This includes the “what happened, why, and what we are doing about it.”
- Analyzing various data sources and pulling information from different vendor systems to make recommendations and identify trends
- Creating Power BI automated dashboards to review weekly/monthly activity of lead volume and make recommendations to the marketing management team
- Ability to tell a story using visualizations /reporting tools and PowerPoint presentations
- Meet with subject matter experts in different areas of the business, be able to quickly learn the concepts and language relevant to their line of business and develop an understanding of how their data supports their business processes
- Understands how student data is structured in the systems, and review, interpret, troubleshoot, and optimize SQL queries. Develop maintainable code via proper structures, comments, and design using best practices
- Ability to segment data based on criteria (market, program, audience, life cycle stage, etc.) to best target a specific market or user type and identifies opportunities within those segments
- Executes Ad-hoc reporting requirements and provides valuable insights with minimal oversight and direction
