# Software Development Engineer—Backend

**Company:** Siemens Energy
**Location:** Orlando, FL
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Siemens Energy is an energy technology company with a commitment to developing the energy systems of the future. As a Backend Software Developer, you will build and maintain software applications that support core business processes, ensuring quality, efficiency, and scalability of the solutions developed.

## Core Skills
- C#
- DotNet platform
- Distributed cloud applications
- AWS
- Relational databases

## Minimum qualifications
- A degree in Computer Science
- Software Engineering, or a related technical field
- 3+ years of professional experience in software development
- Knowledge and experience with C# and the .Net platform
- Experience with development of distributed cloud applications, with AWS experience preferred
- Understanding of relational databases and SQL
- Basic knowledge of machine-learning application flow
- Basic knowledge of Queuing technologies such as RabbitMQ
- Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot and optimize code

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Python and machine-learning related python libraries
- Experience with Entity Framework
- A passion for learning new technologies and improving software development practices

## Responsibilities
- Building and maintaining software applications that support the core business processes
- Designing, developing, and implementing software solutions that are efficient, scalable, and secure
- Writing clean, maintainable code and participating in code reviews
- Collaborating with teams to ensure software quality and project success
- Troubleshooting software issues, optimizing performance, and implementing new features in line with business needs
- Collaborating in the continuous improvement of the development process and contributing to the team's growth by sharing knowledge and best practices
- Developing and delivering high-quality software applications that meet business needs
- Ensuring the software meets performance, security, and scalability standards
- Collaborating with cross-functional teams to ensure smooth integration of software solutions
