# Technical Program Manager

**Company:** Microsoft
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Entry, Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Microsoft is a leading technology company that empowers individuals and organizations to achieve more. They are seeking a Technical Program Manager to lead cross-team development efforts across Azure, focusing on platform experiences that support various Azure services and ensuring high-quality, scalable, and reliable solutions.

## Core Skills
- Distributed Systems
- Artificial Intelligence
- Cloud Infrastructure
- Technical Program Management
- Cross-functional Project Management

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree OR equivalent experience
- Ability to meet Microsoft, customer and/or government security screening requirements are required for this role. These requirements include, but are not limited to the following specialized security screenings: Microsoft Cloud Background Check: This position will be required to pass the Microsoft Cloud Background Check upon hire/transfer and every two years thereafter

## Preferred qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree AND 2+ years experience engineering, product/technical program management, data analysis, or product development
- Experience with Distributed Systems and AI / Cloud Infrastructure OR equivalent experience
- 1+ year(s) of experience managing cross-functional and/or cross-team projects
- 1+ year(s) of experience reading and/or writing code (e.g., sample documentation, product demos)

## Responsibilities
- Act as a technical lead in the cloud infrastructure space, partnering with senior developers and architects to design, reason about, and evolve large‑scale, fault‑tolerant, high‑performance platforms underpinning next‑generation Azure SKUs
- Demonstrate judgment across system design tradeoffs spanning compute, storage, networking, reliability, performance, security, and operability
- Own and drive end‑to‑end architectural alignment across hardware, firmware, software, and control plane components—ensuring that new platform capabilities and SKUs are architected holistically for Azure‑scale deployment, long‑term fleet sustainability, and operational excellence
- Partner with a cross‑functional execution team spanning hardware engineering, software development, capacity planning, supply chain, and datacenter operations to orchestrate the full lifecycle of highly complex SKUs: architectural definition, development, planning, buildout, validation, and eventual production launch into the Azure fleet
- Lead architecture and design tradeoff discussions across organizations by authoring and reviewing technical proposals, evaluating alternative system designs, and driving consensus on scalable solutions
- Translate complex distributed‑systems concepts into clear recommendations for engineering, operations, and business leadership
- Serve as a technical integrator across organizational boundaries, identifying architectural gaps, systemic risks, and cross‑stack dependencies that emerge only at fleet scale
- Proactively drive alignment and decision‑making across Azure and Microsoft partner teams to ensure coherent, end‑to‑end system behavior
- Define and own program goals, technical success criteria, and prioritized roadmaps for the systems and SKUs under management
- Translate ambiguous platform requirements, partner constraints, and customer needs into execution plans grounded in architectural realities, capacity constraints, and long‑term platform strategy
