# Software Development Engineer II, Fleet Automation and Release Orchestration (FAROS)

**Company:** Amazon Web Services (AWS)
**Location:** Seattle, WA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon Web Services (AWS) is seeking a Software Development Engineer to join their Network Product Development organization, focusing on automating and improving the operation of Amazon's network infrastructure. The role involves collaborating with various teams to develop best practices, enhance operational procedures, and drive a culture of proactive problem-solving within the team.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Software Design
- Software Architecture
- Object Oriented Design
- C#

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- 1+ years of software development engineer or related occupational experience
- 1+ years of designing and developing large-scale, multi-tiered, multi-threaded, embedded or distributed software applications, tools, systems, and services using: C#
- C++
- Java, or Perl experience
- 1+ years of Object Oriented Design experience
- Bachelor's degree or foreign equivalent in Computer Science
- Engineering
- Mathematics, or a related field
- Experience programming with at least one software programming language

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- Automate the future of global networks: Control deployment changes across millions of devices, monitor performance at unprecedented scale, and thrive in a team where your voice matters
- Solve complex and ambiguous problems, efficiently and with high-quality
- Play a pivotal role in shaping the definition, vision, design, roadmap, development, and operationalization of customer-facing services
- Contribute to critical and highly-visible function within the AWS business
- Work alongside other talented builders, and across multiple teams
- Hire and shape the team to continue to outperform expectations
- Have a large impact on AWS and the cloud computing industry
- Develop DevOps and automation tasks for test infrastructure
- Use and develop tools and process that collect & rationalize data from different sources and frameworks that reduce workloads
- Use data to measure success and take responsibility for the quality of your service and, regardless of their source
- Strive to prevent faults from impacting your customers
