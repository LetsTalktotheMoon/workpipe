## Core Skills

- Distributed systems
- C++
- Python

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related fields
- 4+ years of professional experience as a software engineer
- Familiar with at least one of C++
- Python, or Golang
- Strong background in scalable backend design, with the ability to make sound design decisions
- Familiar with common cloud-based technologies and services
- Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team
- Must be willing and able to work onsite five days per week

## Responsibilities
- Lead the development of large-scale, high-performance backend systems
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, develop, and maintain the software infrastructure for our cloud camera products
