# Analytics Manager

**Company:** FanDuel
**Location:** Atlanta
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
FanDuel is the premier mobile gaming company in the United States and Canada, seeking a Manager of Analytics to lead a team focused on Fraud & Payments within the Security, Risk & Compliance Analytics team. The role involves managing analysts to provide insights, improve business performance, and reduce risk through actionable data analysis.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Excel
- Python
- Spark
- Data Visualization

## Minimum qualifications
- Minimum 5+ years of relevant work experience in an analytical, technical or commercial field
- Bachelor's Degree in a highly numerate major (Math
- Stats
- Econ, etc.) or relevant experience in data and/or business analysis
- 1+ years managing and coaching analysts, data scientists and/or data engineers
- Ability to organize and prioritize workload for a team of direct reports
- Proficiency in SQL and Excel required
- Demonstrated experience using data visualization software to convey analysis in an easy to digest format
- Strong verbal and written communication skills - demonstrable track record of influencing stakeholders and colleagues

## Preferred qualifications
- Basic ability to use other programming languages for data analysis such as Python
- Spark preferred
- Experience working with Tableau for data visualization preferred
- Experience collaboratively working with colleagues in other countries and time zones preferred
- Experience working within a heavily regulated industry is a plus

## Responsibilities
- Manage a team of analysts to produce specific insights around behavior, year over year trends, and drivers of risk
- Own tools and models that govern measurement of business performance, ensuring they are complete, accurate and regularly reviewed and refreshed
- Audit, review, and help guide the development of our reporting suite to meet stakeholder needs in an effective and efficient manner
- Review team's output ensuring insights are summarized and tools are checked before communication to stakeholders
- Own the day to day management of requests and tasks of your direct reports
- Ensure projects and resources are aligned with current departmental priorities
- Provide management, coaching and professional development to analysts in your team
- Identify and grow top talent within the analytics team
