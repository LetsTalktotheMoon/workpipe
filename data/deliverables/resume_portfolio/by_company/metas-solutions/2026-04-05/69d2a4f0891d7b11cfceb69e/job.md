# Software Developer

**Company:** Metas Solutions
**Location:** Hyattsville, MD
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Metas Solutions is a professional services firm headquartered in Atlanta, GA, offering technical assistance and consultation to the federal government in public health and information technology. The Software Developer will consult on modernizing the public health data ecosystem and develop high-quality technical solutions for C# and SQL Server-based applications.

## Core Skills
- C#
- SQL Server
- Systems Analysis
- Software Development
- Quality Assurance

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science
- Computer Engineering, or a related program
- Entry level to Senior level experience as a Software Developer or similar role
- Five or more years of experience with C#
- Asp.Net
- ADO.Net
- MVC
- JavaScript
- Java
- CSS
- Python
- PHP, and others

## Responsibilities
- Applying experience and knowledge in systems analysis, design, development, quality assurance, and integration to develop high-quality technical solutions for C# and SQL Server-based applications that meet or exceed client goals, delivered on time and within budget
- Ensuring solutions are aligned with business and IT strategies and comply with the organization's architectural standards
- Participating in component and data architecture design and performance monitoring
- Work closely with a scrum master, business analyst, and other development team members to review and coordinate new changes, including version upgrades, on an Agile team
- Manage multiple objectives to completion within the bi-weekly sprint
- Assist team members in development and collaboration and conduct performance tuning/troubleshooting
- Contribute and independently document system design. Provide complete documentation of processes and procedures
