# Applied AI Engineer

**Company:** Abby Care
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Abby Care is dedicated to transforming family caregiving with a tech-powered platform. The Applied AI Engineer will design and build AI systems that assist families and clinicians, contributing to innovative home-based care solutions.

## Core Skills
- Applied AI/ML Engineering
- AI/ML Project Deployment
- Cloud Platforms - AWS
- Python
- AI Model Evaluation

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional experience in applied AI/ML engineering
- Proven track record of working on AI/ML projects from concept to production
- Experience with deploying AI models in production environments
- Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS
- Proficiency in Python

## Preferred qualifications
- Bachelor's/Master's/PhD degree in Computer Science or related field
- Experience with the modern AI toolchain for data processing, orchestration, fine-tuning, and deployment
- Experience with model evaluation and performance measurement

## Responsibilities
- Build AI-powered products from concept to production. You will own the end-to-end development of applied AI systems, from problem framing and model selection to implementation, evaluation, and deployment. This includes prototyping copilots and intelligent features, integrating models into production systems, and iterating based on real user feedback from families and clinicians
- Partner closely with cross-functional teams to apply AI to real-world care. You will collaborate with operations, clinical, compliance, product, and engineering teams to design AI solutions that are safe, trustworthy, and practical in highly regulated environments. You will help translate complex technical ideas into clear product capabilities that directly advance our mission
- Advance technical excellence in applied AI. You will deepen expertise in areas such as LLMs, agentic workflows, machine learning infrastructure, and data security, while also exploring new approaches to human-in-the-loop AI and clinical decision support. You’ll help set the bar for quality, safety, and innovation as we build next-generation AI systems in one of the most impactful and complex sectors in the U.S
