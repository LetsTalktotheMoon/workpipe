# Analyst, Guest Intelligence, Burger King

**Company:** Burger King
**Location:** Miami, FL
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Burger King is part of Restaurant Brands International Inc., one of the world's largest quick service restaurant companies. As an Analyst, Guest Intelligence, you will analyze guest-level data to uncover insights that drive growth, retention, and marketing effectiveness, translating data into actionable recommendations for CRM and promotional strategies.

## Core Skills
- SQL
- Tableau
- Python
- R
- Data analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field (Statistics
- Economics
- Computer Science
- Mathematics, etc.)
- 1-2 years of experience or coursework using SQL to manipulate and analyze large datasets
- Strong analytical thinking with ability to move from raw data to insight and recommendation
- Effective communication skills to explain analytical findings to both technical and non-technical audiences

## Preferred qualifications
- Familiarity with Python or R for deeper analysis
- Experience with BI tools (Tableau
- Power BI, etc.)

## Responsibilities
- Use SQL to analyze guest-level transactions and behavioral data to uncover trends in frequency, retention, and engagement
- Support the definition and standardization of guest-level metrics and the development of segmentation frameworks
- Partner with cross-functional teams to measure campaign impact and guest-level incrementality
- Contribute to test-and-learn frameworks to evaluate initiatives and improve marketing effectiveness
- Proactively identify opportunities to improve guest experience and business performance through data
- Collaborate with Data Engineering team to ensure guest data is reliable, structured, and scalable
- Create clear, compelling visualizations and reports to communicate insights to stakeholders
- Support the enhancement of reporting tools and methods to improve data storytelling
