# Data Scientist

**Company:** Mass General Brigham
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Mass General Brigham is a not-for-profit organization that supports patient care, research, teaching, and community service. The Obstetrics and Gynecology Department at Massachusetts General Hospital is seeking a Data Scientist to provide analytic and project management support for reproductive health research initiatives, involving data management, statistical analysis, and report preparation.

## Core Skills
- Data analytics
- Healthcare datasets
- Claims data
- Electronic health records
- Data management

## Minimum qualifications
- Strong project management and data analytic skills
- Experience with large healthcare datasets (claims, electronic health records, etc.)
- Close attention to detail and timelines
- Discretion in handling sensitive information
- Strong interpersonal skills
- Ability to analyze complex and high-dimensional clinical and operational data for dependencies, patterns, outliers, inaccuracies, and validity
- Knowledge of statistics, machine learning, programming, data modeling, simulation, and/or advanced mathematics
- Ability to recognize patterns, identify opportunities, pose business questions, and make valuable discoveries
- Ability to design and evaluate optimal data-driven metrics
- Ability to design, develop, and evaluate predictive models and advanced algorithms

## Responsibilities
- Analyze complex and high-dimensional clinical and operational data for dependencies, patterns, outliers, inaccuracies, and validity
- Apply knowledge of statistics, machine learning, programming, data modeling, simulation, and/or advanced mathematics to recognize patterns, identify opportunities, pose business questions, and make valuable discoveries
- Contribute to the design and evaluation of optimal data-driven metrics (such as physician/facility performance criteria, bottleneck metrics, productivity limits, processing delay/error reports, etc.)
- Use a flexible, analytical approach to design, develop, and evaluate predictive models and advanced algorithms that lead to optimal value extraction from the data
- Generate and test hypotheses and analyze and interpret the results
