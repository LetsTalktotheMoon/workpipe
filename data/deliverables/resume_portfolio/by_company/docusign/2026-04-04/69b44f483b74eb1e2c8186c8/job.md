# Software Engineer

**Company:** Docusign
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Docusign is a company that brings agreements to life, serving over 1.5 million customers globally. They are seeking a P3 Software Engineer to own the end-to-end delivery of critical infrastructure for their B2B agreement management workflows, focusing on designing, building, and operating scalable backend services and APIs.

## Core Skills
- C#
- TypeScript
- Node.js
- Object-Oriented Programming
- Cloud-native microservices

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or related technical field, or equivalent practical experience
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience building production services or applications using OO languages such as C#
- Java, or TypeScript/Node.js
- 2+ years designing, building, and operating cloud-native microservices or APIs at scale (REST or gRPC), including version control
- CI/CD pipelines, and automated testing as part of your daily workflow
- Experience with relational and/or NoSQL data stores and data modeling for production services
- Demonstrated ability to own features end-to-end, including design docs, implementation, testing strategy, rollout, and operational support

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Microsoft stack (C#, .NET
- ASP.NET) in large-scale SaaS environments
- Hands-on experience with Azure (e.g.
- AKS
- App Services
- Cosmos DB
- Azure SQL) or comparable services in AWS
- Prior work on shared platform components (libraries, pipelines, common services) adopted by multiple teams
- Familiarity with observability and telemetry stacks used for monitoring and incident response (e.g., metrics, logging, dashboards, alerting, big data/stream processing for telemetry)
- Experience participating in or leading RCAs and on-call activities for high-severity incidents, with a focus on prevention and long-term reliability improvements
- Experience working in an agile environment and delivering software iteratively with frequent, high-quality releases
- Strong fundamentals in data structures, algorithms, distributed systems, and troubleshooting in a production environment
- Clear, concise communication skills and the ability to work effectively in cross-functional and cross-team settings

## Responsibilities
- Own feature delivery across the full software development lifecycle: design, implementation, testing, deployment, and operational support for backend services and APIs used by B2B products and platforms
- Design and implement services and APIs using C# or TypeScript/Node.js, following best practices for modularity, testability, and maintainability
- Build cloud-native, microservices-based systems, leveraging Azure services, containers, and modern deployment practices to achieve high availability, resilience, and scalability
- Contribute to shared platform capabilities (e.g., libraries, config pipelines, telemetry and observability tooling) that are reused across multiple B2B services, helping other teams adopt and extend them
- Improve quality and reliability by authoring and maintaining automated tests (unit, integration, contract, and end-to-end where appropriate), defining test plans for your features, and participating in on-call rotations with strong root cause analysis and incident response
- Instrument services with telemetry (metrics, logs, traces) and use data to drive performance tuning, capacity planning, and error-budget–based decisions for the systems you own
- Partner with product management, architecture
- SRE, and peer application teams to refine requirements, align designs, and unblock cross-team initiatives, especially where platform capabilities or shared infrastructure are involved
- Contribute to code reviews and engineering best practices, providing constructive, specific feedback and helping to evolve team standards around coding, testing, and deployment
- Mentor more junior engineers informally by sharing context, reviewing designs, and providing guidance on debugging, testing, and operational excellence consistent with P3 expectations
