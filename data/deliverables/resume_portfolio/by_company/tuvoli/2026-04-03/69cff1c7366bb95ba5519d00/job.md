# Product Analyst – Quality Assurance + Support

**Company:** Tuvoli
**Location:** Nashua, NH
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Tuvoli builds software and financial technology solutions designed to improve sales, payments, and trip logistics in the business aviation industry. They are seeking a quality-focused professional for the Product Analyst – QA role, responsible for QA execution, test automation, release validation, and production support, while collaborating with various teams to ensure high-quality software releases.

## Core Skills
- Quality Assurance
- Manual testing
- Test automation
- Web applications testing
- Defect tracking

## Minimum qualifications
- 4+ years of experience in Quality Assurance, including hands‑on testing
- Experience building and maintaining automated tests
- Strong manual testing experience for web applications
- Familiarity with defect tracking and release validation
- Excellent attention to detail and analytical skills
- Ability to quickly learn complex systems
- Strong communication skills
- Willingness to work on‑site in Nashua
- NH

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Cypress
- ReadyAPI, or similar frameworks
- FinTech or payments experience
- Familiarity with Azure DevOps
- Jira
- GitLab
- Confluence
- Experience supporting production systems
- Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines

## Responsibilities
- Design, execute, and maintain manual and automated tests for web‑based applications
- Build and expand automated regression and smoke test suites
- Perform functional, regression, integration, and UAT testing
- Validate builds and confirm release readiness prior to production deployment
- Identify, track, and verify resolution of defects
- Continuously improve QA processes, tooling, and coverage
- Triage and reproduce reported issues from internal teams and customers
- Assess severity, document findings, and coordinate with Engineering on resolution
- Support root‑cause analysis and post‑release validation
- Partner with Product and Engineering on QA planning for release cycles
