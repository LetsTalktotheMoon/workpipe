# Analytics Developer Power BI

**Company:** SysTechCorp Inc
**Location:** Columbia, MD
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
SysTechCorp Inc is seeking a Power BI/Analytics Developer to translate business needs into technical specifications and develop enterprise-grade Power BI solutions. The role involves designing dashboards, optimizing SQL queries, and collaborating with stakeholders to ensure data quality and project success.

## Core Skills
- Power BI
- Data Modeling
- DAX
- Report Development
- SQL

## Minimum qualifications
- Strong experience in Power BI, including data modeling
- DAX, and report development
- Advanced SQL skills for query optimization, troubleshooting, and data extraction
- Solid understanding of data analysis, data profiling, and data validation techniques
- Ability to translate business needs into technical solutions
- Excellent communication skills for collaborating with business stakeholders and technical teams
- Familiarity with enterprise BI best practices and reporting standards
- Proven ability to work independently and in a team, including with remote team members
- Commitment to compliance with organizational policies, standards, and methodologies

## Responsibilities
- Translate business needs into clear, actionable technical specifications
- Design, develop, and maintain enterprise-grade Power BI dashboards, reports, and datasets adhering to industry best practices
- Review, interpret, troubleshoot, and optimize SQL queries
- write clean, maintainable code with proper structure, comments, and design considerations
- Validate report data for accuracy and consistency to build trust in BI solutions
- provide feedback to internal and external data sources
- Demonstrate proficiency with business intelligence (BI) tools and technologies
- Conduct source data analysis including data profiling, validation, conceptual and logical data modeling to ensure data quality and suitability
- Collaborate with subject matter experts (SMEs) to understand business concepts, processes, and terminology
- Partner with stakeholders to develop business requirements that lead to successful project outcomes and innovative solutions
- Follow project methodology, change management processes, and departmental procedures
- Work effectively in a team environment, including with remote team members
