# Software Developer Engineer 4

**Company:** Adobe
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Adobe is a company focused on changing the world through digital experiences. They are looking for a highly motivated Software Developer Engineer to join the team building a next-generation AI-first marketing application, responsible for developing and deploying critical components, collaborating cross-functionally, and acting as a technical leader.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Data Structures
- Algorithms
- Object-Oriented Design
- Java

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience
- 4- 6 years of hands-on software development experience
- Strong foundation in data structures, algorithms, and object-oriented design
- Proven expertise in Java
- Scala, and clean architecture principles
- Deep understanding of cloud-native architectures, microservices, and 'always-up' systems
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines
- GitOps, and container orchestration tools like Argo CD
- Excellent communication and collaboration skills
- A mindset of curiosity, ownership, and relentless focus on solving real customer problems with high-quality software

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience with Campaign Execution systems and Generative AI is a strong plus

## Responsibilities
- Develop, and deploy critical components of a new Agentic App, delivering production-grade solutions
- Translate high-level product goals into actionable engineering tasks and technical plans
- Evaluate and advocate for the right tools, frameworks, and technologies
- Write clean, testable, and scalable code
- conduct peer reviews and drive best practices in CI/CD, observability, and performance optimization
- Collaborate cross-functionally with Product
- Design, and Engineering to launch customer-centric features
- Communicate effectively with leadership to align on goals and priorities
- Act as a technical leader and mentor, fostering growth across the team
