# Data Scientist

**Company:** Motorola Solutions
**Location:** Richardson, TX
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Motorola Solutions is a global community focused on keeping people safer through technology. They are seeking a Data Scientist to collect, preprocess, and analyze data, develop statistical models, and collaborate with cross-functional teams to deliver insights and analytical solutions.

## Core Skills
- Data Collection
- Data Cleaning
- Data Preprocessing
- Structured Data
- Unstructured Data

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelors degree + 3 years experience or Masters degree + 1 year experience

## Responsibilities
- Collect, clean, and preprocess large volumes of structured and unstructured data from various sources to support data-driven decision-making
- Develop and implement statistical models, machine learning algorithms, and predictive analytics to identify trends, patterns, and actionable insights
- Design and build data pipelines and automated processes for data ingestion, transformation, and storage
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including engineering, product, operations, and business stakeholders to define data requirements and deliver analytical solutions
- Create dashboards, reports, and visualizations to communicate findings and business implications to both technical and non-technical audiences
- Design and implement AI-powered chatbots leveraging large language models (LLMs)
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) architecture, and vectorization techniques to allow business users to query structured and unstructured enterprise data using natural language, enabling real-time, context-aware responses without requiring technical expertise
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA) and hypothesis testing to support business intelligence initiatives and strategic planning
