# Software Development Engineer II, AFSS

**Company:** Amazon
**Location:** Herndon, VA
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon is looking for a Software Development Engineer (SDE) for their Foundational Security Services (AFSS) team. The SDE will design and develop scalable services that address complex problems while mentoring other engineers and collaborating on best practices.

## Core Skills
- Software Development
- Software Design
- Software Architecture
- Programming Languages
- Code Reviews

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- Experience programming with at least one software programming language

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- Design and deliver software solutions to difficult problems
- Create working software which delivers simple solutions to complex problems, from prototype through delivery
- Perform meaningful and timely code reviews while coaching and mentoring other members of the team
- Regularly collaborate with team members to identify process improvements and define and communicate best practices
