# Data Scientist III

**Company:** Komodo Health
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Komodo Health is dedicated to reducing the global burden of disease through smarter use of data. The Data Scientist III role involves building analytics pipelines and complex statistical models to deliver high-value data analytics projects for healthcare organizations, leveraging real-world data to drive insights and decision-making.

## Core Skills
- Python
- SQL
- PySpark
- R
- Healthcare claims data

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree (Master's or PhD degree preferred) in a quantitative field (e.g.
- Statistics
- Mathematics
- Engineering
- Computer Science) or equivalent practical experience
- Analytical mindset and the ability to solve complex problems with limited supervision
- At least three years of industry experience working with Python and large-scale databases using SQL (data query, data cleaning, data transformation)
- Familiarity with PySpark and R
- Experience working with a large anonymized patient level healthcare databases (preferably administrative claims or EHR)
- Comfort communicating complex data science topics to audiences of variable technical capability
- Proficient with complex topics in ML modeling, advanced statistics/biostatistics, algorithm design and knowledge of ML software packages
- Ability to work well both independently and collaboratively in a fast-paced and demanding agile environment
- Interest in growing skills as a Data Scientist and being willing to learn and teach as part of a team
- Strong business acumen demonstrated by a good understanding of how analytics insights (preferably in healthcare analytics) tie into business goals

## Preferred qualifications
- Master's or PhD degree in a quantitative field

## Responsibilities
- Delivered high-value data analytics projects
- Implemented client solutions using techniques like advanced statistical analysis or ML for targeted therapeutic areas in our patient population
- Developed a deep understanding of healthcare claims data
- Constructed complex statistical and mathematical models and developed supporting platforms for their use
- Effectively partnered with teams to translate client requirements into analytics to solve client needs with the capabilities of datasets available at Komodo
- Worked with the data science team to scale our solutions, automate code, and centralize complex logic for reusability
- Advocated for good engineering practices within the team
- Presented findings, insights and analytical results to internal and external partners for client deliveries, as well as the larger DS function for continued growth and learning
- Worked with clients to creatively resolve issues and further develop analyses
