# AI Analyst II

**Company:** McCarthy Holdings, Inc.
**Location:** St Louis, MO
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
McCarthy Holdings, Inc. is a leading employee-owned construction company in America, dedicated to fostering a culture of great experiences and high-performing teams. The AI Analyst II will support the development and governance of AI systems in the construction business, focusing on data workflows and translating technical results into business insights.

## Core Skills
- Data Science
- Computer Science
- Statistics
- Business Analytics
- Data Analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Data Science
- Computer Science
- Statistics
- Business Analytics
- Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent project experience)
- 3 – 5 years of professional experience or strong academic/project experience in data/AI, analytics, data science, or business intelligence
- Execute well-defined analytical tasks with guidance and assist in model monitoring, data quality checks, and prompt testing
- Strong analytical skills with comfort in SQL
- Excel, and Python (pandas, matplotlib, scikit-learn basics)
- Familiarity with cloud data environments (AWS
- Azure
- GCP) and willingness to learn MLOps/PromptOps tools
- Strong understanding of AI/ML concepts and interest in generative AI systems
- Strong attention to detail and a passion for data quality and governance
- Excellent communication skills—able to summarize technical results for nontechnical audiences
- Growth mindset: eager to learn AI governance, prompt engineering, and agentic AI concepts on the job

## Responsibilities
- Take ownership of assigned projects or components, moving ideas from concept to execution while tracking progress, stakeholders, and changes under guidance from senior team members
- Participate in mapping out and documenting processes for the deployment, scaling, and ongoing improvement of AI platforms used across business units
- Prepare and share meeting notes, research summaries, process documentation, and action items with teams in a timely and accessible manner
- Contribute to a collaborative, feedback-driven work culture that encourages continuous improvement and learning
- Assist in maintaining and validating data models and shared definitions that help align analytics
- AI outputs, and business concepts across teams
- Supports data preparation, documentation, and testing activities
- Run exploratory analyses to identify trends, anomalies, and insights in construction and project data
- Assist in testing and evaluating AI/ML model outputs and generative AI prompts for accuracy, relevance, and compliance
- Track key metrics for AI solutions (accuracy, bias, adoption, governance checkpoints)
- Support the development of dashboards, reports, and visualizations to communicate AI outcomes to business stakeholders
