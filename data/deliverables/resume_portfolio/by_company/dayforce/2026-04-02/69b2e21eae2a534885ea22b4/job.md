# Software Developer III

**Company:** Dayforce
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Dayforce is a global human capital management company headquartered in Toronto and Minneapolis, offering a unified Cloud HCM platform. The Software Developer III role involves designing, developing, testing, and documenting software solutions to enhance operational efficiency and improve customer experience.

## Core Skills
- Enterprise Software Development
- High-Performance System Development
- Scalable System Development
- AI-Assisted Development Tools
- Object-Oriented Programming

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or related field
- Experience in an Enterprise Software development company
- Experience developing high-performant and scalable systems
- Experience leveraging AI-assisted development tools
- Experience with object-oriented programming languages such as Java
- C#
- Experience with modern web technologies, such as Angular
- React
- Vue
- Excellent written and verbal communication skills
- Ability to handle multiple competing priorities in a fast-paced environment

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, test, debug, and document software solutions in all areas of our systems: front-end, back-end, database
- Collaborate with developers, testers, and business analysts in working on requirements, software development, and even the way in which the team works
- Learn functional and technical details of the Dayforce application and HCM ("Human Capital Management") domain
