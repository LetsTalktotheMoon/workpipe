# Sr Data Analyst

**Company:** The Walt Disney Company
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
The Walt Disney Company is a global leader in media and entertainment, and they are seeking a Senior Product Data Analyst to enhance user experience and drive business outcomes through data-driven strategies. In this role, you will collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop experimentation strategies, analyze complex data, and influence product direction for Disney's consumer-facing digital products.

## Core Skills
- Product analytics
- Data science
- Business intelligence
- Experimentation
- A/B testing

## Minimum qualifications
- 5+ years of experience in product analytics, data science, business intelligence, or a related role, with a strong focus on experimentation and analytics for digital content, news, entertainment, or streaming products
- Advanced proficiency with SQL and experience working with large, complex datasets
- Strong skills in experimentation (A/B and multivariate testing), statistical inference, and experiment design
- Proficiency with Python or R for analysis and modeling
- Experience with data visualization tools (e.g.
- Tableau
- Looker
- Power BI) and analytics platforms (e.g.
- Optimizely
- Amplitude
- Google Optimize
- Adobe Target)
- Proven ability to clean, manipulate, and analyze data
- to translate technical findings into clear, actionable insights for diverse audiences
- and to influence product decisions
- Excellent communication, collaboration, and organizational skills
- Passion for news, entertainment, or media, and a drive to enhance user experiences through data-driven experimentation and personalization
- Bachelor's degree in Statistics
- Data Science
- Mathematics
- Computer Science
- Economics
- Business Analytics, or a related quantitative field from a four-year accredited college or university and/or equivalent work experience

## Responsibilities
- Collaborate with Product Managers and cross-functional teams to clarify product objectives, help establish success metrics, and support the development of experimentation strategies throughout the product lifecycle
- Assist in scoping, designing, and executing A/B and multivariate experiments, applying sound hypotheses and statistical methods, and ensuring proper segmentation and measurement using established frameworks (such as north stars
- OKRs, and diagnostic metrics)
- Contribute to the creation of test plans, data collection requirements, and experiment tracking protocols
- participate in cleaning and validating experimental and behavioral data to ensure analytical accuracy upon test completion
- Support efforts to forecast and measure the business impact of new features and personalization initiatives—including incremental revenue, user engagement, and retention—and perform deep-dive analyses to uncover improvement opportunities and inform product decisions
- Assist with communicating complex data and experiment outcomes using advanced statistical techniques, clear visualizations, and executive summaries, tailoring insights for both technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Help develop and maintain automated dashboards, self-service tools, and reports to track experiment performance and key business KPIs
- proactively surface data gaps and collaborate with Data and Engineering teams to enhance data quality
- Advocate for best practices in experimentation, data governance, and measurement, while supporting knowledge sharing and contributing to a culture of evidence-based decision making and innovation across the team
