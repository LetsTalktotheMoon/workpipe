# IT Business Systems Analyst

**Company:** Motorola Solutions
**Location:** United States
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Remote
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Motorola Solutions is a global community focused on enhancing safety through technology. They are seeking an IT Business Systems Analyst to collaborate with business partners to understand technical system requests, develop solutions, and drive the implementation of new systems while ensuring accuracy and relevancy of data insights for decision-making.

## Core Skills
- Technical system analysis
- Solution design
- Report development
- Acceptance testing
- Data mapping

## Minimum qualifications
- Masters degree + 3 years experience or Bachelors degree + 5 years experience

## Responsibilities
- Understand technical system requests and develop creative solution designs
- Drive the development, testing, and implementation of new solutions
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand reporting needs and develop reports that align with business objectives
- Determine the objectives of an assignment and select methods, techniques, and evaluation criteria to achieve measurable business outcomes
- Work with business users to develop acceptance test scenarios and identify test data
- Translate complex business issues into requirements and technical solution designs
- Estimate effort, engage IT partners, and manage solution delivery
- Collaborate effectively with both business and IT partners to meet commitments
- Use Data mapping from source to target database schemas and utilize the ETL process to write data extract scripts and test scripts
- Build automated workflows to cleanse, integrate, and extract insights from massive datasets
