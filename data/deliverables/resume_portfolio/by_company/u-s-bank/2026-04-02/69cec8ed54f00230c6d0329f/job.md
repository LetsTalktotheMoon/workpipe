# Software Engineer II - Front End

**Company:** U.S. Bank
**Location:** Irving, TX
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
U.S. Bank is one of the country's most respected and innovative financial institutions, seeking a Software Engineer to contribute to their digital transformation journey. The role involves designing, developing, testing, and maintaining software solutions while adhering to best practices and collaborating with teams across the organization.

## Core Skills
- JavaScript
- DOM manipulation
- JavaScript object model
- React.js
- Flux

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree, or equivalent work experience
- Three to five years of relevant experience
- Responsible for designing, developing, testing, operating and maintaining products
- Takes full stack ownership by consistently writing production-ready and testable code
- Consistently creates optimal design adhering to architectural best practices
- considers scalability, reliability and performance of systems/contexts affected when defining technical designs
- Performs analysis on failures, propose design changes, and encourage operational improvements
- Makes sound design/coding decisions keeping customer experience in the forefront

## Preferred qualifications
- Strong proficiency in JavaScript, including DOM manipulation and the JavaScript object model

- Immutable.js)

- Familiarity with RESTful APIs
