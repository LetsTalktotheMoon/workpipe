# Software Development Engineer, Ads Data Manager

**Company:** Amazon
**Location:** New York, NY
**Seniority:** Mid Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Amazon is a leader in digital advertising, leveraging advanced technologies to enhance user experiences. They are seeking a Software Development Engineer to develop innovative solutions within their Customer Data & Experience group, focusing on the Amazon Marketing Cloud product and its ecosystem.

## Core Skills
- Software development
- Software design, architecture
- Programming languages
- Software development life cycle

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of non-internship professional software development experience
- 2+ years of non-internship design or architecture (design patterns, reliability and scaling) of new and existing systems experience
- Experience programming with at least one software programming language

## Preferred qualifications
- 3+ years of full software development life cycle, including coding standards, code reviews, source control management, build processes, testing, and operations experience
- Bachelor's degree in computer science or equivalent

## Responsibilities
- Build innovative solutions to grow the ecosystem around Amazon Marketing Cloud product
- Work with Professional Services and Tech Integration partners
- Ingest billions of behavioral signals every day
- Develop greenfield services integrating with advertising business verticals within Amazon
- Partner with external customers and build SaaS integration products
