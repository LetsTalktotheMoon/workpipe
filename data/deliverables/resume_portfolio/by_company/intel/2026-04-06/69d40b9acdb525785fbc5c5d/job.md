# Development Tools Software Engineer

**Company:** Intel
**Location:** Santa Clara, CA
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Intel is a leading technology company focused on driving innovation in semiconductor manufacturing. As a Development Tools Software Engineer, you will develop software tools and systems that support design, manufacturing, validation, and integration teams, enhancing the efficiency and quality of Intel's products.

## Core Skills
- Python
- C++
- Java
- Scripting
- Automation tools

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in computer science
- Software Engineering, or a related field with 3+ years of experience or a master's degree in computer science or software engineering or computer engineering or related STEM discipline. Applicant must have the required degree by July 2026
- 6+ months of experience with the following technical skills: Proficiency in programming languages such as Python
- C++, or Java
- Experience with scripting and automation tools
- Familiarity with software debug techniques and tools
- Understanding of software test methodologies and secure coding practices

## Preferred qualifications
- Demonstrated ability to solve complex technical problems and deliver high-quality software solutions
- Knowledge of object-oriented software design and disciplined execution in software development processes
- Strong communication skills and ability to collaborate effectively in cross-functional teams
- Passion for innovation and a commitment to driving impactful results that align with Intel's values

## Responsibilities
- Design, develop, debug, validate, and optimize software tools and algorithms that support design, manufacturing, validation, and silicon production
- Utilize modern software development methodologies, object-oriented programming, and secure coding practices to create robust and reliable tools
- Develop and maintain systems such as debuggers, analyzers
- SDKs
- IDEs, performance monitors, and libraries
- Collaborate with internal and external engineering partners to provide tools that enable impactful product integration and manufacturing processes
- Identify and solve complex problems related to software tools and systems to enhance functionality, usability, and performance
- Contribute to software architecture input and feedback to ensure alignment with Intel's technological goals and values
- Maintain high standards of software quality and testing methodologies throughout the development lifecycle
