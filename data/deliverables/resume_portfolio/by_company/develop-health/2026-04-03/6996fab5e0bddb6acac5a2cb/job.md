# Senior Software Engineer

**Company:** Develop Health
**Location:** Menlo Park, CA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Hybrid
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Develop Health is on a mission to use AI to radically accelerate access to life-saving medications. They are seeking a Senior Software Engineer to design and ship systems and interfaces for their AI-driven healthcare platform, collaborating with AI and Product teams to enhance workflows and user experiences.

## Core Skills
- Python
- TypeScript
- React
- API design
- Backend services

## Minimum qualifications
- 3+ years of experience building and shipping production full-stack software
- Strong proficiency in Python
- TypeScript, and React
- Experience designing, building, and maintaining APIs and scalable backend services
- Strong product intuition and attention to detail in building user-facing experiences
- A self-directed, ownership-driven approach—you prioritize outcomes over process
- Excellent collaboration and communication skills across engineering
- AI, and product teams
- Availability to work on-site in Menlo Park at least three days per week

## Preferred qualifications
- Experience building AI-powered or automation-heavy products
- Exposure to health-tech or clinical data systems (EHRs, payers, or pharmacy integrations)
- Startup or early-stage experience
- a founder mindset and bias for impact
- Familiarity with cloud infrastructure (GCP
- AWS), orchestration, and DevOps tooling

## Responsibilities
- Build and scale Python-based backend systems and APIs that power AI-driven automation in healthcare
- Develop modern frontend experiences using React + TypeScript, enabling clinicians and operations teams to easily leverage AI insights
- Integrate and orchestrate LLM services in production environments, optimizing for performance, reliability, and user experience
- Collaborate with AI and product teams to refine workflows, gather user feedback, and translate insights into product improvements
- Own critical technical decisions around architecture, scalability, and developer experience
- Enhance observability, testing, and CI/CD pipelines to ensure rapid, confident iteration
- Lead development of new AI-powered product features that simplify and accelerate clinical and operational workflows
- Scale our full-stack architecture to support 100× transaction growth and real-time analytics
- Shape the data and API layer that powers both internal analytics and external integrations
- Mentor future engineers and help build a strong engineering culture of quality and velocity
