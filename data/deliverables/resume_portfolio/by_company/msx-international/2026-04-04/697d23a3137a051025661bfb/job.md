# Data Analyst

**Company:** MSX International
**Location:** Southfield, MI
**Seniority:** Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
MSX International is a trusted partner to leading vehicle manufacturers and mobility organizations, helping clients transform their businesses. They are seeking a Data Analyst responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining business data models to provide insights into customer data, while also developing analytic reports and dashboards.

## Core Skills
- Data visualization tools
- SQL
- Data analysis
- Data manipulation
- Statistical analysis

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's Degree in computer science, statistics, mathematics, or relevant discipline
- 1-3 years of automotive operations experience, or similar business (quick-paced environment)
- Experience with data visualization tools (Tableau
- PowerBI, etc.)
- SQL skills for querying relational databases
- Experience with complex data analysis
- Performing data manipulation and statistical analysis
- Strong analytical skills with ability to understand and communicate the meaning of the data being presented
- Excellent Mathematical/Statistical and Logic skills
- Excellent Reasoning skills
- Full Professional Proficiency in English is required

## Preferred qualifications
- Training/Learning operations experience preferred
- Multi-dimensional reporting, data correlation, data analytics and data mining
- Previous external client facing experience
- Experience with ETL tools (SSIS
- Alteryx
- KNIME, etc.)
- Experience supporting a Training
- Learning
- Development or Talent Management team
- Knowledge Retention - In order to provide the highest quality of work, analysts must be able to learn and memorize a large amount of information
- Detail Oriented – Analysts must be able to focus on the details in order to identify and isolate issues
- Organizational Skills – Analysts must be organized and able to keep track of multiple projects at once
- Flexibility - Analysts must be able to handle multiple tasks at once and follow changes in priority level
- Speed - Must be able to work quickly and efficiently without sacrificing the quality of their work

## Responsibilities
- Develop insightful KPI reporting dashboards to drive decision-making and performance tracking
- Develop reports and dashboards via data mining analytics and reporting tools
- Prepare analytics reports in presentation form
- Assist in developing sound data ETL practices and processes
- Conduct research and analyze special studies when needed
- Create and update documentation of process flows and business rules
- Maintain current reports up to date and process large amounts of data as needed
