# Software Development Engineer -  Customer Data Platform

**Company:** Adobe
**Location:** San Jose, CA
**Seniority:** New Grad, Entry Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Adobe is a company that empowers everyone to create through innovative platforms and tools. They are seeking a Software Development Engineer to contribute to the Unified Profile team, focusing on building large-scale systems for user identification and interaction across various digital channels.

## Core Skills
- Java
- Scala
- Data structures
- Algorithms
- Caching strategies

## Minimum qualifications
- Employer will accept a Bachelor's/advanced degree in Information Systems Management
- Computer Science
- Computer Engineering
- Information Technology
- Software Engineering or related field and 0-1+ years of experience in the job offered or in a Software Development Engineer-related occupation
- BS in computer science
- MS in computer science preferred
- Passion for developing high-quality software applications
- Analytical thinking and problem-solving skills
- Good understanding of data structure and algorithms
- Strong communication to articulate ideas and strategies
- Proven record of delivering highly optimized code in Java/Scala
- Proficiency in data structures, algorithms, caching strategies, async patterns
- Ability to adapt and learn cloud technologies, e.g. AWS
- Azure
- GCP
- Docker
- Kubernetes
- Able to understand distributed processing systems like Apache Spark
- Hadoop Stack, or Kafka

## Responsibilities
- Play a critical role in the delivery of highly reliable, scalable, and maintainable enterprise applications
- Work with modern technologies, collaborate on product definitions with Product Managers, implementing technical solutions with hardworking teams
- Gain expertise in the world of big data and building and operating high-scale and highly reliable services that receive Billions of events per day or process PetaBytes of data
- Contribute to engineering guidelines by providing guidance towards code review, test coverage, and promoting high standard methodologies in software development
- Steer technical design and architecture discussions, making critical decisions that drive technical product design
- Lead engineering efforts and projects to deliver critical components, new features and enhance performance of existing features
- Optimize performance and scalability of backend systems to ensure high availability and fast response times
