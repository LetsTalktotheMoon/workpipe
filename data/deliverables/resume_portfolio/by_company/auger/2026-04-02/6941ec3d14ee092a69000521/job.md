# Quality Assurance Engineer

**Company:** Auger
**Location:** Bellevue, WA
**Seniority:** Mid, Senior Level
**Work Model:** Onsite
**Employment Type:** Full-time

## About the job
Auger is the autonomous operating system for supply chains, connecting various enterprise systems into a unified data layer. They are seeking a Quality Assurance Engineer to ensure reliability and performance by designing automated test systems and improving CI/CD processes.

## Core Skills
- Software testing methodologies
- Unit testing
- Integration testing
- System testing
- Acceptance testing

## Minimum qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science
- Engineering, or a related field
- 3–5 years of QA or SDET experience
- Strong understanding of software testing methodologies, including unit, integration, system, and acceptance testing
- Proficiency in at least programming languages such as Python
- Java, or JavaScript
- Experience with automated testing tools (PyTest
- JUnit
- Cypress
- Playwright
- Selenium, etc.)
- Familiarity with CI/CD systems (GitHub Actions
- Jenkins, or similar)
- Strong understanding of test design principles and quality metrics

## Responsibilities
- Build scalable test systems that ensure reliability across distributed and data-driven products
- Design intelligent test suites that catch real-world issues before they reach customers
- Automate everything. From functional to integration testing and embedding quality in the development lifecycle
- Collaborate across teams to drive test-based design and continuous improvement in our CI/CD workflows
- Diagnose, reproduce, and track issues through resolution using tools like Jira
- GitHub Issues, or similar
- Support code reviews and pair with developers to ensure testability and maintainability
- Be a quality advocate, influencing how we build, deploy, and monitor software across environments
